| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Late}} ''"Reinforcements? HA! You're late!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the second Round, then Tenni'syn will interrupt-cast his natural attack on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Pip}} At the end of every Round, Tenni'syn will gain a number of additional power pips that is equal to the number of Wizards that is present in the duel minus one.
*{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} ''"Into the jaws of Death with you!"'' - Tenni'syn will say this at the beginning of the Round that he gains his seventh Shadow pip to cast Lord of the Squall (Shadow).

{{Icon|Dispel}} '''Dispel Cycle:'''
*''"Ah, clever! Don't do that again"'' - When the first Dispel is cast on Tenni'syn, he uses {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm (Shadow)}}.
*''"Oh no - I'm ready for you this time!"'' - When the second Dispel is cast on Tenni'syn, he uses Cleanse Charm (Shadow).
*''"I'm warning you! No more!"'' - When the third Dispel is cast on Tenni'syn, he uses  Cleanse Charm (Shadow).
*''"You can't hurt me - you've made me stronger!"'' - When the fourth Dispel is cast on Tenni'syn, he uses Cleanse Charm (Shadow) and {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on himself.
**''"Volley and thunder! Shatter and sunder!"''' - After the cheat above triggers, he'll cast Cleanse Charm (Shadow), cast his Natural Attack then proceed with the cheat below.
**''"Lightning from the left of you! Lightning from the right!"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, Tenni'syn will repeatedly cast Wild Bolt on random Wizards up to seven times.

{{Icon|Feint}} '''Feint Cycle:'''
*''"Ah, clever! Don't do that again"'' - Tenni'syn will say this when the first Feint per Round is cast on him.
**{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint}} will trigger this cheat
**Balance Feint will '''not''' trigger this cheat

*''"Oh no - I'm ready for you this time!"'' - For every second Feint per Round that is cast on Tenni'syn, he will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (-2)}} on himself removing 2 Traps.

*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"You can't hurt me - you've made me stronger!"'' - When a Damage over Time is used after a Feint is cast on him on the same turn, then Tenni'syn will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, a Supercharge (+12%), a '''+12%''' version of Stormspear, and a version of {{Link|Spell|Storm Hound (150)}} that attacks all Wizards, followed up by {{Link|Spell|Shift}} on a random Wizard and {{Link|Spell|Incindiate}} on all Wizards then use Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All) on himself to remove all Traps.

*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Time to finish this!"'' - Every four Rounds, Tenni'syn will use his ''Natural Attack'' dealing Storm Damage.
**''"Lightning from the left of you! Lightning from the right!"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, Tenni'syn will repeatedly cast Wild Bolt on random Wizards up to seven times.
**If Tenni'syn is damaged on the Round before this cheat occurs, he will use Wild Bolt for every trap that was consumed when he was damaged.
