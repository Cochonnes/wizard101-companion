| cheatnotes =
'''Freddie Kroaker''':
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"One, two, I'm coming for you!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, or if a Wizard joins late, Freddie Kroaker will cast a Shadow Spell that applies a Dispel Charm of the Wizard's school on each Wizard, and then place a 'Nightmare Prone' Stun Shield on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"One, two, here's a nightmare for you!"'' - At the end of each Round, if a Wizard has the 'Nightmare Prone' mark, Freddie Kroaker casts one of the following Spells on the marked Wizard:
**{{Link|Spell|Call of Khrulhu}}
**{{Link|Spell|Monster Mash (Tier 5a)}}
**{{Link|Spell|Shift Grey Wolf}}
**{{Link|Spell|Winged Sorrow}}
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Let the malus grow! Let it feed my beast!"'' - At the beginning of every second Round, Freddie Kroaker casts a Moon version of Balanceblade that says "GROWTH".
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"It lives! My Juggernaut Crab of Malus lives! The Great Barrier will fall!"'' - At the end of each Round, if Freddie Kroaker has 3 "GROWTH" Charms, he will cast a Shadow version of Skeletal Dragon that deals 40% max Health Damage on all Wizards.
**{{Link|Spell|Impair}} or {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} cannot be used to counter this cheat
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"This better not be love conquering all!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, a positive Healing Charm, a Healing Global, or an outgoing Healing Aura, then Freddie Kroaker will cast a version of Enfeeble that removes all Charms from himself, and a version of Cleanse Ward that removes all Wards (including the 'Nightmare Prone' mark) from the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} can be used to prevent removal.
*{{Icon|All Friends (Squared)}} ''"Three, four, we've only started our war!"'' - If a Wizard casts an All Allies Spell, including Living Nightmare, Freddie Kroaker will cast a Shadow Spell that applies a Dispel Charm of the Wizard's school on each Wizard.
**Monstrology minions of Astral or Shadow schools will not receive a Dispel.

'''Arcane Toad (Star-Storm)''':
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"One, two, Freddie's not done with you!"'' - If Freddie Kroaker is targeted or an All Enemies Spell is used, Arcane Toad (Star-Storm) will give all Wizards a Living Nightmare card:
**{{GivesSpell|size = x150px|Living Nightmare}}
**This cheat can still be cast even if Freddie Kroaker is defeated.
