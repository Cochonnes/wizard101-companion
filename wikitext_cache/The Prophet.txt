| cheatnotes = 
'''The Prophet'''
*''"No Sanctuary in the Night."'' - At the beginning of the first Round and if a Wizard joins the duel late, The Prophet casts a negative version of {{Link|Spell|Acrimony}} on all Wizards that turns all incoming Healing into Damage for 99 Rounds. The Aura is applied twice, but the effects do not stack.
**If a Wizard casts an Aura Spell on the first Round, one of the negative Auras will be replaced, and the Wizard will receive the effects of the remaining one, as well the one they cast, at the same time. This will change the 99 Round Aura timer to 4 Rounds to match the Wizard's Aura Spell, and all Aura effects will end once the timer expires.
**Casting any Aura on any other turn will cause The Prophet to cast a no-Damage version of {{Link|Spell|Supernova}}, removing the Wizard's Aura, and then will recast this cheat on all  wizards.
**The Aura does not convert healing received from drain spells
'''Shadow Guardian'''
*At the beginning of the first Round, both Shadow Guardians will cast a permanent version of {{Link|Spell|Shadow Sentinel}} which provides '''Intercept 90%''', '''+80''' Critical Block, and '''-30%''' incoming Damage.
