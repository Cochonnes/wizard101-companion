'''{{Link|Creature|Aphrodite II}}:'''
*The following duel circle spots are considered to be across from each other, in regards to the Boss cheats:
::{{Target|Sun}} <---> {{Target|Dagger}}
::{{Target|Eye}} <---> {{Target|Key}}
::{{Target|Star}} <---> {{Target|Gem}}
::{{Target|Moon}} <---> {{Target|Spiral}}
*{{Icon|Blade}} {{Icon|Global}} If a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Damage Charm or Damage-Increasing [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spell is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Darkwind}}), the Tendril across from the Wizard will cheat-cast the following Spells:
**''"Terrify the vermin!"'' and ''"Find their weak points!"'' - The Death Tendril will cheat-cast Scarecrow and Feint (+75%) on a random Wizard.
**''"Tear down the Blades!"'' and ''"Seek out the cracks in their foundation!"'' - The Life Tendril will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Forest Lord}} and {{Link|Spell|Guidance}}.
**''"Shake the earth!"'' and ''"Stun the strongest!"'' - The Myth Tendril will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Earthquake}}, then {{Link|Spell|Stun}} a random Wizard.
**When the Tendril across from the Wizard has been defeated, that Wizard may safely cast Blades without triggering this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} If a Wizard casts a Damage over Time Spell, the Tendril across from the Wizard will cheat-cast the following Spell:
**''"Summon the deadly hunt!"'' - The Death Tendril will cheat-cast Deer Knight.
**''"Shake it off!"'' - The Life Tendril will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Mass Triage}}, which remains active for 3 Rounds.
**''"Steal the strength!"'' - The Myth Tendril will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Shift}} at the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} When a Wizard is defeated, the Tendril across from the Wizard will cheat-cast the following Spell:
**''"Find their weak points!"'' - The Death Tendril will cheat-cast Feint (+75%).
**''"Seek out the cracks in their foundation!"'' - The Life Tendril will cheat-cast Guidance.
**''"Stun the strongest!"'' - The Myth Tendril will cheat-cast Stun on a random Wizard.

'''Interrupt Cycle:'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I am untouched! My spores will spread over everyone!"'' - Any Round that a Tendril is not attacked, Aphrodite II will cast a unique Spell on all Wizards, which deals 333 Shadow Damage and 666 Shadow Damage over 3 Rounds, and applies a '''-30%''' Damage Charm, a '''-40%''' Accuracy Charm, and a '''-50%''' Healing Charm.
**A Wizard fleeing will trigger this cheat.
**A Wizard joining late will not trigger this cheat.
**''"No! That stings!"'' - Attacking any of the Tendrils will interrupt this cheat.
