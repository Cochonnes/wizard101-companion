| cheatnotes =
'''Omen Stribog'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins after the duel has already started, then Omen will cast an enchanted version of {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} dealing 1,500 base Damage.
**{{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} can be used to bypass this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"We are not so easily fooled!"'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on any enemy, then Omen will then remove the Feint from himself or his allies, unless it's protected.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} will remove the trap placed on the user of the spell.
** The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this.
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"You are darkened by Shadow as well?"'' - If a Wizard casts a Shadow Creature Spell, then Omen will cast a Moon Spell that adds '''+40''' Backlash to the Wizard.
**Because of updates to Shadow mechanisms, this cheat no longer uses Backlash system.
*{{Icon|Aura}} If Omen is the last Boss remaining, he will cast these variations of {{Link|Spell|Amplify}}. (Needs clarification on how often Aura changes)
**+10% outgoing Damage for 99 Rounds.
**+20% outgoing Damage for 99 Rounds.
**+30% outgoing Damage for 99 Rounds.
**+40% outgoing Damage for 99 Rounds.
**+50% outgoing Damage for 99 Rounds.
***This Aura has the appearance of {{Link|Spell|Berserk}}.
***If a Wizard removes the Aura, he will recast it.

'''Dark Sentinel'''
*{{Icon|Round}} At the beginning of the first Round, Dark Sentinel will cheat-cast an enhanced version of {{Link|Spell|Shadow Sentinel}}, which intercepts '''75%''' Damage, lasts '''99''' Rounds, and has no contract.
**If Dark Sentinel is revived from a Heal All Spell, then it will not cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Shadow Sentinel}}.
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"Your parlor tricks are to no avail"'' - If a Wizard casts any type of Charm, Dark Sentinel will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble (Shadow)}} on both the caster and receiver.
**If a Wizard casts a Blade on themself, then the Dark Sentinel will cast Enfeeble on them twice.
**Negative charms such as {{Link|Spell|Entangle}} will result in all blades being removed from the targeted opponent as well as the caster.
**If a multi-target Spell is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}), then the Dark Sentinel will only remove the Blade from the Wizard who cast the Spell.
*{{Icon|Ward}} If a Wizard casts any type of Ward Spell, there is a chance that Dark Sentinel will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on the Wizard who cast the Spell.

'''Dark Seraph'''
*{{Icon|Ward}} Whenever the Dark Seraph uses a Healing Spell, an [[Spell:Spirit Armor|Absorb]] will be placed on all enemies, similar to the legacy {{Link|Spell|Shadow Seraph}} effect.
