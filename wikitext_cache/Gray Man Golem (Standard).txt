| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"You cannot kill the undying"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, each Gray Man Golem will receive a {{Link|Spell|Guardian Spirit}} effect.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Magic mine... your power... mine!"'' - At the end of each Round, if there is at least one Wizard with a Positive Charm, a Gray Man Golem will target a random Wizard and receive a number of Power Pips depending on the amount of Positive Charms.
** 1 or 2 Charms = 1 Power Pip
** 3 Charms = 3 Power Pips
** 4+ Charms = 4 Power Pips
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Pain... makes... power!"'' - At the beginning of each Round, if a Gray Man Golem is not at full health, it will cast a special version of {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}} that increases in value the lower his Health is.
** The minimum Blade value is 20% and the maximum Blade value is 100%. If the Blade is 100%, it will also be Protected.
** If there are multiple Gray Man Golems under full Health, the value of the Blade will be based on the first one in the duel circle. This cheat will only activate one per Round, regardless of how many Gray Man Golems are in the battle.
** Further testing is required to determine Blade values between the minimum and maximum and their corresponding HP thresholds.
