| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Fire pierces all!"'' - After Lambent Fire casts a Damage Spell, he receives a {{Link|Spell|Firespear}}.
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"The spirits of the past strike you down!"'' - If a Wizard casts any non-Fire, non-universal Blade, Lambent Fire will cast {{Link|Spell|Brimstone Revenant}} on the receiver.
** {{Link|Spell|Quench}} will dispel this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Your edge is mine!"'' - If a Wizard casts a universal Blade, Lambent Fire will cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the receiver.
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"The Fire hungers."'' - Casting any Fire Damage Blade Spell, including {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}}, randomly triggers the Steal Charm cheat.
***Pet May-Casts will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Jinx}}''"Your world shall burn!"'' - If a Wizard casts a universal Trap on Lambent Fire, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Immolate}} on the caster.
**The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} and Mass Hex will not trigger this cheat.
**Mass Feint will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} When any Skirker Buster casts {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Fire 2,050)}}, it will cast a version of Immolate that sacrifices 100% Max Health on itself.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Come to the light!"'' - At the end of every Round, Lambent Fire will resummon all Skirker Buster Minions that were defeated or missing on the battlefield.
'''Death Interrupt Cycle:'''
*When all three Skirker Busters are present, this cheat will cycle at the end of each round and repeats itself after three rounds.
**{{Icon|Charm}} ''"I give you my strength!"'' - At the end of the first round, the first Skirker Buster will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Pact (+30%)}} on Lambent Fire.
**{{Icon|Health}} ''"I give you my health!"'' - At the end of the second Round, the second Skirker Buster will cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice (Death)}} on Lambent Fire.
**{{Icon|Curse}} ''"I give of myself to curse you!"'' - At the end of the third Round, the third Skirker will cast {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju}} on a random Wizard.
***The third Round will not trigger if any Wizards flee the duel.
