| descrip =
Ruler of the Undead that have been freed from the Haunted Cave, Lord Nightshade is the last storyline Boss in Wizard City. Nightshade was released by and is working for {{Link|Creature|Malistaire Drake}}, who tasked the wraith with finding out as much as he could about the Order of the Fang. This task was delegated to the ghost {{Link|Creature|Foulgaze}}, who directs Wizards to Lord Nightshade after they learn that Malistaire stole [[NPC:Bartleby|Bartleby's]] Eye of History.

Two additional Field Guard Minions will join the duel when more than two Wizards are present.
