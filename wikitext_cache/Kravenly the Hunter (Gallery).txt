| cheatnotes =
:'''Kravenly the Hunter'''
*{{Icon|Curse}} Kravenly will remove any negative [[:Category:Charm Spells|charms]] that are placed on him (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Weakness}} or dispels).
*{{Icon|Round}} At the beginning of every other Round, Kravenly will cheat-cast a {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Kravenly)}} at a random Wizard, which deals 340 initial Life damage plus 630 Life damage over 3 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Health}} '''Health Thresholds'''
**''"Health at 75%"'' - Once his health reaches 75% (19,100 health remaining), he will cast an multi-target version of {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blast}}, that deals '''1,120''' Death Damage, '''1,095''' Life Damage, or '''1,190''' Myth Damage on all Wizards.
**''"Health at 50%"'' - When his health reaches 50% (12,733 health remaining), he will cast his {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blast}}.
**''"Health at 25%"'' - When his health reaches 25% (6,366 health remaining), he will cast his {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blast}}.
**His Backlash can trigger this cheat.
**Bringing his health down to 50% or 25% from one attack, will still trigger each stage of this cheat to be cast consecutively.
*{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} When Kravenly has 2 Shadow Pips, he will use his turn to cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Fiend}}.
**Because of changes to Shadow Creatures and updates, attempting to cast Dark Fiend will cause Kravenly to always pass due to a targeting bug.

:'''Ghost Dog'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Your body and soul are mine once again!"'' - At the end of Round 1, [[Creature:Ghost Dog (Gallery)|Ghost Dog]] will summon one {{Link|Creature|Seijun Killer}} Minion and one {{Link|Creature|Hagakure Blade}} Minion. (They become active in Round 3.)
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}}''"Again feel the poetry of war!"'' - At the end of Round 2 (and every 5 Rounds thereafter), he will cast a Damage over Time (DoT) spell that deals 400 Death Damage per Round for 10 Rounds (575 Death damage in Shadow form).
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"The way of the Samoorai is found in death!"'' - At the end of Round 5 (and every 5 Rounds thereafter), he will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Detonate (Death)}} that detonates 5 DoTs on all Wizards dealing 2,800 Death Damage. This includes the "''poetry of war''" DoT, but DoTs placed before this cheat DoT will remain.
***The target of these attacks will be the "most intimidating" Wizard (most [[:Category:Blade Spells|blades]] or casted [[:Category:Trap Spells|traps]]), or the Wizard in the first position (Sun spot) by default.
***If the Detonate (Death) cheat fizzles, he'll cast it again to hit his target, then recast it two more times (for a total of 4 casts - though nothing actually occurs the last two times). This may be intentional, for the purpose of removing any {{Link|Spell|Strangle}} dispels that are placed on him.
***{{Link|Spell|Shift}} or {{Link|Spell|Triage}} can be used to safely remove the DoT (make sure to time it in between the Detonate (Death).
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"I retain my resolve!"'' - If a Wizard casts a positive [[:Category:Ward Spells|ward]] (e.g. shields), he will cast a 0-pip {{Link|Spell|Shatter}} to remove the shields.
**Shields placed on other Wizards will not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Itemcard|Mass Feint}} will trigger this cheat.
**Multi-target spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}} or the {{Link|Spell|Spirit Armor}} applied by {{Link|Spell|Rebirth}}, will trigger this cheat, but only for the Wizard that cast the spell.
