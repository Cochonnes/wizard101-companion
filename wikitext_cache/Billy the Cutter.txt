| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Shield}}{{Icon|Absorb}} ''"I can use that!"'' - If a Wizard casts a universal Shield or single-target Absorb Spell, then Billy will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on the receiver.
**If a multi-target universal Shield Spell is cast, such as {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}}, then Billy will only steal the Ward from the caster.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"That'll help!"'' - If a Wizard casts an Ice, Fire, or universal Blade, then Billy will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the receiver.
**If a multi-target universal Blade is cast, such as {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}, then Billy will only steal the Blade from the caster.
**Using {{Link|Spell|Aegis}} will prevent this cheat.
**Pet Spells and Maycasts will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"This will cool you off!"'' - At the end of the fourth Round, and at the end of every eighth Round after, Billy will interrupt-cast Frost Giant.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"This will warm you up!"'' - At the end of the eighth Round, and at the end of every eighth Round after, Billy will interrupt-cast an enhanced version of {{Link|Spell|Fire Dragon}} that deals [[Spell:Fire Dragon (495+395)|495 Fire Damage plus 395 Fire Damage over three Rounds]].
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"You'll pay for that!"'' - After casting Fire Serpent, Fire Dragon, Heck Hound, using his cool off or warm up cheat, sometimes after using his {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack}} and Scald, and rarely after Meteor Strike, Billy will interrupt-cast a Fire Prism on a random Wizard, and then use his Natural Attack on that Wizard.
**If this cheat triggers while Billy has no Pips, then his Natural Attack will act as though he has one Pip.
**Billy will only use this cheat after his Natural Attack if the sound that accompanies his Natural Attack is more of a growl than a grunt.
**Although his Natural Attack is not cast when used through this cheat, it can still be Dispelled.
