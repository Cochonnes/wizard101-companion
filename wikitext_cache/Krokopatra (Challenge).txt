{{Icon|Late}} ''"Your Queen has no patience for tardiness!"''' - If a Wizard is late to combat, Krokopatra casts a Moon version of '''Insane Bolt''' to all enemies dealing '''550''', '''1,050''' or '''1,550''' Moon Damage.

{{Icon|Damage}} ''"You dare strike your Queen?!"'' - If Krokopatra is attacked, she will cast a version of '''Healing Current''' that heals for '''110''', '''440''', or '''1,100''' Health, before casting a Moon version of '''Insane Bolt''' to all enemies dealing '''550''', '''1,050''' or '''1,550''' Moon Damage.

{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"I shrug off such paltry magic"''' - If a Wizard casts a Damage over Time effect on Krokopatra, she will cast {{Link|Spell|Stormzilla (Tier 5b)}}.

{{Icon|Blade}} ''"You can't charm me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade or Global, Krokopatra will either cast an AoE Moon version of Enfeeble, or Healing Current on herself.

{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Your clumsy traps are no match for my grace"'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap, Krokopatra will remove all traps on herself.

{{Icon|Weakness}} ''"You can't weaken the queen!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Damage Debuff on Krokopatra, she will cast a version of Hammer of Thor dealing 525 Damage.

{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"I will not be triumphed over!"'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Dissipate}}, Krokopatra will cast a Moon version of Insane Bolt to all enemies dealing '''550''', '''1,050''' or '''1,550''' Moon Damage.
