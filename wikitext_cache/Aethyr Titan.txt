The Aethyr Titan is an enhanced version of the Storm Titan. He is the final boss of Empyrea as well as the last boss of the Third Arc. The Wizard takes the form as the {{Link|Polymorph|Divine Paradox}} and must recover the Chaos Heart from him.
*This Creature is named "Aethyr Titan", but his speech calls him "Aether Titan".
*The Paradox Wizard is Immune to Stun.
**Due to the bug where Power Pip Chance and Stun Resistance would not apply to the Paradox wizard correctly, the wizard can still be stunned.
*The Quest, ''Braving the Storm'', and the battle can be repeated if the Wizard returns to the summit.
