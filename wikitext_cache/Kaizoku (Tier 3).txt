*{{Icon|Shield}} At the beginning of the first Round, Kaizoku will interrupt-cast a '''-80%''' version of Tower Shield on himself.
**{{Icon|Damage}} If Kaizoku is damaged by any Spell, then he will interrupt-cast to replace the Tower Shield.
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} Manipulation Spells that remove helpful Wards can be used to remove Kaizoku's Tower Shield without causing him to replace it.
**This cheat will stop occurring after all of Kaizoku's Minions have been defeated.
*{{Icon|Trap}} If a Trap is cast on Kaizoku, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} on himself to remove it.
**Mass Feint does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} At the beginning of every second Round, Kaizoku will interrupt-cast a Star Spell that deals '''300''' Star Damage to all Wizards.
**The first time this cheat occurs, Wizards will be damaged in the normal order from right to left.  The second time this cheat occurs, Wizards will be damaged from left to right.  The order in which Wizards are damaged alternates every time this cheat occurs.
**Sometimes, the Spell will deal '''600''' Star Damage instead of the usual '''75''' Star Damage.
**{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} After all of Kaizoku's Minions have been defeated, Kaizoku will begin interrupt-casting this Spell ''every'' Round, and twice in a row every fourth Round.
**{{Icon|Ward}} ''"You stopped my Halberd?"'' - If a Wizard that is damaged by this cheat had any Wards, not including Damage Absorption Wards, then the Spell will not damage any other Wizards, and Kaizoku will interrupt-cast a Spell to remove all Wards from that Wizard and himself.  This will not trigger if the Wizard that has a Ward is the last Wizard to be damaged.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Time to turn things up!"''  - After all of Kaizoku's Minions have been defeated, he will remove all Wards from himself.
