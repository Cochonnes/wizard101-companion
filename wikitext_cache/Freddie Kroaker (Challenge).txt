'''Freddie Kroaker''':
*{{Icon|Dispel}}{{Icon|Ward}} ''"One, two, I'm coming for you!"'' - At the beginning of the the first Round, Freddie Kroaker will cast a Shadow Spell that applies a Dispel Charm of the Wizard's School on each Wizard, and then place a 'Nightmare Prone' Stun Shield on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Charm}}{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"Let the malus grow! Let it feed my beast!"'' - At the beginning of every Round after the second Round, Freddie Kroaker casts a Moon version of Balanceblade that says "GROWTH," and then a Dispel Charm of the Wizard's School on each Wizard.
**The above cheat will not activate if a "GROWTH" Charm was cleared by his cheats the previous turn.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"It lives! My Juggernaut Crab of Malus lives! The Great Barrier will fall!"'' - At the end of each Round, if Freddie Kroaker has 3 "GROWTH" Charms, he will cast a Moon version of Call of Khrulhu that deals 1,000,000 Damage on all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Healing|25px}} - ''"This better not be love conquering all!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, a positive Healing Charm, a Healing Global, or an outgoing Healing Aura, then Freddie Kroaker will cast a version of Enfeeble that removes all Charms from himself, and a version of Cleanse Ward that removes all Wards (including the 'Nightmare Prone' mark) from the receiver.
*{{Icon|All Friends (Squared)|25px}} - ''"You're spread too thin! Three, four, we've only started our war!"'' - If a Wizard casts an All Allies Spell, Freddie Kroaker will cast an Equalize Global, setting the Damage cap to 3,000.
*{{Icon|Charm|25px}} - ''"Blades are the forte of Storm, Death and Life!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Helpful Charm and is not Storm, Death, or Life, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Ice Wyvern (Tier 5b)}} on the receiver.
*{{Icon|Curse|25px}} - ''"Negative Charms are the forte of Death, Storm and Ice!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Harmful Charm and is not Death, Storm, or Ice, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Stone Colossus (Tier 5b)}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Ward|25px}} - ''"Shields are the forte of Ice, Myth and Death!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Helpful Ward and is not Ice, Myth, or Death, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Helephant (Tier 5b)}} on the receiver.
*{{Icon|Jinx|25px}} - ''"Traps are the forte of Myth, Fire and Ice!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Harmful Ward and is not Myth, Fire, or Ice, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Centaur (Tier 5b)}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time|25px}} - ''"DoT's are the forte of Fire, Myth and Life!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Damage over Time and is not Fire, Myth, or Life, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Stormzilla (Tier 5b)}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Heal over Time|25px}} - ''"Heals are the forte of Life, Storm and Fire!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Heal over Time and is not Life, Storm, or Fire, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Skeletal Pirate (Tier 5b)}} on the receiver.
*{{Icon|Pip|25px}} - ''"Pips are the forte of Balance!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Pip manipulation Spell, such as {{Link|Spell|Empower}} or {{Link|Spell|Dark Surge}}, and is not Balance, Freddie Kroaker will cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} on the receiver.
