Krampus is a Boss that appears during the Yuletide event. He is located in Krampusgarten, which requires a {{Link|House|Gold Skeleton Key}} to enter.
*Minions can be any combination of Chilling Helper (Tier 1) and Stormy Helper (Tier 1).
*This Tier is for level 1-50 Wizards.

| speech =

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Naughty naughty! Punctuality is a virtue!"'' - If a Wizard joins after the duel has started, Krampus will cheat-cast an enhanced version of Power Link on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"A gift for Krampus? You shouldn't have!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell, he '''may''' cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm (Shadow)}} that steals all Charms from the Wizard who cast the Blade.
**He will occasionally cast this throughout the battle.
**Pet Spells can trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Naughty, naughty! Krampus see's all!"'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} Spell, he will cast a version of [[:Spell:Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)|Cleanse Ward]] two times (which makes {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} ineffective).
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Krampus is everywhere!"'' -  At the beginning of every third Round, he will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that transfers all negative hanging effects that are on him (i.e. [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]]) to a random friend or foe.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"'Tis the season of Krampus!"'' - At the beginning of Round 5, he will cast a Shadow Spell to summon any combination of Chilling Helpers and Stormy Helpers.
**Whenever a Minion is defeated, he will resummon another one after one Round.
