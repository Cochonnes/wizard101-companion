Frithir is a Dark Sprite that resides in a fairy circle outside of the [[Location:Boar's Camp|Splithoof Barbarian camp]].  When assisting {{Link|NPC|Hugin Fateseer}} in gathering ingredients for a potion, Wizards are sent by the old Raven to bring him Frithir's wings.<br/><br/>

Frithir is flanked by two Forest Pests.  While lone Wizards will only have to worry about one Forest Pest Minion, groups of Wizards will encounter Frithir accompanied by both Forest Pests in combat.
