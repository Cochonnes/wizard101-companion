| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Myth}} '''Zeus Sky Father'''
*{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"Did you think you could silence an Immortal?"'' - If a Dispel is placed on any enemy, Zeus Sky Father will remove it by cheat-casting {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm}}.
*{{Icon|All Enemies Spell}} ''"You deserve an immortal boon."'' - If an All Enemies Spell is used, Zeus Sky Father will cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current (325)}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Fallen Immortals must be avenged!"'' - If another Boss is defeated, Zeus Sky Father will cast his '''Earthquake''' Natural Attack every two Rounds.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Punishment shall be swift!"'' - If a single-target Spell is used, Zeus Sky Father may cast '''Shift Minotaur''' on the caster, dealing '''310''' Myth Damage and stunning for one Round.
*{{Icon|Storm}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Unleash the wrath of Sky and Earth!"'' - At the beginning of every fifth Round, Zeus will cast a version of {{Link|ItemCard|Wrath of Zeus}} dealing '''995''' Storm Damage to all Wizards, and casts Astraphobia.

{{Icon|Life}} '''Apollo Bright One'''
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"You try to smite an immortal?"'' - If any Boss is defeated before Apollo Bright One, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Eternal Leviathan (777)}}, which will revive the defeated Bosses.
*{{Icon|All Enemies Spell}} ''"The light shines upon those who share it!"'' - If any type of All Enemies [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell is used, Apollo Bright One will give the Wizard a {{Link|Spell|Guiding Light}}.

{{Icon|Fire}} '''Ares Savage Spear'''
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Your underhanded tactics are unbefitting of a true warrior!"'' - If a [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] is placed on an enemy, Ares will cheat-cast an {{Link|Spell|Efreet (1,000)}} on the Wizard who cast the Spell.
:*If Apollo Bright One and Zeus Sky Father have been defeated, this cheat can be prevented by using {{Link|Spell|Quench}}.
:*Spells that target all enemies, such as Windstorm do not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Such prowess, but I think you can do better!"'' - If a Damage Spell is used, Ares Savage Spear will give the Wizard a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] by casting {{Link|Spell|Dark Pact (+30%)}}.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"We battle on! For Zeus!"'' - If Zeus Sky Father has been defeated, Ares Savage Spear will use his Natural Attack at the start of each Round.

{{Icon|Storm}} '''Poseidon Earth Shaker'''
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Observe the might of a true weapon!"'' If a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell is used, Poseidon Earth Shaker will cast Shift Atlantean on the Wizard who received the Blade(s), causing an {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} and '''360''' Storm Damage.
:*{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} enchantment can be used to prevent any positive Charms from being removed.
:*{{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}} will only affect the caster.
*{{Icon|Aura}}{{Icon|Shadow Creature}} ''"The Seas are puddles without stars"'' - If an [[:Category:Aura Spells|Aura]] or [[:Category:Shadow Spells|Shadow Spell]] is used, Poseidon Earth Shaker will place a {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"The Sea mourns for mighty Zeus"'' - If Zeus Sky Father is defeated, Poseidon Earth Shaker will cast his Tempest Natural Attack every three Rounds.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Hades the Unseen'''
*{{icon|Healing}} ''"The selfish belong in Tartarus!"'' - If a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell is used on themselves, Hades the Unseen will cast Shift Wraith, dealing '''335''' Death Damage and adds a -90% {{Link|Spell|Infection}} to the Wizard.
:*This cheat will not trigger if a Wizard uses a single-target heal on another player.
:*AoE heals will trigger this cheat.
:*Pet heals will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"Your donation to Tartarus is greatly appreciated."'' - If a Wizard or a Boss is defeated, Hades the Unseen will cast {{Link|Spell|Donate Power (+3 Pips)}} to whoever cast the defeating Spell.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Zeus sends his regards from Tartarus"'' - If Zeus Sky Father is defeated, Hades the Unseen will cast his Natural Attack on a random Wizard every two Rounds.

:*''"Let our army shake the Earth"'' - Every tenth Round, Zeus will use his Earthquake Natural Attack
:**Ares Savage Spear will follow up to use his Natural Attack.

::*This cheat is followed by his {{Link|Spell|Lernaean Hydra}} Natural Attack.

*'''Zeus''' & '''Poseidon''' {{Icon|Myth}} {{Icon|Storm}}
:*''"Angry Skies make vengeful Seas!"'' - At the beginning of every fifth Round, Zeus Sky Father will cast his Earthquake Natural Attack which deals '''630''' Myth Damage to all Wizards and removes [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]] and [[:Category:Ward Spells|Wards]].
::*Poseidon may then follow this cheat with his Tempest Natural Attack, dealing '''960''' Storm Damage and stealing one Pip from each Wizard.

*'''Poseidon''' & '''Apollo''' {{Icon|Storm}} {{Icon|Life}}
:*''"The Seas boil beneath the angry Sun!"'' - Poseidon Earth Shaker will use his '''Snow Shark''' Natural Attack dealing '''650''' Ice Damage to all Wizards.
::*This cheat follows with Apollo Bright One's Natural Attack in the same turn.

*'''Poseidon''' & '''Hades''' {{Icon|Storm}} {{Icon|Death}}
:*''"Tsunami from the Underworld!"'' - Every fifth Round, Poseidon Earth Shaker will use his Snow Shark Natural Attack.
:**Hades will follow up with his {{Link|Spell|Lycian Chimera}} Natural Attack, which deals '''590''' Death Damage to all Wizards and adds a -45% version of {{Link|Spell|Virulent Plague}}.

:*''"Let the Underworld Seas turn to rage!"'' - Every tenth Round, Poseidon Earth Shaker will use his '''Snow Shark''' Natural Attack dealing '''650''' Ice Damage to all Wizards.
:**Hades the Unseen will follow up with {{Link|Spell|Lernaean Hydra}} Natural Attack, dealing '''795''' Storm Damage and adding a {{Link|Spell|Black Mantle}} to all Wizards.
:**During the first Round, Hades will cast his 4 Damage over Time Natural Attack before Poseidon.

*'''Hades''' & '''Ares''' {{Icon|Death}} {{Icon|Fire}}
:*''"Tartarus is the true victor in war!"'' - At the beginning of every first Round, Hades the Unseen will use his Natural Attack on a random Wizard that deals 4 Death Damage, then applies 4 DoT that deals '''1,175''' Death Damage four Rounds later, 1 Death Damage three Rounds later, 2 Death Damage two Rounds later, and 3 Death Damage for one Round with 100% Armor Piercing.
::*Ares Savage Spear will then follow this cheat with their Natural Attack.

*'''Ares''' & '''Apollo''' {{Icon|Fire}} {{Icon|Life}}
:*''"Wither under the Sun's rage!"'' - Every first Round, Ares Savage Spear will cast a Spell similar to {{Link|ItemCard|Wrath of Ares}} dealing '''705''' Fire Damage to all Wizards and {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} one DoT from each.
::*This cheat follows Apollo Bright One's Natural Attack which deals '''30 + 780''' Fire Damage over 3 Rounds to all Wizards, and '''525''' Healing to all Bosses over 3 Rounds.
