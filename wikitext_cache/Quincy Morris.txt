'''Quincy Morris'''
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"*Rrrrroooooaaaarrrrr!*"'' - At the beginning of the first round, Quincy Morris will polymorph into werewolf form.

***{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Gah! Snagged! I will recover!"'' Casting a {{Icon|Trap}} on Quincy Morris will decrease the time it takes for him to unpolymorph by a single round.
