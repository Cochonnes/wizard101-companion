| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Death}} '''Rattlebones'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the second Round, Rattlebones will cast '''{{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}}''' at the Wizard, which deals 3,000 Damage.
:*{{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*''"Rattlebones will be avenged!''" - The Fairy Queen will cast this cheat if Rattlebones has been defeated.
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"Let's dampen the Heals!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell, he will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}'''.
:*{{Link|Spell|Strangle}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
::*Once dispelled, heals can be cast successfully (for an unconfirmed amount of time).
:*Pet heals will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Why don't we trade!"'' - If a Feint is placed on Rattlebones, he will cast an Ice version of '''{{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}}''' to switch the [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]] so that the higher percentage is on the Wizard.
:*{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent this cheat.
:*{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
:*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.

{{Icon|Life}} '''Fairy Queen (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Fairies! Protect your Queen!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round (and every 4 Rounds after a Minion's death), the Fairy Queen will summon 2 [[Creature:Fairy|Fairies (Rank 14)]] of random Schools, which become active the following Round, and have 4,160 Health.
:*{{Link|Spell|Entangle}} will not dispel this cheat, but instead will cause the Minions to become active two Rounds later.
*{{Icon|Charm}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"You'll never be as charming as Rattlebones!"'' - If a [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charm]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] spell is cast, there is a random chance that she will cast {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} on the Wizard who received the Charm or cast the Global spell.
:*{{Link|Spell|Dissipate}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*If a multi-target Blade spell is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}), then she will only remove the Blade from the Wizard who cast the Spell.
:*Plague and Virulent Plague can trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Why don't we trade!"'' - If a Wizard places a Feint on the Fairy Queen, she will cast Swap Ward to switch the Traps so that the higher percentage is on the Wizard.
:*Melt can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*Indemnity can be used to prevent this cheat.
:*{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat
:*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"We fight as a team this time. Our victory is assured."'' - The Fairy Queen will cast '''{{Link|Spell|Satyr (990)}}''' on Rattlebones.

:'''Fairies'''
*The following Fairies will cast these cheats.
**''"I shall protect you at all costs, my queen!"'' - Whenever any Fairy Minion is under ~25% Health besides the Fire one, she'll cast a version of Sacrifice that deals 2,100 Death Damage to herself to heal [[:Creature:Fairy Queen (Rank 14)|Fairy Queen]] for 700 Health; (1,050 with Fairy Queen's Incoming Heal).

*{{Icon|Fire}} ''"Ashes to ashes, we all burn down!"'' - Whenever the [[:Creature:Fairy (Fire) (Rank 14)|Fairy]] is under ~25% Health, she'll cast a version of Immolate that deals 2,100 Fire Damage to herself to deal 600 Fire Damage to the Wizard who last attacked her.
**This spell isn't cast when the Wizard who attacked her is defeated.
