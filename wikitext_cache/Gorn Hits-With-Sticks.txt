| cheatnotes =
'''Gorn Hits-With-Sticks'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Sticks and stones won't break these bones."'' - At the beginning of the first Round, or any Round Gorn doesn't have an Aura, he will cheat-cast an ''"Invulnerability"'' -300% Brace Aura for 99 Rounds.
**This cheat will stop after Pork removes Gorn's Aura.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"I shall hit you with sticks!"'' - Gorn will say this when he casts his Natural Attack.

*Gorn's casts are scripted:
**Round 1: Nature's Wrath
**Round 2: Nature's Wrath (Tier 5a)
**Rounds 3 & 4: Drop Bear Fury (Tier 4a)
**Rounds 5 & 6: Centaur (Tier 5a)
**Round 7: Forest Lord (Tier 5a)
**Round 8: Natural Attack (Gorn Hits-With-Sticks)
**Rounds 9 & 10: Bonetree Lord
**Round 11+: Natural Attack (Gorn Hits-With-Sticks)

'''{{Link|Minion|Captain Pork}}'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"But I can... with some preparation. Hold your ground, Wizard, I'll think of something."'' - Captain Pork will say this when he joins the battle.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} If Captain Pork is defeated, he will cast a version of Blessing, restoring himself to 100% Health.
