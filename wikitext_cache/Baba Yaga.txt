| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard enters the duel late, Baba Yaga's Egg will interrupt the duel casting a Myth Spell that polymorphs the tardy Wizard into a {{Link|Polymorph|Polymorph Egg}}.
**The Wizard will have only one Spell present, {{Link|Spell|Hatch}}, which will unpolymorph the Wizard for 3 Pips.
**This cheat will still be triggered if a Wizard joins during the planning phase of the first Round.
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"Learn that at Ravenwood? How quaint!"'' - If a Wizard casts a positive Ward, such as a Shield, she may cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Shatter}} at the Wizard.
{{Icon|Round}} '''At the start of the 3rd Round'''
*{{Icon|Sun}}{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Let's take the bull by the horns!"''' - Baba Yaga will cast a Sun version of {{Link|Spell|Frost Minotaur}} on a random Wizard, which deals '''50 + 445''' Death Damage.  The Minotaur Statue Minion is summoned to the first spot (Dagger).
{{Icon|Round}}'''At the start of the 6th Round'''
*{{Icon|Sun}}{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Fall before my gorgon's gaze!"'' - Baba Yaga will cast a Sun version of {{Link|Spell|Medusa}} resembling Gorgon Statue on a random Wizard, which deals '''770''' Storm Damage and Stuns for 2 Rounds.  The Gorgon Statue Minion is summoned to the second spot (Key).
{{Icon|Round}} '''At the start of the 10th Round'''
*{{Icon|Sun}}{{Icon|Minion}} ''"My wyrm will cool things down!"'' - Baba Yaga will cast a Sun version of {{Link|Spell|Catalan}} resembling Wyrm Statue which deals '''700''' Ice Damage to and '''-25%''' Accuracy to all Wizards.  The Wyrm Statue Minion is summoned to the third spot (Gem).
