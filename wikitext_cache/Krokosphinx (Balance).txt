Krokosphinx (Balance) is a secret Boss, accessed after defeating {{Link|Creature|Khonsu (Standard)}}. This Boss is in a unique zone and can also only be accessed by teleporting to a placed mark, as well as after defeating Khonsu. The teleporter that appears after defeating this Boss does not bring you back to this zone.
*As the Boss in this battle, all drops are recorded on this page.

'''1st Riddle'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I am caustic and invisible; I can be measured, but never touched; and I am infinite, but always in short supply. What am I?"'' - At the end of the first Round, Krokosphinx will change each Wizards' Spell Decks, giving all Wizards the following Spells and '''20,000 Health''':
**{{GivesSpell|spells = 5|size = x150px|Candy|Crowns|Space|Summer|Time}}
**'''Correct Answer: Time'''
*''"Correct. Time rots the world, renders empire into ruins."'' - If answered correctly, Krokosphinx will cast a +100% Damage Trap on itself.
*''"WRONG!"'' - Krokosphinx will say this if answered incorrectly.

'''2nd Riddle'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"What is a bringer of death and life, light and shadow, and energy and exhaustion?"'' - At the end of the second Round, Krokosphinx will change each Wizards' Spell Decks, giving all Wizards the following Spells and '''20,000 Health''':
**{{GivesSpell|spells = 5|size = x150px|Conversation|Exercise|Sugar|The Moon|The Sun}}
**'''Correct Answer: The Sun'''
*''"Yes, the sun enables, but also burns. Its light casts shadow. And its energy brings wearying heat."'' - If answered correctly, Krokosphinx will cast a +100% Damage Trap on itself.
*''"WRONG!"'' - Krokosphinx will say this if answered incorrectly.

'''3rd Riddle'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I haunt like a ghost, ensnare like a trap and lie to my creator. I bring gentle comfort or bitter sickness. I am faded, but forever. What am I?"'' - At the end of the third Round, Krokosphinx will change each Wizards' Spell Decks, giving all Wizards the following Spells and '''20,000 Health''':
**{{GivesSpell|spells = 5|size = x150px|Hunger|Memory|Pictures|Poltergeist|Regret}}
**'''Correct Answer: Memory'''
*''"Indeed, memory is a fickle thing. It can deceive us, dismay us or remind us of happier days."'' - If answered correctly, Krokosphinx will cast a +100% Damage Trap on itself.
*''"WRONG!"'' - Krokosphinx will say this if answered incorrectly.
