| descrip =
Gnissa is a Spider that makes their home in the hollowed out trunk of the felled Gloomwood tree in the center of the Webwood region of Savarstaad Pass.  Wizards will most likely encounter them when gathering Gloomwood Bark for [[NPC:Hugin Fateseer|Hugin Fateseer's]] potion during the Quest "{{Link|Quest|Worse Than Its Bark}}".  While defeating Gnissa is not required for the Quest, Wizards who are not careful may end up having to duel them.<br/><br/>

Three Webwood Creepers circle around Gnissa in the Gloomwood trunk.  While lone Wizards will only have to worry about one Webwood Creeper Minion, groups of Wizards will encounter Gnissa accompanied by up to three Webwood Creepers in combat depending on the size of the group.
