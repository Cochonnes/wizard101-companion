| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Riposte!"'' - If Margherita is damaged by a Spell, then she will interrupt-cast Mythblade on herself, then use her Natural Attack on the Wizard that attacked her.
**This Mythblade will stack with her regularly cast Mythblade.
**If Margherita is damaged more than once by a single Spell, then she will use this cheat for every time that she is damaged on her attacker.
**Ticks from Damage over Time effects will not trigger this cheat.
**If this cheat triggers while Margherita has no Pips, then her Natural Attack will act as though she has one Pip.
**Although her Natural Attack is not cast when used through this cheat, it can still be Dispelled.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"I have studied in Crab Alley!"'' - Margherita will interrupt-cast Storm Prism and then use {{Link|Spell|Triton}} on a Wizard due to a few triggers.
**{{Icon|Friend}} If there are two Wizards present in the duel, then this cheat will trigger on the first Wizard to cast any kind of Spell.
***May-Cast Spells will trigger this cheat.
***If there was initially only one Wizard present in the duel, and a second joins later or a Minion is summoned, then this cheat will activate on the first Wizard to cast any kind of Spell when the second character becomes active in the duel.
***This cheat will not activate if there are initially more than three Wizards in the duel and a Wizard(s) flee, leaving only two Wizards left.
**{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} If a Lanyon Scratcher is defeated, then this cheat will trigger on whoever defeated the Scratcher.
***This cheat will only trigger once if more than one Laynon Scrathcer is defeated with a single Spell.
***This cheat will only trigger once if there are only two Wizards present in the duel and the first Spell cast by the Wizard team defeats a Lanyon Scratcher(s).
***This cheat will not trigger if a Lanyon Scratcher is defeated by a Damage over Time effect.
**Although Triton is not cast, it can be Dispelled.
*{{Icon|Spell}}{{Icon|Shield}}{{Icon|Trap}} ''"I pierce your defenses!"'' - If Margherita uses her Natural Attack on a Wizard and they still have a Shield or [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] placed on them after her Natural Attack, then she will interrupt to use {{Link|Spell|Pierce}} on the Wizard that she attacked.
*{{Icon|Spell}}{{Icon|Shield}}{{Icon|Trap}}{{Icon|Charm}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"I disarm your words!"'' - If Margherita uses her Natural Attack on a Wizard and they still have a Shield, Trap, or positive [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charm]] placed on them after her Natural Attack, or there is an active [[:Category:Global Spells|Global Spell]], and her previously used Natural Attack activated her Pierce cheat, then she will interrupt to use {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the Wizard that she attacked.
**Although Disarm is not cast, it can be Dispelled.
*{{Icon|Round}}{{Icon|Health}} ''"I invoke the power of Myth!"'' - At the beginning of the Round after Margherita's Health drops to 50% or lower, she will interrupt-cast a +35% version of {{Link|Spell|Time of Legend}}.
*{{Icon|Round}}{{Icon|Health}} ''"I'll back you into a trap!"'' - At the beginning of every Round after Margherita's Health drops to 50% or lower, she will interrupt-cast Myth Trap on a random Wizard.
