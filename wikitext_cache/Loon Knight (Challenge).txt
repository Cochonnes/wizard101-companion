'''Loon Knight'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Become Khonsu's Avatars!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Loon Knight will Polymorph each Luna-Tick Minion into one of the following forms for 99 Rounds, depending on the number of Wizards present:
**'''2 or fewer''': {{Link|Polymorph|Old Quack (Challenge)}}
**'''3 or more''': {{Link|Polymorph|Old Quack (Challenge)}}, {{Link|Polymorph|Quentin Quail (Challenge)}} and {{Link|Polymorph|Drake Lockley (Challenge)}}.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I am the Beak of Vengeance!"'' - At the beginning of every 3rd Round, Loon Knight will cast {{Link|Spell|Shatterhorn (Moon)}} on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"The Moon Phases..."'' - If there are no Luna-Tick Minions present, Loon Knight will Polymorph into one of the above forms, retaining all cheats listed above while gaining all cheats of that Polymorph. 
**{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"QUACK!"'' - Loon Knight will Polymorph into another one of the above forms, gaining the cheats of both that form and the previously Polymorphed form.

'''Old Quack'''
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"That's not the real you!"'' - If a Wizard casts a universal Helpful Charm, or a multi-blade Spell, such as {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}}, Old Quack will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} on the receiver of the Blade.
**If a multi-target Blade Spell is cast, such as {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}, Old Quack will cast an all enemies version of Enfeeble.
**A wizard casting a blade-type spell that is not of their original school will also trigger this cheat.
**Spells with secondary effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Savage Paw}}, will not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} '''will not''' prevent the removal of Blades.

'''Quentin Quail'''
*''"Let me see. Oh, yes! That's the ticket!"'' - If a Wizard casts one of the following hanging effects, Quentin will cheat-cast one of the following Spells:
**Secondary effects, such as the Blade on {{Link|Spell|Sound of Musicology}}, will trigger this cheat.
**{{Icon|Friend}}{{Icon|Charm}} Single Target Helpful Charm - {{Link|Spell|Weakness}}
**{{Icon|All Friends (Squared)}}{{Icon|Charm}} Helpful Charm on All Allies - {{Link|Spell|Mass Weakness}}
**{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Curse}} Single Target Harmful Charm - {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}}
***{{Link|Spell|Starfall}} will also trigger this cheat.
**{{Icon|All Enemies (Squared)}}{{Icon|Curse}} Harmful Charm on All Enemies - {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm (+25%)}}
**{{Icon|Friend}}{{Icon|Ward}} Single Target Helpful Ward - {{Link|Spell|Hex}}
**{{Icon|All Friends (Squared)}}{{Icon|Ward}} Helpful Ward on All Allies - {{Link|Spell|Mass Hex}}
**{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Jinx}} Single Target Harmful Ward - {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (Balance)}}
***{{Link|Spell|Attenuate}} will also trigger this cheat.
**{{Icon|All Enemies (Squared)}}{{Icon|Jinx}} Harmful Ward on All Enemies - {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}}

'''Drake Lockley'''
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Trap}}{{Icon|Aura}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"Time for the old one, two PUNCH!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Damage or Pierce Charm, Trap, Aura, or Global, Drake will cast a Storm version of {{Link|ItemCard|Duck Savage}} that deals '''800''' Storm Damage on the receiver.
**Multi-Target Spells will result in the Duck Savage being cast on the caster.
**Secondary effects will trigger this cheat.
