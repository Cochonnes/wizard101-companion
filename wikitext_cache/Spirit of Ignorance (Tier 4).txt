| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I... Am... INVINCIBLE!!!"'' - At the start of the Round, a '''-300%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Brace}}, lasting 99 Rounds, will be placed on Spirit of Ignorance (Tier 4).
**The Aura will be recast if it runs out.
**''"YIKES! What was that?!"'' - Spirit of Ignorance will say this if his Aura is removed, but will not recast it.
***Negative auras will trigger this cheat however the boss will retain his invulnerability.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"You can't Charm your way out of this!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, he'll use {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble (Shadow)}} on the target of the Blade.
**Multi-target Blade Spells, like {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}, will trigger this cheat for the caster only, and not the other Wizards.
**Global spells like {{Link|Spell|Darkwind}} will target the user with this cheat.
**Blades that are created as a secondary effect of a spell (such as {{Link|Spell|Catch of the Day (Tier 2b)}}) will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Feint? There's no time to pass out. I'm calling backup!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Feint on Spirit of Ignorance, he'll use a version of {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} with '''+500%''' Armor Piercing on himself.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"I won't fall into your Traps!"'' - If a Wizard casts a single target Trap on Spirit of Ignorance, he'll use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Star, -All)}} on himself.
**''"What? Why didn't that work?!"'' - Boss will react if a Wizard has a protected Charm that didn't get removed by Enfeeble, or if a protected Trap didn't get removed by Cleanse Ward.
**Using AoE Trap Spells will cause the Spirit of Ignorance to remove all Traps from the caster, instead of himself.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I cannot be defeated! I know your schools!"'' - The Round after his Aura is removed, Boss will use a '''-100%''' protected Shield of the Wizard's School, not including Balance.
**Each time after a Damage spell is used on the Boss, he'll recast all Shields based on the Wizard's School.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"No you don't!"'' - If a Wizard uses {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on his Shield, he'll recast all Shields based on the Wizard's School.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Muhahaha! Two rounds 'till you go DOWN!"'' - Every 5 Rounds, he will place a Shadow Deferred Overtime dealing 100,000 Shadow Damage after two Rounds on the Wizard.
