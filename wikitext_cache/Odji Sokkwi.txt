A member of the Sokkwi family, Odji Sokkwi guards the Frozen Forge in the [[Location:Krokosphinx|Krokosphinx's]] Hall of Champions.  After finding {{Link|NPC|General Khaba}}, Wizards help Khaba gather the materials necessary to create a new sword with which he can lead the Order of the Fang against the reawakened Tuts.  Once all of the components are collected, the general sends his troops into the hall's passageways while Wizards confront Odji to gain access to the forge and complete Khaba's weapon.

*Two additional Sokkwi Crushers will appear if more than two Wizards are present.
