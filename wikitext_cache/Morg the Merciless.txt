| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"I will not abide tardiness!"'' - If a Wizard is late and a Minion is defeated, Morg the Merciless will cast a Shadow spell to summon another random Minion.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"You will serve me forever!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Morg will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Guardian Spirit}} on a random enemy Minion.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Morg will protect you!"'' - Every round, Morg will cheat-cast a {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on a random enemy Minion, favoring Minions with the least amount of Shields.
**If a Wizard beguiles one of the Minions before this cheat begins and Morg has no allies, this will be cast on said Wizard.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"The stars burn for Morg!"'' - At the start of every third Round, a random enemy Minion will cheat-cast a Shadow {{Link|Spell|Power Nova}} that deals 500 Shadow Damage, steals 1 pip from each target, and grants each enemy in combat 1 pip.
**The outgoing Damage boost of the Creature casting it impacts the Damage it does.
**If all Wizards are beguiled, all enemies will still gain 1 pip despite no damage being dealt.
**If a Wizard beguiles one of the Minions before this cheat begins and Morg has no allies, said Wizard will cast this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Be as merciless as Morg!"'' - If a Damage over Time effect is placed on a Wizard, a random enemy Minion will cheat-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} that affects all enemies. This cheat does not trigger if the target was previously beguiled in the same Round.
**Shadow Detonates cheat-cast by his Minions can use Fireblades and Fire Traps.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Now you will see my true power!"'' - When all enemy Minions are defeated, Morg will cheat-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Regenerate}} on himself that removes 99% of his total Health. He will have 120 Health remaining.
