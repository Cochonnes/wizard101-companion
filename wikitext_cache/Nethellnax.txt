| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Heal over Time}} ''"The Life Energy won't stop!"'' - At the beginning of every Round, Nethellnax will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Ha-ha! A 1-Up!}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Healing}} If Nethellnax is defeated while his Heal over Time cheat is still active, he will revive with full Health.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"I am not this, I am Death!"'' - At the beginning of the fourth Round, and when Nethellnax revives, he gives Wizards the following Spell:
**{{GivesSpell|cards=1|size=x150px|Skeletal Pirate (Tier 2b)}}
*{{Icon|Death}} ''"Is that one of my tricks?"'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Skeletal Pirate (Tier 2b)}} on Nethellnax, he removes all Heal over Time effects on himself and stops his Heal over Time cheat.
**''"What... what just happened?"'' - Other Spells that remove Heal over Time effects for Weaknesses or Shields, such as {{Link|Spell|Dr. Von's Monster (Tier 2b)}} or {{Link|Spell|Primal Stampede}}, will trigger Nethellnax to remove all Heal over Time effects on himself and stop his Heal over Time cheat.
