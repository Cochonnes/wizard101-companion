| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Time for physical education!"'' - At the end of the first Round, this Boss says  and spawns his Minions.
**The Minions have higher Health than in the last (Level 2) battle: the two Edacious Ogre (Tier 3) Minions have 785 Health, and the Rude Goblin (Tier 3) has 750.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Extra credit time!"'' - Boss will only say this at the start when he has full minions. On every Round after the second Round, each Creature will use a {{Link|Spell|Dragonblade}} to the right Creature until the last Creature cheat-casts {{Link|Spell|Tempest}} dealing '''240''', '''320''', '''400''', or '''480''' Storm Damage.
**This cheat will occur even if the boss isn't in combat.
**Barbarous Van Der Borst will not cast Dragonblade if he's not in the first duel slot.
**If three Minions have their turn before the Boss, they'll cast in order second, third, and first.
**Barbarous Van Der Borst doesn't cast Tempest when in the last duel slot.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Time for extra credit!"'' - If the Minions are defeated before the Boss, he will summon one Rude Goblin (Tier 3) at a time.
