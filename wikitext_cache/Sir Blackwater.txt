| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Charm}} {{Icon|Global}} ''"I disarm you Wizard!"'' - If a Wizard casts a positive [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charm]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] spell, Sir Blackwater will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the Wizard who cast the Spell.
**"Weapon Cast"  and Pet "Maycast" that grant Blades will trigger this cheat.
**Armor Piercing Charms will not trigger this cheat. (Example: Stormspear)
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} can be used to avoid this cheat.
*{{Icon|Weakness}} ''"I reach inside myself to harm you!"'' - If a Wizard places any type of {{Link|Spell|Weakness}} Spell on Sir Blackwater, he will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute (+30%)}} on himself.
**AOE Weakness Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Virulent Plague}}, will not trigger this cheat.

'''Interrupt Cycles:'''
All cycles are 5 Rounds each, and repeat until Sir Blackwater is defeated.
:{{Icon|Death}} '''Death Interrupt Cycle''' (First Cycle):
:*''"I make this sacrifice!"'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, Sir Blackwater will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice (Death)}} on himself.
:*''"I call upon the spirits of the deep!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Age of Reckoning}}, {{Link|Spell|Virulence}}, a {{Link|Spell|Deathblade (+45%)}}, and {{Link|Spell|From Beyond}}. This Round will be the end of the Death cycle and the beginning of the Life cycle.

:{{Icon|Life}} '''Life Interrupt Cycle''' (Second Cycle):
:*''"Darkmoor armors us!"'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, Sir Blackwater will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Armor (500)}} on himself.
:*''"I am steadfast in my resolve!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Sanctuary}}, {{Link|Spell|Cycle of Life}}, a Guiding Light, and {{Link|Spell|Rebirth (730, 90%)}}. This Round will be the end of the Life cycle and the beginning of the Storm cycle.

:{{Icon|Storm}} '''Storm Interrupt Cycle''' (Third Cycle):
:*''"I lay on hands."'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, Sir Blackwater will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on himself.
:*''"The power of the Storm will destroy you!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Astraphobia}}, {{Link|Spell|Galvanic Field}}, a {{Link|Spell|Stormblade}}, and {{Link|Spell|Gloambugs!}}. This Round will be the end of the Storm cycle and the beginning of the Death cycle.
