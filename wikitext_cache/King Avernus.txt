| descrip =
The fight against King Avernus will vary depending on which path was taken through the Gauntlet.

'''Minions'''
*If "''Follow Nefertus''" (Right Room) was chosen first, the Earl of Lichenten will not be present in the fight.
*If "''Destroy Deep Spawn Nest''" (Middle Room) was chosen second, the Deep Spawn Queen will not be present in the fight.
*If "''Stop Nefertus''" (Left Room) was chosen third, Nefertus will not be present in the fight.

'''Cheats'''
*If "''Seek Blessing''" (Middle Room) was chosen first, King Avernus' Relic of Eternal Avernus cheat will not trigger, and it will instead trigger for the Wizard's team.
*If "''Find Treasure''" (Left Room) was chosen second, King Avernus' Emblem of Avernal Shielding cheat will not trigger, and it will instead trigger for the Wizard's team.
*If "''Sneak Around''" (Right Room) was chosen third, King Avernus' Medallion of Shadow Summon cheat will not trigger, and it will instead trigger for the Wizard's team.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Random}} ''"King Avernus casts Gut Punch of Paralysis. Does it succeed?"'' - At the end of every second Round, King Avernus will cast an Ice Spell on the Wizard with the highest Health that will randomly do one of the following effects:
**''"Critical success! You're stunned!"'' - The targeted Wizard will take 200 Ice Damage and is Stunned.
**''"It does succeed! You take damage!"'' - The targeted Wizard will take 200 Ice Damage.
**''"It fails!"'' - Nothing happens.
**''"Critical fail! He stuns himself!"'' - King Avernus is Stunned.

'''King Avernus' Relic Cheats'''
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"I call upon the Relic of Eternal Avernus to revive me!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, if "''Seek Blessing''" (Middle Room) was ''not'' chosen first,  King Avernus will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Guardian Spirit}} that restores 17% of his maximum Health (362 Health) upon being defeated.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Emblem of Avernal Shielding, protect me!"'' - If "''Find Treasure''" (Left Room) was ''not'' chosen second, the first time King Avernus is targeted or Damaged by an attack Spell each turn, he will cast a -50 Damage Ward on himself.
*{{Icon|Shadow Creature}} ''"Medallion of Shadow Summon, bring me a thrall from the void!"'' - At the beginning of the third Round, if "''Sneak Around''" (Right Room) was ''not'' chosen third,  King Avernus will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Dark Fiend}} that deals 200 Shadow Damage to all enemies with no additional effects.

'''Wizards' Relic Cheats'''
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"You have my Relic of Eternal Avernus? No!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, if "''Seek Blessing''" (Middle Room) was chosen first, a version of Guardian Spirit will be cast on all Wizards that restores 50% of their maximum Health upon being defeated.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"No, Emblem of Avernal Shielding, don't protect them!"'' - If "''Find Treasure''" (Left Room) was chosen second, after a Wizard is targeted by an attack Spell, a -50 Damage Ward is applied on them.
**If the Wizard's team is targeted with an All Enemies Spell, all Wizards will receive a -25 Damage Ward.
*{{Icon|Shadow Creature}} ''"You have stolen my Medallion of Shadow Summon?"'' - At the beginning of the third Round, if "''Sneak Around''" (Right Room) was chosen third, a random Wizard will cast a version of Dark Fiend that deals 1,700 Shadow Damage to King Avernus and has no additional effects.
