| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Late arrivals may not be seated!"'' - If a Wizard joins the Duel after the first Round, then MacDeath will interrupt-cast a '''+75%/+20%''' version of [[Spell:Feint (+75%)|Feint]], a '''+25%''' version of [[Spell:Curse (+25%)|Curse]], and a '''+35%''' version of [[Spell:Hex (+35%)|Hex]] on the late Wizard.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"To be or not to be? To be obviously"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, MacDeath will interrupt-cast a '''-100%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Fortify}}.
**{{Icon|Damage}}{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Et tu, Wizard?"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Damage Spells|Damage]] or [[:Category:Steal Spells|Steal]] Spell on MacDeath, then he will interrupt-cast a version of Fortify that is 10% less effective than his current Aura.  This cheat will repeat every time that MacDeath is damaged until his Aura reduces incoming Damage by '''-10%'''.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"May a choir of understudies sing thee to my rest"'' - MacDeath will interrupt-cast a Moon Spell that summons a MacStorm, MacLife, or MacDeath Understudy.
*{{Icon|Global}}{{Icon|Healing}} ''"Out, out, brief candle!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spell, then MacDeath will interrupt-cast a '''-75%''' version of [[Spell:Doom and Gloom (-75%)|Doom and Gloom]].
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"We few, we happy few, we band of beguileds"'' - MacDeath will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Beguile}} on a Wizard.

*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"This is your moment in the spotlight!"'' - If a Wizard casts Feint on MacDeath, then one of his Understudies will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} on him.

*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Wizards die a thousand times before their death!"'' - If a Damage over Time effect is cast on MacDeath, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Shift}} on the caster.
**Spells that apply Damage over Time effects on all enemies may result in the effect being shifted to a Wizard that was not the caster.
