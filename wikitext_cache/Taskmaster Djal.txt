Taskmaster Djal is the Krok in charge of the enslavement of the Manders in the Karanahn Barracks.  Wizards are sent by {{Link|NPC|Salid K'fur}} to face him during the Quest "The Betrayer".  Wizards can place a sleep potion obtained from the [[Quest:The Awakening|prior Quest]] to poison Djal's guards' food, located behind {{Link|Creature|Kiwu}}, so that he may be fought alone; if his guards' food remains unpoisoned, then there will be 4 {{Link|Creature|Karanahn Elite Guard}} minions present to assist him.
