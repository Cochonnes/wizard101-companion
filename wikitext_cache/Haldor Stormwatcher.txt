Haldor Stormwatcher is the quartermaster of the Red Claw, maintaining the raiders' armory in their fort of Frostholm with ambitions of invading {{Link|Location|Northguard}}.  When arriving in Frostholm to reach Ivar Andersson, Wizards are sent by {{Link|NPC|Gunnar Quickaxe}} to give Haldor the weapon which they presented as an offering to the Red Claw.  When Wizards arrive at the armory but refuse to give Haldor their weapon, he takes offense and attacks them.

Two additional Red Claw Strikers will appear if more than two Wizards are present.  These additional Red Claw Strikers will already be wandering the area prior to talking to {{Link|NPC|Haldor Stormwatcher}} and can be defeated before having to duel him.
