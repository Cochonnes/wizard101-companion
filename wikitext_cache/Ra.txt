| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Let us accelerate this contest"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Ra will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Balance of Power}}.
**{{Icon|Global}} ''"No no, we play by my rules"'' - If a Wizard attempts to replace the Global, Ra will recast Balance of Power.
*{{Icon|Random}} ''"Accuracy is such a fickle thing"'' - Randomly, Ra will use his turn to cast Black Mantle or a '''+10%''' version of Precision, either of which can affect either all allies or all enemies.
**If Ra is [[ItemCard:Arcane Deduction|Spied]] when this cheat occurs, only the normal versions of Precision or Black Mantle will be revealed.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Let's balance things out a bit!"'' - At the end of every fourth Round, Ra will interrupt-cast a Spell that applies an Aura on all participants in the duel. The first Aura that is cast will be {{Link|Spell|Amplify}}, then {{Link|Spell|Fortify}}, and then it will cycle between the two until the duel ends.
