*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Lets make this interesting."'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Apep will interrupt cast a {{Link|Spell|Balance of Power}}.
**''"You'll have to do better than that!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Global Spell that gives a Damage bonus, such as {{Link|Spell|Balefrost}}, he will immediately replace it back with {{Link|Spell|Power Play}}.
***{{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom}}, and {{Link|Spell|Sanctuary}} will trigger this Cheat.
***{{Link|Spell|Power Play}} will not trigger this Cheat.
***Damage Spells that cast a Global effect, such as {{Link|TreasureCard|White Rat Magician}}, will not trigger this Cheat.
***Global Spells that give Critical and Armor Piercing effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Katabatic Wind}}, will not trigger this Cheat.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} If a Pit Viper is defeated (or is missing at the start of the battle due to being defeated in an earlier attempt), Apep will summon a Minion with 1,150 Health. This Minion will attack for two Rounds, before Apep sacrifices it with {{Link|Spell|Draw Health}}, {{Link|Spell|Sap Power}}, {{Link|Spell|Siphon Health}}, or {{Link|Spell|Take Power}}.

*{{Icon|Spell}} When Apep uses one of his Minion Sacrifice Spells, he casts the Spell from first to last once. He then hidden casts the Spells two more times on each Minion.
**When Dispelled once, it skips over the first Minion, but after others are sacrificed, the Minion he skipped over will still be sacrificed.
**When with all his Minions, it takes 7 Dispels to save one, 8 to save 2, and 9 to save all 3.
**When one Minion is in combat with two other Minions, it takes 3 Dispels to save him.
**When two Minions are in combat with one other Minion, it takes 6 Dispels to save them.
**The healing Spells will heal Apep even if the Minion he is trying to sacrifice is already dead (so if all three Pit Vipers have been defeated then he will be healed nine times). This may be a bug. The Pip-gaining Spells will only give Apep Pips once per Minion.
