| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You're late... or are you early?"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the second Round, then Grandfather Spider will interrupt-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}} on the Wizard, dealing 2,000 Shadow Damage and removing 3 pips.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"You will not defeat me!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Grandfather Spider will summon a {{Link|Creature|Spiderling}} Minion.  The Minion will become active on the next Round and interrupt-cast a permanent version of {{Link|Spell|Shadow Sentinel}}  which provides  '''Intercept 90%''', '''+80''' Critical Block, and '''-30%''' Incoming Damage.
:*If the Spiderling is defeated, then Grandfather Spider will summon another one three Rounds later.
:*Only one {{Link|Creature|Spiderling}} can be up at a time.
:*Grandfather Spider will only summon as many Spiderlings as there are Wizards engaged in the duel. Once that number of Spiderlings has been defeated, he will unsuccessfully attempt to summon a Minion every three Rounds.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Spin the wheel! I'm enjoying this!"'' - At the end of every third Round, Grandfather Spider will interrupt-cast a Spell that switches the first turn of the duel.
*{{Icon|Shadow}}{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Time, time, time, see what's become of me"'' - At the end of a number of Rounds dependent on the number of Wizards engaged in the duel, Grandfather Spider will interrupt-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that steals all positive charms and wards from all Wizards.
:*Every 8 Rounds with 1 Wizard present.
:*Every 7 Rounds with 2 Wizards present.
:*Every 6 Rounds with 3 Wizards present.
:*Every 5 Rounds with 4 Wizards present.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Surprised? So am I!"'' - Grandfather Spider will sometimes use his Natural Attack on a random Wizard and then cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Storm}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"You had your chance, I'm ending this"'' - Grandfather Spider will cast a {{Link|Spell|Shadow Sultan}}.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"That's the spirit!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on Grandfather Spider, then he will cast Swap Ward to switch the traps with the Wizard who cast it.
**This can be avoided with {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} or {{Link|Spell|Melt}}.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
** The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Time needs chaos! One direction is boring!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju}} is placed on Grandfather Spider, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} on himself.
