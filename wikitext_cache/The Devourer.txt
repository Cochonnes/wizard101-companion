*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"<SHRIEK!>"'' - If a Feint is placed on him, The Devourer will cast Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (-2)}} on himself which removes the last two Traps placed.
**This can be avoided by using {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}}.
**The balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.

{{Icon|Round}} '''Devour Cheat'''
*After the first Round, and once more after the sixth, he will cast a Natural Attack that "devours" a random Wizard.
**The Natural Attack removes all unprotected Wards, Charms, and Auras from the Wizard.
**After being devoured, the Wizard will lose their combat interface and will be forced to pass for the duration of the idle timer. The Wizard is not able to be targeted and gains no Pips during this state.
**Three rounds later, during his turn, he will cheat cast an AoE Shadow Natural Attack for '''1,500''' Shadow Damage, and return the devoured Wizard to combat.
**He will then summon one of the above Minions.

{{Icon|Round}} '''Interrupt Cycles'''
*At the beginning of every third round, if a positive Charm is present on any wizard, The Devourer will cast an AoE version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm (Shadow)}} that steals one positive Charm.
*At the end of every fifth Round, if a negative ward is present on himself, The Devourer will cast a Shadow version of Cleanse Ward on himself, which will remove all Traps.
**For each Trap he removes, he will add a protected {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade (+100%)}} to his Minions or himself.
***Indemnity-Protected Traps will result in two Blades being added.
