| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Myth}} '''Meowiarty'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Let's start this party off with a bang!"'' - At the start of the first Round, he will attack a random Wizard, leaving a defered DoT that deals '''2,345''' Shadow Damage after 3 Rounds.
:*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Tisk, tisk!"'' - Using {{Link|Spell|Triage}} or Mass Triage will trigger a 2,000 Shadow Damage {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Round}} After every five Rounds, Meowiarty will place +10% Traps Damage Ward on him and his Allies.
:*''"Blast! You foiled my ingenious plan!"'' - If all the "locks" Damage Wards are removed, he will cheat-cast Mass Triage on himself.
:*''"Poor Wizard, you can't win for losing."'' - If the last "lock" Damage Ward is removed by an Attack All Enemies Spell, he will cheat-cast a 2,000 Shadow Damage {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}} on the Wizard who cast the AoE.
:*''"Tick... Tock... Boom! Too late!"'' - If any of the "locks" Damage Wards remain after five Rounds, he will remove all "Locks" and apply the same Shadow deferred DoT and replace the "locks."

{{Icon|Balance}} '''O'Leary Thug (Rank 13)'''
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Every star fears the Supernova!"'' - If a Wizard casts an Aura Spell, the O'Leary Thug will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Supernova (535, 85%)}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"A thug's job is to balance the fight!"'' - If a Wizard has five or more Pips while casting a Spell, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} on the Wizard.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Midnighter (Rank 13)'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Keep up the good work!"'' - If an enemy successfully casts an attack, the Midnighter will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Empower}} on the Ally.
*{{Icon|Accuracy}} ''"Bad luck abounds!"'' - If an enemy Spell fizzles, he will cheat-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Black Cat}} dealing '''1,000''' Damage on the fizzled Spells' target (or a random Wizard if the Spell was intended for an Ally).
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Sharp move, my friend!"'' - If an enemy casts a Blade Spell, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Pact (+30%)}} on the receiver of the Blade.

{{Icon|Storm}} '''Dr. Von Katzenstein (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Death}}{{Icon|Spell}} '''Interrupt Cycle:'''
:*''"Tis a good time for my monster to come out to play!"'' - At the end of every third Round, Dr. Von Katzenstein will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Dr. Von's Monster (1,005)}} on a random Wizard.
:*''"Don't worry, there's time for everyone to play!"'' - At the start of every fourth Round, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Monster Mash (1,170)}} on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"It's not safe to run with Blades!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Damage Charm, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} on the receiver of the Blade.
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"One must keep a sanitary work environment!"'' - If a Wizard casts a negative Damage Charm, he will cast an AoE version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm}}.

{{Icon|Fire}} '''Gibson O'Leary (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Blade}} '''Blade Cheats:'''
:*''"Uh oh, you dropped your little Blade!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade Damage Charm, Gibson O'Leary will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the receiver of the Blade, then uses a Fire version of Cleanse Charm to remove the Blade from himself.
:*''"I think that Blade would look better on me!"'' - If a universal Blade Damage Charm, {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}}, or {{Link|Spell|Fireblade}} is cast, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the receiver of the Blade.
*{{Icon|Trap}} '''Trap Cheats:'''
:*''"You can't mess with us!"'' - If any type of universal Trap Damage Ward is placed on an enemy, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Backdraft}} on the Wizard who cast the Trap.
:*''"Allow me to lower the boom!"'' - When a single Trap Damage Ward or DoT is placed on a Wizard, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} on the Wizard.

{{Icon|Ice}} '''Jacques the Scratcher (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Shield}} '''Shield Cheats:'''
:*''"For me? You shouldn't have!"'' - If any type of universal Shield Ward is cast, Jacques the Scratcher will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on the receiver of the Shield.
:*''"No need for such things here!"'' - If any type of School-specific Shield Ward is cast, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on the receiver of the Shield, and then proceed to destroy it.
*{{Icon|Heal over Time}} ''"You can't have nice things when there are cats around!"'' - If any type of Heal over Time Healing Spell is cast, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Snow Drift}} on the receiver of the Heal.
:*Pet Heals will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Allow me to scratch some of that dirt off you, friend."'' - If an enemy takes Damage from a DoT Spell, he will cheat-cast Cooldown on the Ally.
