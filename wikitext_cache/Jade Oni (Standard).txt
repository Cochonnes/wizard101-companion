| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Life}}{{Icon|Storm}} '''Jade Oni'''
*{{Icon|Ward|22px}} ''"Jade ancestors, protect me!"'' - On the fifth Round, Jade Oni will cast a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Armor}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Trap|20px}} ''"Your Tricks become my Treasure"'' - If a Trap is placed on Jade Oni, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Oni's Naturalism}}.
**Multi-Target Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time|20px}} ''"Sharing is Caring. Bwahaha!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Damage over Time on Jade Oni, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Camp Bandit (Tier 4b)}}.
*''"Your Doom is Imminent"'' - Jade Oni casts a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blade}} on self, warning of the impending Gambit.
*'''x4''' {{Icon|Blade|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"You were warned. Bwahaha!"'' - If Jade Oni reaches a Gambit of having four Blades, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Primal Oni}}. All Blades will be removed from all enemies after this Gambit has been used.

{{Icon|Death}}{{Icon|Ice}} '''Death Oni'''
*{{Icon|Blade|20px}} ''"You dare mock Death?"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, Death Oni will cast {{Link|Spell|Putrefaction}} on the receiver.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} will not protect Blades from removal.
**Spells with secondary effects will trigger this cheat.
**Multi-Target Blade Spells will trigger an AoE version of Putrefaction.
*{{Icon|Heal over Time|20px}} ''"You must play by the Death Oni's Rules!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Heal over Time, Death Oni will cast {{Link|Spell|Oni's Morbidity}}.
*{{Icon|Ward|22px}} ''"Spirits of the Dead Protect Me!"'' - Death Oni casts a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Shield}} on himself after casting a Shield, warning of the impending Gambit.
*'''x4''' {{Icon|Ward|22px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Think you're tough?"'' - If Death Oni reaches a Gambit of having four Shields, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom Oni}}. All Wards will be removed from all enemies after this Gambit has been used.

{{Icon|Balance}}{{Icon|Death}} '''Plague Oni'''
*{{Icon|Curse|20px}} ''"A pox upon your house!"'' - On the third Round, Plague Oni casts {{Link|Spell|Plague}}.
*{{Icon|Blade|20px}} ''"Those with the Most have the Most to lose."'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, Plague Oni will cast {{Link|Spell|Oni's Shadow}}.
**Spells with secondary effects will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Trap|20px}} ''"Time for the tables to turn"'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap, Plague Oni will cast {{Link|Spell|Righting the Scales}} on the caster.
**Multi-Target Spells will trigger this cheat.
*''"Your Doom is Imminent"'' - Plague Oni casts a {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}} on itself and {{Link|Spell|Mass Infection}}, when it has a Blade, and a Wizard has a negative Charm, warning of the impending Gambit.
**This Balanceblade can stack with the Balanceblade he casts from his hand.
*'''x2''' {{Icon|Blade|20px}} '''& x2''' {{Icon|Curse|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Greed will be your downfall!"'' - If Plague Oni reaches a Gambit of having two Blades on itself, and 2 harmful Charms on the Wizard, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Tribunal Oni}}. All Charms will be removed from all enemies and allies after this Gambit has been used.

{{Icon|Fire}}{{Icon|Life}} '''War Oni'''
*{{Icon|Ward|22px}} ''"Your armaments are no match for the Heat of the Forge"'' - If a Wizard casts a Shield, War Oni will cast {{Link|Spell|Oni's Forge}} on the receiver.
*''"Let the war rage on!"'': 
**{{Icon|All Enemies|40px}} If War Oni is damaged with an AoE Spell, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Pigsie (Tier 5b)}}. 
***If War Oni is defeated with an AoE Spell, he will instead cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Pigsie}} that restores 100% Health.
**{{Icon|Heal over Time|20px}} If War Oni has a HoT, he casts {{Link|Spell|Sprite}} on himself, warning of the impending Gambit.
*'''x2''' {{Icon|Heal over Time|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Survival is the dominion of the fittest!"'' - If War Oni reaches a Gambit of having two HoTs, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Infernal Oni}}. All HoTs will be removed from all enemies after this Gambit has been used.
