High Priestess Cameca is the Boss summoned on the Southern portion of the Central Pyramid, after inputting the wildlife, school, and astral tokens found in the {{Link|Location|Xibalba Wastes (South)}}. The drum used to spawn Cameca only appears after using the {{Link|Spell|Reveal Invisible}} Cantrip Spell.
