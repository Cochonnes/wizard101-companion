Krampus is a Boss that appears during the Yuletide event.  He is located in Krampusgarten, which requires a {{Link|House|Gold Skeleton Key}} to enter.
*Minions can be any combination of {{Link|Creature|Chilling Helper (Tier 3)}} and {{Link|Creature|Stormy Helper (Tier 3)}}.
*This Tier is for level 110-{{MaxLevel}} Wizards.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Naughty, naughty! Punctuality is a virtue!"'' - If a Wizard joins after the duel has started, Krampus will cheat-cast a Shadow version of Scald dealing '''750''' Fire Damage over three Rounds and a '''-75%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Smoke Mantle}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"A gift for Krampus? You shouldn't have!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell, he may cast Steal Charm (Shadow -All) that steals all Charms from the Wizard who cast the Blade.
:*He will occasionally cast this throughout the duel.
:*Pet Spells can trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Naughty, naughty! Krampus sees all!"'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} Spell, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -1)}} two times (which makes {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} ineffective).
**{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
**Balance {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Krampus is everywhere!""'' - After Krampus has summoned his Minions and has Traps, Krampus will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that transfers all negative hanging effects that are on him (e.g. [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]]) to a random ally.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"'Tis the season of Krampus!"'' - At the beginning of Round 5, and randomly thereafter, when he has no Minions, he will cast a Shadow Spell to summon 3 random minions.
