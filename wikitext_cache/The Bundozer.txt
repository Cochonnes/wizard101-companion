*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Oh, it does not like the surprise crowds!"'''- If a Wizard joins the battle after the first Round, The Bundozer will cast its Natural Attack on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"No, don't do that! It eats those!"'''- If a Wizard casts a Blade or Global, The Bundozer will cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm (Sun)}} to remove one Charm from the receiver.

**''"That will not work!"''- Otherwise, at the end of the Round, The Bundozer will cast its Natural Attack on the Wizard with the least Health.

'''Natural Attack {{Icon|Damage}}'''
*Each time The Bundozer casts its Natural Attack, including as a regular cast, it will immediately follow up with a Sun version of Mass Supernova, dealing a percentage of every Wizard's Health:
**1st time: 33% max Health Damage on all Wizards.
**2nd time: 66% max Health Damage on all Wizards.
**3+ times: 99% max Health Damage on all Wizards.
