| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Your tardiness offends me!"'' - If a Wizard enters the duel late, Moth-Amon will interrupt the duel and polymorph the tardy Wizard into a {{Link|Polymorph|Polymorph Egg}}.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Swarm to me!"'' - At the start of the duel, Moth-Amon casts a Moon spell to summon the following Minions.
**Moth-Amon is not targetable while he has Minions. Single target hits are canceled on the first round, and AoE hits don't hit him.
***{{Link|Creature|Projection (Balance)}}
***{{Link|Creature|Projection (Life)}}
***{{Link|Creature|Projection (Storm)}}
*{{Icon|Friend}}{{Icon|Minion}} ''"You're the fool, sorcerer! Now I will finish you at last!"'' - At the start of the duel, [[Creature:Mandar the Barbarian (Mandoria)|Mandar the Barbarian]] is summoned as an ally to aid Wizards in the fight.
**If the Wizard in the first duel slot flees, this summon will disappear.
**If the Wizard in the first duel slot summons a Minion, it will replace Mandar the Barbarian.
**Wizards who cast after the initial caster can summon Minions without replacing Mandar the Barbarian.
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"No blade can slay me!"'' - If a Blade or Global is cast by an ally or foe, Moth-Amon will cast an AoE Moon Enfeeble on all Wizards.
**Pet 'May Cast' Blades will trigger this cheat.
**Aegis can prevent them from being removed once.
**Armor Piercing Charms will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"No, my guards!"'' - The round after his Minions have been defeated, he'll shout this out.
**Boss will become targetable the next Round after he says this.
