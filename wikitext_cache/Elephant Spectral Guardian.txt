| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Shield}} At the beginning of the battle, the Elephant Spectral Guardian will cast a '''{{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (-90%)}}''' on himself. He replaces this Tower Shield every time he is attacked, until all of the other Spectral Guardians are defeated.
**{{Link|Spell|Pierce}}, {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} and {{Link|Spell|Shatter}} can be used to safely remove the Tower Shield. (He will still replace it when attacked.)
'''Marked Wizard Interrupt Cycle:''' (Resets every 5 {{Icon|Round}})
*''"Only charms can save you now!"'' - At the beginning of the third round in the cycle, the Elephant Spectral Guardian will mark a random Wizard for the third and fourth round. 
:*If a Marked Wizard casts a Charm, the Elephant Spectral Guardian will remove the Marked shield and all positive and negative wards from the Marked Wizard.
::*Pet spells will not affect this cheat and do not remove the Marked shield.
::*The Marked shield can be removed by the Lion Spectral Guardian's {{Link|Spell|Earthquake}}.
:*At the beginning of the fifth round of the cycle, if a Wizard has a Marked shield active, the other Spectral Guardians will punish all Wizards with the following spells:
:::{{Link|Creature|Gorilla Spectral Guardian}} will cast '''{{Link|Spell|Smoke Screen (-50%)}}'''.
:::{{Link|Creature|Lion Spectral Guardian}} will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Orthrus}} dealing '''700''' Myth Damage.
:::{{Link|Creature|Zebra Spectral Guardian}} will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Blizzard}} dealing '''750-790''' Ice Damage.
:*''"Are you still marked?"'' - At the beginning of the fifth round, the Elephant Spectral Guardian will remove all positive and negative wards from the marked Wizard.
:*Defeating all other Spectral Guardians or the Elephant Spectral Guardian stops this cheat.
'''Scarecrow Interrupt Cycle:''' (Resets every 3 {{Icon|Round}})
*''"We can't fail!"'' - If all the other Spectral Guardians have been defeated, the Elephant Spectral Guardian will remove all wards from himself (including his {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}}).
:*''"Your pursuit ends here!"'' - After all the other Spectral Guardians have been defeated, he will cast '''{{Link|Spell|Ominous Scarecrow (Death)}}''' every three Rounds.
