'''Cheat Cycle'''

*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I won't let you destroy me!"'' - Every turn, Bartleby Quietus will cast a Spell that applies hanging effects corresponding to one of the six non-Balance Schools of Magic. Whenever there are enough of any hanging effect to trigger a Scion Gambit, he will cheat-cast the corresponding Scion Spell.
**This cheat can trigger multiple times in the same Round.

'''Hanging Effect Counters'''

{{Icon|Charm}} ''Positive Charms''

If the Wizard casts a Positive Charm, Bartleby Quietus will remove all Positive Charms from the Wizard and do one of the following:

*''"Death withers Storm."''
** Place two 30% Deathblades on himself
** Place two -20% Negative Wards on the Wizard
*''"Ice freezes Storm."''
** Place two 35% Ice Traps on the Wizard
** Place two -40% Tower Shields on himself

{{Icon|Curse}} ''Negative Charms''

If the Wizard casts a Negative Charm, Bartleby Quietus will remove all Negative Charms from himself and do one of the following:

*''"Myth conquers Death."''
** Place a Bomb Damage Over Time effect on the wizard that deals 291 Myth damage after 3 Rounds
** Place two 35% Myth Traps on the Wizard
*''"Ice survives Death."''
** Place two -20% Negative Charms on the Wizard
** Place two -40% Tower Shields on the Wizard

{{Icon|Jinx}} ''Negative Wards''

If the Wizard casts a Negative Ward, including Prisms, Bartleby Quietus will remove all Negative Wards from himself and do one of the following:

*''"Fire burns Myth."''
** Place a Damage Over Time effect on the Wizard that deals 312 Fire Damage over 3 Rounds
** Place two 70% Damage Over Time Traps on the Wizard
*''"Life surpasses Myth."''
** Place two 30% Lifeblades on the '''Wizard''' (possible bug)
** Place a Healing Over Time effect that heals 321 Health over 3 Rounds on the '''Wizard''' (possible bug)

{{Icon|Ward}} ''Positive Wards''

If the Wizard casts a Positive Ward, Bartleby Quietus will do one of the following:

*''"Fire melts Ice."''
** Clear all Shields from '''himself''' and place a Heal Over Time effect on himself that heals 432 Health over 3 Rounds
** Clear all Shields from the '''Wizard''' and place a Damage Over Time effect on the Wizard that deals 436 Fire Damage over 3 Rounds

{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''Damage Over Time''

If the Wizard casts a Damage Over Time Spell, Bartleby Quietus will remove all Damage Over Time effects from the himself and do one of the following:

*''"Storm douses Fire."''
** Place a {{Link|Spell|Weakness}} on the Wizard.
*''"Life survives Fire."''
** Place a Healing Over Time effect on the Wizard that heals 432 Health over 3 Rounds
** Place a Bomb Damage Over Time effect on the Wizard that deals 374 Life Damage after 3 Rounds

{{Icon|Aura}} ''Auras''

*''"Your future is my memory."'' - If the Wizard casts an Aura on themself, it will immediately be removed.
**Bartleby Quietus will also say this at the beginning of the first Round with no effect.

{{Icon|Global}} ''Global''

*''"Your future is my memory."'' - If the Wizard casts a Global spell, it will immediately be removed.
