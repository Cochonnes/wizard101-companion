| cheatnotes =
'''Takanobu the Masterless'''
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"No! I disarm you."'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade Spell, Takanobu the Masterless will cast Disarm on the Wizard who received the Blade.
**If a triple Blade is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}} or {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blade}}), he will only remove the last Blade (Fire or Life).
**If a multi-target Blade is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}), he will only remove the Blade from the Wizard that cast the Spell.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"My katana dances around your shield."'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Storm Shield}}, {{Link|Spell|Volcanic Shield}}, or {{Link|Spell|Glacial Shield}}, he will cheat-cast Storm Prism on the Wizard who received the Shield.
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"KIAI!"'' - If Takanobu has 5 Pips at the end of a Round, he will cheat-cast Supercharge on himself.
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"This wind is an omen!"'' - If Takanobu has 6 or more Pips at the beginning of the Round, he will cheat-cast Darkwind.

{{Icon|Damage}} '''Bladeless Ronin'''
*''"We must stay centered!"'' - If a Wizard attacks any enemy with a Balance Spell, the Bladeless Ronin in the first position (Dagger) will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} on the Wizard.
**If an All Enemies attack is used, the Bladeless Ronin will cast Unbalance for each enemy that was hit.
**Attacking with {{Link|Spell|Chimera}} results in the Bladeless Ronin casting three separate {{Link|Spell|Spirit Defuse}}s.
**Attacking with {{Link|Spell|Hydra}} results in the Bladeless Ronin casting three separate {{Link|Spell|Elemental Defuse}}s.
**Attacks that deal no direct damage, but apply aura effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Scald}} or {{Link|Spell|Heck Hound}} will not activate any cheats by the Bladeless Ronin.
*''"Guard against the elements!"'' - If a Wizard attacks any enemy with a Fire, Ice, or Storm Spell, the Bladeless Ronin in the second position (Key) will cheat-cast Elemental Defuse on the Wizard.
**If an All Enemies attack is used, the Bladeless Ronin will cast Elemental Defuse for each enemy that was hit.
**Triggering a Prism that converts damage to Life, Death, or Myth, will result in the Bladeless Ronin casting Spirit Defuse instead of Elemental Defuse.
*''"Spirits, guard us!"'' - If a Wizard attacks any enemy with a Life, Death, or Myth Spell, the Bladeless Ronin in the third position (Gem) will cheat-cast Spirit Defuse on the Wizard.
**If an All Enemies attack is used, the Bladeless Ronin will cast Spirit Defuse for each enemy that was hit.
**Attacks that hit multiple times, such as {{Link|Spell|Minotaur}}, will trigger the Bladeless Ronin to cast a separate Spirit Defuse for each hit.
**Triggering a Prism that converts Damage to Fire, Ice, or Storm, will result in the Bladeless Ronin casting Elemental Defuse instead of Spirit Defuse.
