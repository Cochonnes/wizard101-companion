| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Charm}} ''"I revel in my power!"'' - If a Wizard casts a helpful Charm, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Supercharge (+12%)}}''' on himself.

{{Icon|Curse}} ''"I will not bow down to you!"'' - If a Wizard casts a harmful Charm on Shane, he will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Sacrifice (Death)}}''' on himself.
:*Secondary Spell effects '''will''' trigger this cheat.
:*Harmful Charms that target all enemies '''will not''' trigger this cheat.
{{Icon|Feint}} ''"I can see through you"'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on Shane, he will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute (+30%)}}''' on himself.
:*{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} '''will not''' trigger this cheat.
{{Icon|Healing}} ''"More life for me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast an enhanced version of '''{{Link|Spell|Vampire}}''', dealing '''1,050''' Death Drain Damage on a random Wizard and heals the caster for half the Damage dealt.
:*Healing Wizards that are Beguiled on the round they are hit with Monster Mash (1,170) '''will not''' trigger this cheat.
{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Bah, you are nothing!"'' - Whenever Shane is Damaged, he will cheat-cast a version of '''{{Link|Spell|Healing Current}}''' on himself that restores '''100''', '''400''' or '''1,000''' Health.
:*{{Link|Spell|Dissipate}} can be used to Dispel this cheat.
:*Damage over Time effects '''will''' trigger this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Supernova}} '''will not''' trigger this cheat.
:*His {{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute}} '''will''' trigger this cheat.

'''Interrupt Cycles''' - This fight revolves around 2 independent cheat cycles: a "Storm Interrupt Cycle" and a "Death Interrupt Cycle."

:{{Icon|Storm}} '''Storm Interrupt Cycle''' (4 {{Icon|Round}}) - This cycle starts at the end of Round 1, and repeats every 4 Rounds.
::*''"Prepare for the thunder!"'' - At the end of the first Round in this cycle, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Stormblade}}''' on himself.
::*''"And the lightning!"'' - At the end of the second Round, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Stormspear}}''' on himself.
::*''"The storm grows!"'' - At the end of the third Round, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Galvanic Field}}'''.
::*''"And shatters you!"'' - At the end of the fourth Round, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast his '''Natural Attack''' on a random Wizard.

:{{Icon|Death}} '''Death Interrupt Cycle''' (4 {{Icon|Round}}) - This cycle starts during Round 1 of battle.
::*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Hiss!"'' - Whenever Shane von Shane is Damaged by a Wizard, the cycle resets to Round one on the following turn. Otherwise, nothing happens during the first Round of this cheat cycle.
::*''"We feast tonight!"'' - At the beginning of the second Round in this cycle, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast an enhanced version of '''Vampire''', dealing '''1,050''' Death Drain Damage on a random Wizard and heals the caster for half the Damage dealt.
::*''"The monster begins to rise!"'' - At the beginning of the third Round, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast a '''{{Link|Spell|Dr. Von's Monster (900)}}''' on a random Wizard.
::*''"It's a graveyard smash!"'' - At the beginning of the fourth Round, Shane von Shane will cheat-cast '''{{Link|Spell|Monster Mash (1,170)}}''' on a random Wizard, [[Spell:Beguile|beguiling]] them.
::**A soloing Beguiled Wizard '''will not''' occur since Shane Von Shane cannot target any Wizards within the field until Beguile ends on the next round.
::**This cheat will continue to occur every Round until Shane von Shane is hit again.
::**{{Link|Spell|Supernova}} and {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} '''will not''' reset this cycle.
::**His {{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute}} '''will''' reset this cycle.
