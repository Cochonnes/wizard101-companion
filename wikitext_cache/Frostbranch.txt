| cheatnotes =
'''Interrupt Cycle''' (Resets every 5 {{Icon|Round}})
:*''"You have little time to hurt me!"'' - At the start of the duel (and every five Rounds thereafter), Frostbranch will cheat-cast two Smoke Screens ('''-50%''') at each Wizard.
::*The first Smoke Screen cheat-cast has a casting animation; however, the second one does not.
::*{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to Dispel the Smoke Screen.
:*''"Who doesn't have a Mark of Courage?"'' - At the start of every fifth Round, Frostbranch will cheat-cast Efreet dealing '''895''' Fire Damage applying a '''-90%''' Weakness at any Wizard who does not have a ''"Mark of Courage"''. On the same Round, Frostbranch will use its turn to cast '''Earthquake (310)''' without using Pips, regardless of if any Wizard in the battle has a ''"Mark of Courage"''.
::*{{Link|Spell|Vaporize}} can be used to Dispel the Earthquake.
::*{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to Dispel Efreet, even though it has no casting animation.
:::*One Quench is required for every Efreet Dispelled.
:*''"A true warrior!"'' - If a Wizard attacks Frostbranch with a single-target Spell, he will remove all Smoke Screens from the Wizard, and place a ''"Mark of Courage"'' on the Wizard in the form of a Stun Shield.
::*Pet Maycast Talents will not activate this cheat.
::*Select Target spells such as {{Link|Spell|Frost Giant (Tier 2b)}} that target more than one enemy '''will''' activate this cheat.
::*If Frostbranch has reached the ''"Who doesn't have a Mark of Courage?"'' phase of his cycle most recently (he has not recast his Smoke Screens yet), direct attacks will not yield any Stun Shields.
::*Since the Marks of Courage are Stun Shields, they are not removed by Earthquake nor stolen by Thieving Dragon. This means that wizards need only attack directly once to avoid Frostbranch's Efreet cheat.
