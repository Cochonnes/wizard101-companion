| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness is unacceptable"'' - If a Wizard is late after the second Round, Boss will cast a Moon version of Ship of Fools dealing '''100,000''' Moon Damage. If a Damage cap is active, the Damage will be set to the maximum.

{{Icon|Global}} '''Equalize Cycle'''
*''"Your doom approaches"'' - At the start of Round 1, the Boss will cast a Death version {{Link|Spell|Equalize}}, which sets the Damage cap to 250.
*''"Closer"'' - At the start of Round 3, the Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 500.
*''"and closer..."'' - At the start of Round 5, the Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 1,000.
*''"It's almost here"'' - At the start of Round 7, the Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 2,000.
*''"Your doom is NOW!"'' - At the start of Round 10, the Boss will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (Moon)}}.

*''"You wish to hasten your doom?"'' - If a Wizard changes the Global, Lord Nightshade will immediately replace the Equalize, resetting the Damage cap to 250. The cycle will continue normally, and future Equalize casts will set the Damage cap to the proper limit for that round.

{{Icon|Round}} '''End of Round Cycle'''
*''"Kneel! For I am the harbinger of your demise!"'' - At the end of every Round, Lord Nightshade will interrupt with a Moon version of '''Deer Knight''' dealing '''100,000''' Moon Damage. If a Damage cap is active, the Damage will be set to the maximum.
**If a Wizard changes the global, the Deer Knight will deal 250 damage due to the Equalize being temporarily reset.
