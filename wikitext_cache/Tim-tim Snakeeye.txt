'''Healing Interrupt Cycle:'''

This cycle can only activate when a Wizard casts a healing spell.
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"Feel better? Now I'll show you my stuff!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell, he will use '''{{Link|Spell|Pierce}}''' to remove one Tower Shield.
**Pet Spells, Pet Talent Cards (e.g. {{Link|PetAbility|Heart o'Pixie}}), and subsequent Rounds of Heal over Time Spells (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Sprite}}) will not affect this cheat.
**Although Pierce is not cast, it can be Dispelled.
*{{Icon|Damage}} On the first round after healing (including healing within the round), he will not cast his Tower Shield when hit by a damage spell.
*{{Icon|Shield}} At the beginning of the second round after the Wizard casts a healing spell, he will recast his Tower Shield.
