{{Icon|Late}} '''Tardiness Cheats:'''
*'''The Rat'''
*''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins a Round late, The Rat will cast a '''+60%''' Fire, Ice, and Universal [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]], and a Shadow-version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} transfering all negative hanging effects on The Rat to the late Wizard.
*'''The Scorpion'''
*''"Oh Yes! You will fall too!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, The Scorpion will cast {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju (Shadow)}} on the Wizard.

{{Icon|Round}} '''Round 1 Cheats:'''
*Starting Round 1, The Scorpion casts a version of {{Link|Spell|Donate Shadow}} that gives himself 2 Shadow pips.
*''"Thank you, dear brother"'' - The Rat then will cast a Shadow spell that steals one Shadow pip from The Scorpion.
*''"I'll take that, weakling"'' - The Scorpion will retaliate back casting {{Link|Spell|Steal Pips}} at The Rat, stealing 2 Pips.

'''The Rat:'''
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Fool me once?"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on The Rat, he will shout this as a warning. 
**''"Can't get fooled again!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} has already been placed on The Rat, any subsequent attempts to Feint him will trigger a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that removes all negative hanging effects on him, and places them onto the Wizard who cast the {{Link|Spell|Feint}}.
:*{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
:* The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
:*Pet Spells do not trigger this cheat.

'''The Scorpion:'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"I invoke the power of Shadow!"'' - At the end of every three rounds, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Poison (Shadow)}} to all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Now... your doom!"'' - At the end of every Round, he will cheat-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} to all Wizards, as long as one Wizard has a DoT.
:*The HoT portion of healing spells, such as from {{Link|PetAbility|Spritely}}, can prevent a DoT from detonating when the HoT is positioned before the DoT. The HoT is removed by the Detonate.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"The Rat warned me of that!"'' - If a Wizard places a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on The Scorpion, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)}} to remove all unprotected traps from himself.
:*{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"I planned this special for you!"'' - If a Wizard uses a healing spell, he will cast his [[:Spell:Natural Attack (The Scorpion)|Natural Attack]] at the Wizard.
:*Pet heals will not trigger this cheat with the exception of {{Link|PetAbility|Fairy Friend}} and {{Link|PetAbility|Happy Accident}}.
:*The HoT portion of healing spells, such as from {{Link|PetAbility|Spritely}}, will not trigger this cheat.

{{Icon|Random}}'''Retaliation Cheats:'''
*If either The Rat or The Scorpion casts a spell that upsets the other, they'll retaliate and cast the following cheats on each other.

*'''The Rat'''
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Fool! Stay out of my way!"'' - If The Scorpion casts {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble (2 Pip)}} or {{Link|Spell|Glowbug Squall (Shadow, 1,120)}}, The Rat will cast {{Link|Spell|Scorching Scimitars}} at The Scorpion.
**{{Icon|Damage}}{{Icon|Aura}} ''"You DOLT! I have had enough of you!"'' - Sometimes after The Scorpion's {{Link|Spell|Iron Sultan (Shadow)}} cheat aimed at The Rat, he will retaliate by casting {{Link|Spell|Grand Blue Phoenix}} at The Scorpion, and casting {{Link|Spell|Impede}} at The Scorpion.

*'''The Scorpion'''
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"No! That spell was mine!"'' - If The Rat casts {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} or {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}}, The Scorpion will cast {{Link|Spell|Iron Sultan (Shadow)}} at The Rat.
**{{Icon|Aura}}{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Enough! I'm telling father!"'' - Sometimes after The Rat's {{Link|Spell|Scorching Scimitars}} cheat, The Scorpion will cast {{Link|Spell|Impede}} and {{Link|Spell|Supernova (Shadow)}} at The Rat.
