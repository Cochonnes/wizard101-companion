| cheatnotes =
'''Interrupt Cycle''' (Resets every 6{{Icon|Round}}
This battle revolves around a cheat cycle that spans the length of five Rounds. This cheat repeats itself every five Rounds.
*{{Icon|Round}} At the beginning of the first Round in this cycle (starting in Round 6), Ponce will remove marks he placed during the second Round in this cycle (see below). This is considered a normal Round.
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"First, Let's cloud the mind!"'' / ''"Mind Weakened"'' - At the beginning of the second Round in this cycle, Ponce will place a "mark" on a random Wizard, in the form of a Stun Shield.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be placed on Ponce in order to Dispel this cheat.
**The Stun Shield marker does not block Stuns.
*{{Icon|Round}} The third Round in this cycle is considered a normal Round. However, it is the last Round for a marked Wizard to damage Ponce, in order to get rid of the mark. Failure to do so results in the worst part of his cheat to take place in the fourth and fifth Rounds of this cycle.
**{{Icon|Round}} ''"Bah! You are too strong willed!"'' - If the marked Wizard attacks him, he will remove the Stun Shield mark and any other [[:Category:Ward Spells|Wards]] from the Wizard. Stun Shields aren't removed from this spell.
**If Ponce has a Damage over Time effect already on him, this does not remove the mark, regardless if the marked Wizard cast it on him. 
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Are you sure you won't join us?"'' - In Round 4, if a marked Wizard did not attack Ponce by the end of the third Round in this cycle, he will {{Link|Spell|Beguile}} the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"If you aren't with us, you're against us!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, he will interrupt-cast a version of Sacrifice that deals '''650''' Death Steal Damage to the Wizard, and heals Ponce for '''1,820''' Health, (as though the Wizard used Sacrifice on him).
**Universal and Death [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blades]] that are active on the Wizard, will increase the amount of Damage dealt by the Sacrifice.
**Universal and Death Shields that are active on the Wizard will decrease the amount of Damage dealt by the Sacrifice.
**Infection will not lessen this Damage, however, it will decrease the Healing effect by -50%. ''Needs confirmation.''
