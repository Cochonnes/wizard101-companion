Copy Qhat (Star) is considered a Boss.  All cheats and drops are recorded on this page.
*The point of these cheats lies in the fact that Copy Qhat (Moon) sabotages its own side of the battle.
*The Copy Qhat (Moon) summoned on the Wizard's team is considered a Minion and is affected by Minion-specific Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Draw Health}}, which provide an easy way of getting rid of it.

| cheatnotes =
'''Copy Qhat''' {{Icon|Star}}
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"More of you? That's '''my''' trick!"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle after the second Round, Copy Qhat (Star) will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn (Star)}} on them.
*Responses towards {{Link|Creature|Copy Qhat (Moon)}}.
**{{Icon|Blade}} ''"No, Don't..."'' - Ally Copy Qhat (Moon), on the enemy side, will cast Bladestorm (+25%) on the Wizard's team.
**''"You fool!"'' - ??? ''(needs clarification)''
**{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Stop that!"'' - Copy Qhat (Star) will say this after Copy Qhat (Moon) casts {{Link|Spell|Major Snowburst}}.

'''Copy Qhat''' {{Icon|Moon}}
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"I am ENDLESS!"'' - Copy Qhat (Moon) will summon a Copy Qhat (Moon) to any empty space on the enemy side.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"We gonna get you! Pow!"'' - Summons a Copy Qhat (Moon) on the Wizard's team in the first opening slot.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"There's more where she came from!"'' - If there isn't currently a Copy Qhat minion on the Wizard's team, a new one will be summoned if a slot is available.
**Copy Qhat (Moon) will replace a Minion that was summoned by the Wizard, but won't if all duel slots are occupied.
**Defeating the enemy Copy Qhat (Moon) will stop all these cheats.
