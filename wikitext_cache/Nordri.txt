*{{Icon|Round}} At the start of Round 1, Nordri will use his turn to cast a '''-65%''' {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Round}} At the start of Round 2, Nodri he will cast a {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (-90%)}} on himself.
**This Shield will be replaced every time Nordri is hit.
:*Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Pierce|}}, {{Link|Spell|Shatter|}}, and {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward|}} can be used to remove the Tower Shield, but will still trigger the "Tag" cheat.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Tag! You are It!"'' - At the start of the fight, and every three Rounds thereafter, one of the Bosses will cast a [[Spell:Stun Block|Stun Shield]] labeled ''"Tag! I'm It!"'' on a random brother, "tagging" them.
:*On repeats of this cheat, the previously "tagged" Boss will have all Wards on him removed, including the ''"Tag! I'm It!"'' Stun Shield.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Wrong brother! I'm it!"'' - Casting Spells at any Boss who is not "tagged" will trigger an interrupt-attack from the "tagged" boss. The attack varies depending on which boss is "tagged".
:::Nordri casts {{Link|Spell|Wraith}}
:::{{Link|Creature|Austri}} casts {{Link|Spell|Colossus}} dealing 460-540 Ice Damage.
:::{{Link|Creature|Sudri}} casts {{Link|Spell|Hydra}} dealing '''190''' Fire, Ice, and Storm Damage.
:::{{Link|Creature|Vestri}} casts {{Link|Spell|Rebirth}} for 750 Healing, without the Absorb.
:*May Cast Spells will trigger this cheat.
:*Multi-target, all-enemies attack Spells will not trigger this cheat.
:*This cheat will stop when Nordri is defeated.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Now where did my brothers go?!"'' - When all of Nordri's brothers are defeated, he will remove all Wards from himself at the beginning of the following Round.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"You hurt my brothers!"'' - Four rounds after all of his brothers have been defeated, and every three Rounds thereafter, Nordri will cheat-cast a version of Meteor Strike that deals '''500''' Fire Damage.
