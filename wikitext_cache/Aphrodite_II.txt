|width="70%"|'''Description:'''

*This over-sized Aberrant Plant, found in [[Location:Castle Darkmoor|Darkmoor's]] {{Link|Location|Graveyard}}, wants only one thing from Wizards: food.  Whether that food is a Pet Snack or the Wizards themselves, it probably doesn't care.
*Wizards can access this unique Boss by using a {{Link|House|Stone Skeleton Key}} in the Graveyard, and may duel against four of Aphrodite's Tendrils, varying in school from Life, Death, and Myth.
*She yields powerful loot from a Golden Chest that appears if her Tendrils are successfully vanquished<br/>All drops from the Golden Chest are listed on the page for the Death Tendril.

*Defeating this Creature grants the [[Basic:Badges#Skeleton Key Badges|Flower Power]] Badge.
