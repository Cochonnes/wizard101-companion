*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the Second Round, Shadowbones will cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} at the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Shadow-shadow! I love shadow!"'' - He will randomly cast Dark Convert once in the fight, which converts three Pips into one Shadow Pip for himself.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Why don't we trade!"'' - If a Wizard places a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on Shadowbones, he will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} to exchange it with the Wizard.
:*{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} does not trigger this cheat.
:*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to avoid this cheat.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Let's dampen the heals!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}.
:*Pet Heals will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Oh, how cute!"'' - If any type of Fairy Spell is used (by friend or foe), he will cast {{Link|Spell|Magnify}} on himself.
