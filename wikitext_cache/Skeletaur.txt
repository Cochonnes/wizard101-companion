| cheatnotes =
*''"Latecomers shall be DESTROYED!"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, Skeletaur will cast his Natural Attack on the Wizard. This will also trigger the "Feel the might of Skeletaur" cheat, which casts {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} on the Wizard.
*''"Skeletaur is the last to fall!"'' - If Skeletaur is defeated before or during the same Round as his Minions, he'll cast a Moon version of Blessing, restoring 100% Health.
*''"Such an obvious feint!"'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on Skeletaur, he will convert half the percentage value of the Trap. (Example: +70% becomes +35% Trap)
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can not be used to avoid this cheat.
**The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} will not trigger this cheat.
*''"Obey the will of Skeletaur!"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, he'll cast a 2 Round Confuse on the Wizard.
*''"Feel the might of Skeletaur!"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, he'll cast {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} on the Wizard which Stuns for 2 Rounds.
*''"Skeletaur is your king now!"'' - At the beginning of every 4th and 5th Round, Skeletaur will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Beguile}} on a random Wizard.
*If Skeletaur has one Minion and it's defeated, Skeletaur will immediately cast a Shadow Spell to spawn in an {{Link|Creature|Armor Totem}}, {{Link|Creature|Hex Totem}} or {{Link|Creature|Taunting Totem}}.
**If Skeletaur has more than one Minion defeated having no Minions, he will cast the summoning Spell multiple times, but the Minion doesn't spawn from this.
