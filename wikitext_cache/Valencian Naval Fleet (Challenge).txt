'''Dog Tracy''' - ('''4''' {{Icon|Charm}})
*''"We're fleas biting at a rhino. Need more punch..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Power rerouted to weapons!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to all enemies and removes all helpful Charms from all Wizards. Each Wizard is given a '''Storm Mark'''.

'''Solomon Crane''' - ('''4''' {{Icon|Curse}})
*''"The machines have identified weak points..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Target these areas for increased effect!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage and removes all harmful Charms from all enemies. Each Wizard is given a '''Death Mark'''.

'''Duck Savage''' - ('''4''' {{Icon|Ward}})
*''"Need some help? Your shields need reinforcement..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Let's polarize the hull with... bronze!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to all enemies and removes all helpful Wards from all Wizards. Each Wizard is given an '''Ice Mark'''.

'''Mandar the Barbarian''' - ('''4''' {{Icon|Jinx}})
*''"Bah, only fools fall into traps..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"By the power of Mandar!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to and removes all harmful Wards from all enemies. Each Wizard is given a '''Myth Mark'''.

'''Buck Gordon''' - ('''2''' {{Icon|Damage over Time}})
*''"Sorry I'm late. You'll see why..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Took a detour for some sabotage."'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to and removes all Damage over Time effects from all enemies. Each Wizard is given a '''Fire Mark'''.

'''The Bantam''' - ('''2''' {{Icon|Heal over Time}})
*''"Damage reports from all decks..."'' - Charge Up
*''"Oh, what's this button do?"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to all enemies to and removes all Heal over Time effects from all Wizards.
**Heal over Time effects do NOT have to be on the same Wizard for the condition to be met. Each Wizard is given a '''Life Mark'''.

'''Stallion Quartermane''' - ('''2''' {{Icon|Charm}} & '''2''' {{Icon|Curse}})
*''"Ha-ha! That's how you adventure..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Targeting systems engaged!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to all enemies and removes all helpful Charms from all Wizards and all harmful Charms from all enemies. Each Wizard is given a '''Balance Mark 1'''.
