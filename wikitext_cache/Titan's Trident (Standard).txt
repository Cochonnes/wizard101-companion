Titan's Trident is the Storm Titan's weapon and the penultimate Boss of Empyrea.  In order to defeat it, Wizards must deal 7,750 Damage to it three times.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Global}} On the first Round, Titan's Trident will cast an Equalize Global which sets the Damage cap to '''7,999''', and will recast it when replaced.
**{{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} can dispel this cheat.
**If a Wizard uses a {{Link|Spell|Nightbringer}} Enchanted Attack and changes the Global the next Round, the Global will not be changed back until after the next hit.

{{Icon|Health}}{{Icon|Damage}} '''Cheats:'''
*'''{{Link|NPC|Mellori}}''' - ''"Aww, you gotta be kidding me! The Paradox Chain just keeps charging him back up! Look out!"'' - After taking more than '''7,750''' Damage, the Storm Titan will emerge at the end of the Round and completely restore the Trident's Health by grasping the Paradox Chain.
*'''Mellori''' - ''"The Paradox Chain just keeps recharging him. This is hopeless!"''
*'''{{Link|NPC|Bat}}''' - ''"No it's not! I know what we have to do now! Wizard, just keep attacking!"'' - After taking another '''7,750''' Damage, the Storm Titan will again completely restore the Trident's Health by grasping the Paradox Chain.
*After the Trident takes '''7,750''' Damage for a third time, a cutscene will occur where Mellori breaks the Paradox Chain, destroying the Trident, and defeating the Boss.
