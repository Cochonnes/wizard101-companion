| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"I cannot be stopped"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Malistaire will cheat-cast a Shadow {{Link|Spell|Mega Taunt}} on the first Wizard.
**This cheat restores Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) to 100 health and spawns as Haunted Malistaire after being defeated for the first time.
*{{Icon|Blade}} {{Icon|Trap}} ''"What was yours is mine"'' - If a Wizard casts a universal Damage [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}}) or universal positive Damage [[:Category:Ward Spells|Ward]], he will cast Steal Charm or Steal Ward on the Wizard that received the effect.
**Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
**If a multi-target Blade or Ward Spell, such as {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}} or {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}} is used, only the caster will be affected.
**Secondary Spell effects, such as the Ward from {{Link|Spell|Abominable Weaver}}, will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Curse}} {{Icon|Jinx}} ''"I cast off your spells!"''
**If a universal Trap (e.g. Feint) is placed on Malistaire, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} on himself.
**If a universal negative Charm (e.g. Black Mantle) is placed on Malistaire, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm}} on himself.
***Pet Spells, {{Link|ItemCard|Life Feint}}, and secondary Spells other than negative Damage Charms will trigger this cheat.
***All Enemy Charms and Wards, (e.g. {{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}}), and negative Health Charms (e.g Infection) will not trigger this cheat.
**If {{Link|Spell|Elemental Defuse}} or {{Link|Spell|Spirit Defuse}} is used, Malistaire the Undying will use a version of Cleanse Charm to remove all three Dispel Charms.
*{{Icon|Weakness}} ''"I grow stronger every moment"'' - If any {{Link|Spell|Weakness}} is placed on Malistaire, he will cast a version of Empower that deals 300 Death Damage on himself and gains 3 pips.
**Weakness-based pet spells, roshambo spells (even if no Weakness is placed on Malistaire), and secondary Spell effects (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Efreet}}) will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Can you face my stare?"'' - If a Wizard uses a single target Damage Spell against Malistaire, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Black Mantle (-55%, 0 Pip)}} on the Wizard after the attack.
**Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
**The Secondary Interrupt Cycle after attacking Malistaire before the last round can remove the Mantle from the Wizard.

'''Interrupt Cycles'''
Malistaire's first form revolves around two cheat cycles; a "Primary Interrupt Cycle" and a "Secondary Interrupt Cycle." Both cycles overlap each other throughout the battle.

:'''Primary Interrupt Cycle:'''
:The primary interrupt cycle repeats every 5 Rounds.
:*{{Icon|Moon}} ''"Fear the power of Dragonspyre"'' - At the end of the third Round in this cycle, Malistaire will polymorph into a Black Dragon and attack a random Wizard, dealing 535 Moon Damage and destroying any Aura effects.
:*{{Icon|Sun}} ''"A gift from Tartarus"'' - At the end of the fourth Round, he will cast a Sun version of Deadly Minotaur on a random Wizard.
:*{{Icon|Star}} ''"My curse upon you!"'' - At the end of the fifth Round, he will cast an [[:Category:Aura Spells|Aura]] that grants him '''+20%''', '''+15%''', and '''+10%''' outgoing Death Damage and three '''+15%''' Power Pip Chance for five Rounds.

:'''Secondary Interrupt Cycle:'''
:The secondary interrupt cycle activates at the beginning of the fifth Round, repeating itself every four Rounds.
:*''"Its power shakes your spells apart!"'' -  At the beginning of the fifth Round, Malistaire's Skeletal Dragon will remove all Charms and Wards from each Wizard.
:**Wizards using {{Link|Spell|Empowerment}} or {{Link|Spell|Adapt}} will gain one Pip or Power Pip, respectively.

===Haunted Malistaire (second form) {{Icon|Polymorph}}===
*''"I touch Shadow!"'' - Haunted Malistaire, a Shadow Boss, will spawn within the battle after Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) has been defeated or reaches below 1,000 health. The Boss interrupts a Shadow Spell at the beginning of the round that sets its max health to 48,000 and restores 99,999 Health, followed by a Secondary Interrupt Cycle.
**This cheat occurs once, even if Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) is not defeated.
***If Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) is not defeated and only Haunted Malistaire is defeated first, he will revive himself with 100 Health, retain his stats from Haunted Malistaire, and delay his Shadow version of Primary Interrupt Cycle by one round.
**Malistaire's Health after healing can be decreased to 25,050 if a Treasure Card {{Link|TreasureCard|Doom and Gloom}} is present when Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) is defeated. 
***A regular {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom}} will not reduce his health below 48,000.
***If Malistaire the Undying (Shadow) is not defeated, the health can be reduced further using negative Healing Charms, such as Mass Infection.
Haunted Malistaire retains all the above cheats except his Primary Interrupt Cycle, along with the one cheat below.
*''"Curse you beast! Do not defy me!"'' - Malistaire's Skeletal Dragon will randomly place a '''+100%''' universal Trap on Malistaire and his allies.

'''Interrupt Cycles'''
The "Primary Interrupt Cycle" is replaced with a Shadow version. The "Secondary Interrupt Cycle" stays the same.

:'''Primary Interrupt Cycle (Shadow version):'''
:This cycle repeats every 4 Rounds.
:*''"I call upon Shadow"'' - At the end of the first Round, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Shadowblade}} on himself.
:*''"I call upon Shadow"'' - At the end of the second Round, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Shadowspear}} on himself.
:*''"I call upon Shadow"'' - At the end of the third Round, he will cast an All Enemies version of {{Link|Spell|Shadow Trap}}.
:*''"I will show you the Shadow"'' - At the end of the fourth Round, he will cast Shadow Steal, which deals 1,600 Shadow Damage, and converts 100% of the Damage to Health.

:'''Secondary Interrupt Cycle:'''
:Same as with his first form (repeats every four Rounds).
:*''"Its power shakes your spells apart!"'' -  At the beginning of the fourth Round, Malistaire's Skeletal Dragon will remove all Charms and Wards from each Wizard.
:**Wizards using Empowerment or Adapt will gain one Pip or Power Pip, respectively.
