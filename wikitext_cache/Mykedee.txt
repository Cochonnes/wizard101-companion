{{Icon|Blade}} If a Wizard casts a Blade Spell on another target, Mykedee will perform one of the following actions:

:*''"This ain't no Charm School"'' - The second time, Mykedee will cast Virulent Plague.

:*''"Feeling better? I fix that!"'' - The second time, Mykedee will cast Infection on the Wizard.
:*''"Ha! Now everyone get ill!"'' - The third time, Mykedee will cast Mass Infection. After the third time, the cycle begins again.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Hold it -- now hit it!"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, Mykedee will follow up with a {{Link|Spell|Feint (+75%)}} on the target.
*{{Icon|Random}} ''"Time for one last Beatdown"'' - Randomly, if Beets is in the battle, Mykedee will use his turn to cast Guardian Spirit (20%) on Beets.
