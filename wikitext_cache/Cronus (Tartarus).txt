Cronus is the father of [[Creature:Zeus Sky Father (Mount Olympus)|Zeus Sky Father]], [[Creature:Poseidon Earth Shaker (Atlantea)|Poseidon Earth Shaker]], and [[Creature:Hades the Unseen (Tartarus)|Hades the Unseen]]. He is the secret hidden Boss in Tartarus, the Level 90+ final dungeon in [[Location:Garden of Hesperides|Aquila]], a challenge for the mightiest of Wizards.<br/>This Boss will always occupy the duel circle's final spot.<br/>This Boss is typically known for dropping the {{Link|Item|Blade of the Felled Titan}} and the Exalted Amulets.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} Every Round, Cronus will cast a Sun Spell that places a '''+40%''' Trap of the following Schools.
**Balance, Death, Fire, Ice, Myth, Life, Storm.
***Sometimes Cronus casts an invisible trap, probably due to being a Balance trap, that seems to act like a universal trap. This trap is not listed in effects.

*{{Icon|Round}} Every four Rounds, he will interrupt cast {{Link|Spell|Lord of Night}} on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Trap}} When a Wizard places a trap on him, he will place a '''Marked''' {{Link|Spell|Stun Block}} on himself.
**Cronus will only place up to one marked stun block on himself, regardless of how many traps are placed on him, until he is attacked with a non-DoT spell.
**When attacked by a single-targeting spell, a Cleanse Ward will be cast on him removing all Traps, and the '''Marked''' {{Link|Spell|Stun Block}}.
***{{Link|Spell|Vaporize}} can dispel this cheat.
***Spells that only place a DoT on Cronus will not remove the mark.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Attacks alone will never hurt me!"'' - If any Wizards attack without placing a trap on him, Cronus will interrupt cast his Natural Attack of the opposite School that a Wizard cast (Example: Storm damage spell = Myth [[Spell:Natural Attack (Cronus Myth)|Myth Natural Attack]] '''+40%''' to the next Myth damage ward spell).
**Casting a Balance damage spell will result in Cronus using {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Storm)}}.
**Multiple damage spells such as {{Link|Spell|Minotaur}} will trigger him casting his natural twice.
**All enemies damage spells will trigger this cheat.
**DoT spells will trigger this cheat only on initial hit.
**Any Astral spells trigger an unknown damage school.
**Shadow spells trigger an unknown damage school.
