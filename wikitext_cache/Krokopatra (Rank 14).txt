{{Icon|Storm}} '''Krokopatra'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"More fodder! Protect your Queen!"'' If a Minion is defeated, Krokopatra will summon another random School Minion which will become active after two Rounds.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Time to ride the Lightning!"'' - Various triggers can occur: If no Minions are present, if she has been defeated and healed, if some Minions are defeated and healed, Krokopatra will shout this phrase and use her turn to cast Insane Bolt. '''Needs confirmation on triggers'''.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Why don't we direct all that potential?"'' Krokopatra will randomly cast Beguile on the Wizard with the most Blades at the beginning of the Round.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Loose the winds of destruction!"'' - Krokopatra will interupt-cast Windstorm (+25%).
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"I'm crackling with rage!"'' - Krokopatra will interupt-cast a zero-Pip Supercharge for the amount of Pips she has. '''Needs confirmation on trigger'''.
*{{Icon|Global}} ''"Beware! There's a storm brewing!"'' Krokopatra will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Astraphobia}}.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Queen's Thrall (Death) (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"Anything for my Queen"'' If Krokopatra receives a [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charm]], this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute}} on her.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"No one threatens my queen!"'' - The Wizard with the most Blades may randomly get a {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju}} by this Minion at the beginning of the Round.

{{Icon|Life}} '''Queen's Thrall (Life) (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} '''We must protect ourselves to protect the Queen!"'' If a DoT Spell is cast, this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Mass Triage}}.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Our Queen needs her army!"'' - If Krokopatra or a Minion is attacked, this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Unicorn}}.

{{Icon|Storm}} '''Queen's Thrall (Storm) (Rank 14)'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Protect the Queen!"'' - If Krokopatra is attacked, this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}}.
*{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"Stand Strong for the Queen!"'' - If a Dispel is placed on Krokopatra or a Minion, this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm}}.
