*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Careful now. Mind the Hostages."'' - At the beginning of Round 1, Mr. Cane will cast a 6,000 "Restraints" Damage Absorb Virulence Aura for 99 Rounds on both Hostages.
**The Aura can sometimes prevent Criticals from doubling Damage, or prevent Traps from being activated.
**{{Icon|Afterlife}} If Mr. Cane is defeated while either of the Hostages are still in combat, he will use a Sun version of Blessing, restoring 100% max Health to all allies, and will recast the Auras.
***Removing the Aura will also trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Worthless hostages can't even stay captured!"'' - If a Hostage takes more than 0 Damage (more than 6,000 after the Absorb Aura) in one hit, Mr. Cane will force the Wizard to cast {{Link|TreasureCard|Exit Combat}} on the Hostage, removing them from combat.
**Drain Spells will go directly through the Aura, triggering this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"I'll show you corruption!"'' - At the beginning of every third Round, Mr. Cane will cast {{Link|Spell|Poison}}.
