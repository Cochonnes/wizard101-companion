| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Dispel}} At the start of the duel, the Pendragon will interrupt-cast an '''Entangle''' Treasure Card on all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} During Round 1, he will use his turn to cast '''Fire Dragon (650+20,000)''' which deals 100 Damage Over Time per Round lasting 200 Rounds. This can 'Critical' for double Damage.
:*{{Link|Spell|Triage}} and {{Link|Spell|Shift}} can be used to safely remove the DoT.
:*{{Link|Spell|Shift}} and {{Link|Spell|Detonate|}} can be used as a counter attack to this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to dispel the Fire Dragon if Wizard has the initial cast.
:**On the fifth Round, Pendragon will use his turn to recast this Spell if initially dispelled.
*{{Icon|Ice}} If a Wizard attacks the Pendragon, he will cast an Ice Wand attack that doesn't deal Ice Damage dealing 500 Damage on himself.
:*If a Wizard attacks the Pendragon with the Sword of Kings Wand hits, he will re-cast the Wand hit as a Natural Attack on himself, and sometimes take the 500 Damage from the Ice Wand attack that doesn't deal Ice Damage.
:*The Sword of Kings Wand attacks will always trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Steal}} If a Wizard casts a Rank 6 or higher single-target Spell (including X-Pip), he will interrupt-cast an Ominous Scarecrow dealing 550 Damage.
