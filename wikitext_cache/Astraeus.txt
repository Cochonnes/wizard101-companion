| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"The constellations form again!"'' - At the end of the first Round, Astraeus will summon three Ptolemos (Rank 6 Minion) Minions, which become active two Rounds later.
*{{Icon|Shield}} During the first Round, Astraeus will use his turn to cast a '''-90%''' Tower Shield on himself.
**He will interrupt-cast the Tower Shield (-90%) every time he is damaged for as long as his Minions are alive.
***Sometimes Damage over Time (DoT) Spells will not trigger this cheat.
***If all his Minions are defeated, he will no longer cast the Tower Shield (-90%), until he re-summons them two to three Rounds later.
****On occasion, he will not re-summon the Minions.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Pierce}} or {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} can be used to safely remove the Tower Shield (-90%).
