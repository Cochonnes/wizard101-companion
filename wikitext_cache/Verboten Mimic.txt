Verboten Mimic is a Stone Skeleton Key Boss located in the River District of Aggrobah.
*This Creature is named Mimic, but his speech calls him Verboten Mimic.

| speech =
:''"Ohhh! Somebody clearly didn't get the memo! Or perhaps you just can't read. This is the Vault of the Verboten. No one, and I mean no one allowed! First rule in Vault of the Verboten is don't talk about Vault of the Verboten. The second, slightly less well known - don't enter the Vault. You just broke the second rule, thereby forcing me to break the first! What did you expect in here? Treasure? Hah! I've got your treasure right here!"''

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"A surprise for you too!"'' - If a Wizard is late, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Pips (Moon)}} to steal 1 Pip from the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"A treasure of tricks!"'' & ''"I can change forms all day long!"'' & ''"Perhaps this form's too easy?"'' - Every three Rounds, the Verboten Mimic will Polymorph into one of the forms listed below.

:{{Icon|Myth}}  '''{{Link|Polymorph|Cyrus Drake}}'''
::*''"You can't trick a Mimic!"'' - He will remove all [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]] from himself the first time trapped.
::*''"Oh! I've got an idea!"'' - He will cast Mystic Colossus.

:{{Icon|Moon}}  '''{{Link|Polymorph|Prince Gobblestone}}'''
::'''Cheat Cycle:'''
::*During this Cycle, the Boss will pass since all these Spells are interrupt.
::*Round 1 - He will cast a triple Tower Shield on himself, Legion Shield, and Brace.
::*Round 2 - He will cheat-cast Wings of Fate.
::*Round 3 - He will cast {{Link|Spell|Blinding Freeze}}.

:{{Icon|Balance}}  '''{{Link|Polymorph|Luska Charmbeak}}'''
::*''"Only attack if you have been inked!"'' - At the beginning of each Round, he will "Ink" the Wizard in the first spot (Sun spot), which applies a {{Link|Spell|Smoke Mantle}}.
::*''"Still fighting? Have some more ink!"'' - After losing 25% of his health, he will begin to ink the Wizard in the second spot (eye).
::*''"If you can still see, don't look at me!"'' - If a non-inked Wizard uses a single target attack Spell on him, he will cast one of the following Spells.
::**{{Link|Spell|Poison}} - '''800''' + '''2,000''' Death Damage over 3 Rounds
::**{{Link|Spell|Power Link}} - '''400''' + '''1,500''' Fire Damage over 3 Rounds & '''200''' + '''800''' Heal over Time on itself.
::*He will remove traps which are used on him.
:::*Indemnity can be used to to avoid this cheat.
:::*If a multi-trap Spell is used, such as {{Link|Spell|Elemental Trap}}, he will only remove the last trap applied (Storm/Life Trap).

:{{Icon|Sun}}  '''{{Link|Polymorph|Mithraya}}'''
::*''"Protector!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, she will summon a [[Creature:Stellar Protector (Rank 17)|Stellar Protector]].
::*She will cast Smoke Screen (-50%) on her first turn and every round when a Wizard successfully casts a Spell.
:::*''"The cards align! Now's your chance!"'' When both [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blades]] and [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]] are present in the battle, the cheat will end until they are no longer present.

:{{Icon|Shadow}}  '''{{Link|Polymorph|Ghost Dog}}'''
::*''"Your body and soul are mine once more."'' - At the end of the first Round, he will summon a [[Creature:Hagakure Blade (Rank 17)|Hagakure Blade]] and a [[Creature:Seijun Killer (Rank 16)|Seijun Killer]].
::*''"Again face the poetry of war!"'' - On any given turn, he will cast a Death Damage over Time Spell that deals '''4,000''' Death Damage over 10 Rounds.
:::*This can be countered with Triage or Mass Triage.
::*''"The way of the Samoorai is found in death!"'' - Casts a {{Link|Spell|Detonate (Death)}} on the target. 
::*''"I retain my resolve!"'' - If a Wizard casts any Ward, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Shatter}}.
