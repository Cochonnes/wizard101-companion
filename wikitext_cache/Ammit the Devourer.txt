| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Why don't we redirect all that potential?"'' - At the end of every Round, Ammit the Devourer will cast a Spell that deals 300 Death Damage to herself or one of her Pit Vipers, then [[:Category:Healing Spells|Heal]] herself for 100% of the Damage dealt.
**Negative Healing [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]] do not affect this healing.
**If all of the Pit Vipers have been defeated, this cheat will not be triggered.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Ammit is the judge of souls now!"'' - At the beginning of every second Round, she will cast a 0-Pip Judgement on a random Wizard, which deals 1,000 Balance Damage.
*{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Health}} ''"The Condemned serve the mighty Ammit! / Behold the mighty ranks of the Army of the Condemned!"'' -When a Pit Viper has low Health, it may cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Brilliant Light}}, followed by Ammit cheat-casting Sap Health on the Minion, which deals 2,735 Balance Damage.
*{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Health}} ''"Ammit's rebellion shall never fail! / More fodder! Protect your Queen!"'' -If a Pit Viper has low Health, it may cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Donate Power}}, followed by Ammit cheat-casting {{Link|Spell|Take Power}} on the Minion, which deals 2,375 Fire Damage.
*{{Icon|Curse}} If a Wizard places a negative [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charm]] on Ammit, she will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} on herself.
** {{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} does not trigger this cheat.
**Target All Enemy Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Plague}} and {{Link|Spell|Virulent Plague}} do not trigger this cheat.''
**This cheat can also occur when a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Shield}} is cast on Ammit. It's unclear what exact conditions are required. {{Link|Spell|Death Prism}} and / or {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} seem to be related.
