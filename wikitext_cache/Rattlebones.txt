Rattlebones is an evil undead skeleton who is responsible for the Dark Fairy invasion on Unicorn Way. He works under the direction of {{Link|NPC|Malistaire Drake}}, with the goal of stirring up trouble in Wizard City and eventually bringing the city to its knees.
*Like the {{Link|Creature|Fairy Queen}} and {{Link|Creature|Lady Blackhope}}, he doesn't have resistance to or weakness against any of the schools of magic. However, his damage values are reduced by '''-30%''' from normal (and his Minion's by '''-50%'''), so that this solo battle doesn't present too much of a challenge for beginning Wizards.
Prior to the June 2025 Summer Update, Rattlebones was classified as a Rank 1 Elite, rather than a Boss.
