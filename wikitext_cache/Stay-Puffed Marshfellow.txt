| cheatnotes =
'''Stay-Puffed Marshfellow'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Fun for everyone!"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast S'more Machine (Tier 3a) on the Wizard.
**{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Hot Potato!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round and every four Rounds, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast a Shadow DoT (Damage-over-Time) Spell on a random Wizard. This DoT has about 100% extra pierce dealing '''2,000''' Damage after 4 Rounds. (Deals 5,265 Damage with the Damage Outboost).
**The Wizard who receives the DoT will be given a {{Link|Spell|Hot Potato: Enemy}} Spell and a {{Link|Spell|Hot Potato: Friend}} Spell. These Spells are 0-Pip versions of {{Link|Spell|Shift}} and allow the Wizard to shift a DoT to an enemy or an ally, depending on the Spell used.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"That not how you play!"'' - If a Wizard uses {{Link|Spell|Triage}}, {{Link|Spell|Mass Triage}}, or {{Link|Spell|Shift}} to remove the Hot Potato DoT, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast a Shadow Spell to instantly Expel both the caster of the Triage and the Wizard that had their Hot Potato DoT removed by the Triage.
**{{Link|Itemcard|Hamadryad}} will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Pass the potato!"'' - If a Wizard uses {{Link|Spell|Hot Potato: Enemy}} to pass the Hot Potato to Stay-Puffed Marshfellow, it will shift the Hot Potato to a random Marshfellow Minion in the battle.
**This cheat only seems to activate if the Wizard uses the {{Link|Spell|Hot Potato: Enemy}} the Round after the Hot Potato cheat.
**If there are no Marshfellow Minions present in battle, this cheat will not activate.
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"That not how you play!"'' - Stay-Puffed Marshfellow has three cheats that trigger when a Wizard uses Blades or Global Spells. They will cycle in the order they are listed below. After the third cheat, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will start from the first cheat again.
**The first time a Blade or Global Spell is cast, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn (Shadow)}} on the receiver of the Blade.
**The second time a Blade or Global Spell is cast, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju (Shadow)}} on the receiver of the Blade.
**The third time a Blade or Global Spell is cast, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm (Shadow)}}, which removes two Blades from the receiver.
***Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will only target the caster of {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}} with these cheats.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"That not how you play!"'' - When a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is cast, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast a Spell to remove all Traps from all his allies.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent a Trap’s removal.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} can trigger this cheat.
** The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.

*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Go BOOM!"'' - After a Marshfellow is defeated by the Shadow Damage overtime that deals 5,265 Damage, Stay-Puffed Marshfellow will cast a Shadow Spell to heal 100% max Health to all allies reviving the minion.

'''Marshfellow Minions'''
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Potato to you!"'' - At the end of a Round, if a Marshfellow Minion has the Hot Potato, the Marshfellow Minion will use a Fire School Spell to shift it onto a random Wizard.
