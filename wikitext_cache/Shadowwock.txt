| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"It's after brillig!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, Shadowwock will cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} at the Wizard, dealing 3,000 Balance damage.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Snicker-Snack"'' - Beginning on Round 2, he will interrupt to remove all [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]] from himself at the start of every second Round (Round 4, Round 6, etc.).
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to protect traps from this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"You make me frumious!"'' - At the beginning of every three Rounds, he will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Meteor Strike}} that randomly deals '''1650 - 3,300''' Shadow damage and places a '''+200%''' universal [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] on himself.
:*He will not always cast the trap if a Wizard is defeated with the Meteor Strike.
:*Using [[Basic:Spell Mutations#Weakness|Weakness-type spells]] can help reduce the amount of damage dealt by his Meteor Strike.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"How mimsy!"'' - If a Wizard places a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on Shadowwock, he will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} to swap the Traps with the Wizard.
:*{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to avoid this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} doest not trigger this cheat.
:*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Whenever a Fizzle occurs, Shadowwock will cast a Shadow version of Empower, similar to {{Link|Spell|Dark Surge}} to give himself 2 Power Pips.
*{{Icon|Curse}} After casting his Natural Attack, Shadowwock will cast a protected {{Link|Spell|Black Mantle (Shadow)}} on the Wizard.
