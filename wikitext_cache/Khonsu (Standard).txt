**{{Icon|Ice|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Snowball (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Splat Flurry}}
**{{Icon|Storm|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Glowbug Zapper (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Zap, Crackle, Pop!}}
**{{Icon|Myth|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Snipe (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Fur Baby}} (cast on Minion after meeting condition)
**{{Icon|Life|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Horned Frog (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Everyone Croaks}} (added to hand by {{Link|Spell|Nature Call}})
**{{Icon|Death|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Gummy Bunny (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Sweet Death}}
**{{Icon|Balance|25px}} {{Link|Polymorph|Wombat (Standard)}} - {{Link|Spell|Wombat Frenzy}}
**If a Wizard joins the battle after the second Round, Khonsu will Polymorph each Wizard into one of the above forms at random and remove all of their Pips, then heal himself to 100% Health.
**The Snipe Polymorph will not be assigned to any Wizard if there are 4 Wizards present.
*{{Icon|Friend}} ''"Hmm. Perhaps I should increase the adorability quotient"'' - If there is more than 1 Wizard in battle when attacked, Khonsu will Polymorph the Wizard who attacked him into another one of the above forms and remove their Pips.
**Sometimes this cheat may activate more than once from only a single attack, which will lower the amount of times needed to attack before the following cheat activates.
*{{Icon|Spell}} After the above cards have been used the same number of times as wizards in the duel (IE if there are 2 wizards in the duel, 2 spells need to be used), Khonsu will give all Wizards the following:
**{{GivesSpell|spells = 1|size = x150px|Break Free (Moon)}}
**It is not necessary to use the 'Break Free' Spell. Wizards may continue to obtain copies of their Spell and fight as their Polymorph, regardless of the number of Wizards present.
{{Icon|Polymorph}} '''Polymorph Cycle'''
*After Wizards have been given a 'Break Free' Card, Khonsu will begin to Polymorph into one of the following forms on that Round and the end of every 5th Round afterward.
**''"If I can't change you, I will upgrade myself"'' - Polymorphs into {{Link|Polymorph|Malistaire Drake}} for 3 Rounds.
**''"The Shadow shall fall!"'' - Polymorphs into {{Link|Polymorph|Morganthe}} for 3 Rounds.
**''"Perhaps something more chaotic..."'' - Polymorphs into {{Link|Polymorph|Grandfather Spider}} for 3 Rounds.
**''"You will learn to fear Nothing..."'' - Polymorphs into {{Link|Polymorph|Dasein}} for 3 Rounds.
