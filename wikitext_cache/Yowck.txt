**''"Second one has a cover charge!"'' - Further casts in the same round will trigger Yowck to use {{Link|Spell|Pierce (Star)}} on the recipient of the Ward and {{Link|Spell|Freeze}} on the caster.

**''"Group Concert? I'll Allow it This Time!"'' - Boss will allow the first cast.
**''"This party is out of control!"'' -  Further casts in the same round will trigger Yowck to use {{Link|Spell|Pierce (Star, All Enemies)}} and {{Link|Spell|Blinding Freeze}} on all Wizards.
***This cycle resets every round.
