| cheatnotes =
'''Sir Greggor'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"More Wizards? The fighting never ends"'' - If a Wizard is late, after Squire Greggor is defeated, Sir Greggor will cast a Shadow version of Mass Tower Shield, giving him three protected -55% Shields.
**(Confirmation Needed)
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I will weather these attacks, I can't feel them anymore"'' - At the start of the first Round, Sir Greggor casts a version of Shadow Sentinel that gives '''Intercept 75%''', '''+100''' Critical Block, and '''-30%''' Incoming Damage to his allies.
**If a Trap is cast on Squire Greggor, Sir Greggor will repeat this line and cast a Double Steal Trap Shadow spell to move it to himself.
***{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint}} also triggers this cheat.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"All things wither, all defenses break"'' - Sir Greggor casts a {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)}} to remove all negative wards from himself and a protected {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (Shadow)}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"The battlefield is littered with the careless"''- Sir Greggor casts three protected Mass Tower Shields (-55%). (Trigger clarification Needed)

'''Squire Greggor'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Your reinforcements won't save you!"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, Squire Greggor will cast a {{Link|Spell|Rebirth (Sun)}}.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Let us race forward to glory!"'' - At the start of the first Round, he casts Quicken.
**''"My zeal will not falter!"'' - Whenever his Aura is about to run out, he recasts Quicken.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"You won't leave us yet, old man"'' - Sometimes, if Sir Greggor is hit, he will cast Rebirth.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"You will pay for this!"'' - The Round after Sir Greggor is defeated, he will change his Aura to one that gives '''+100%''' {{Link|Spell|Amplify}} for 99 Rounds.
**If the Aura is removed, he will recast it.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"The future is ours for the taking"'' - Every fourth Round, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Chromatic Blast}} twice.
**If Sir Greggor has been defeated, he will only cast one Blade.
