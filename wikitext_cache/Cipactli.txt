| cheatnotes =
'''Interrupt Cycles'''
:This battle revolves around two alternating '''Interrupt Cycles:''' "Ward Dance" and "Charm Dance." Each cycle is three Rounds in length. The first cycle, Ward Dance, starts in Round 3 (Rounds 3,4,5), and the second cycle, Charm Dance, starts in Round 6 (Rounds 6, 7, 8), making the entire interrupt cycle 6 Rounds in length (starting over at the beginning of Round 9).

:{{Icon|Ward}} '''Ward Dance Cycle''' ''(starts in Round 3)''
:At the beginning of the first Round in this cycle, Cipactli will place a {{Link|Spell|Hex}} on a random Wizard and a [[Spell:Stun Block|Stun Shield]] "mark" on all Wizards. 

:*''"Can you follow the steps of my war dance?"'' - At the beginning of the first Round in each Dance cycle, Cipactli will place a Stun Shield "mark" on each Wizard. If the mark is not removed in the first or second Round of each Dance cycle, then he will cheat-cast a {{Link|Spell|Dr. Von's Monster}} dealing '''820''' Steal Damage, and places a -25% Healing Charm at the Wizard in the beginning of the third Round.
:*''"Those who cannot keep up must perish!"'' - At the beginning of the third Round in each Dance cycle, he will cheat-cast Dr. Von's Monster at any Wizard who did not get rid of the Stun Shield mark in the first or second Round.

:{{Icon|Charm}} '''Charm Dance Cycle''' ''(starts in Round 6)''
:At the beginning of the first Round in this cycle, Cipactli places a {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}} on himself and a Stun Shield "Mark" on all Wizards.

:'''General Rules:'''
::Wizards can remove the "mark" during each cycle by casting either [[:Category:Ward Spells|Wards]] (e.g. Shields, [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]], Prisms) or [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]] (e.g. [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blades]], Dispels, {{Link|Spell|Weakness}}, etc.) - this will also cause him to remove all Wards from the Wizard.
::*{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to Dispel him from placing his mark on Wizards.
::*Ward Shields may only be cast on the Wizard in the first position (Sun spot). Shields cast on other Wizards are removed.
:::*If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}}, the Shield will only be removed from the caster.
:::*The self-trap portion of {{Link|Spell|Feint}} will be immediately removed after casting.
::*[[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Sanctuary}} and {{Link|Spell|Wyldfire}}, will trigger him to remove the mark.
::*The {{Link|Spell|Earthquake}} cast by his Minion(s) will remove the mark.
::*[[:Category:Aura Spells|Aura]] Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Galvanic Field}} and {{Link|Spell|Sleet Storm}}, will not trigger him to remove the mark.
::*Pet Spells will not trigger him to remove the mark.
::*Gear/Equipment Item Cards will not trigger him to remove the mark.
