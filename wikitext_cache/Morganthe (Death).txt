{{Link|NPC|Morganthe}}'s power over Death and Shadow magic allows her to bend reality to her whim, materializing all five forms of herself before the Wizards' eyes.  This is the final battle in Khrysalis.  Death Morganthe is considered the main Boss for the purpose of drops.  All drops from this fight are recorded on this page.<br/>This Boss will always occupy the third circle duel slot.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} If a prism is placed on Death Morganthe (including Mass Prisms), she will cast a DoT on herself that deals 350 Damage over one Round, and use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} to remove the Prism. (DOT does not deal Death Damage).
**{{Icon|Global}} Storm Morganthe places this DoT on herself after a Global is used.
**{{Icon|Damage}} Ice Morganthe reacts to any enemy attacks.
*{{Icon|Damage}} At the beginning of every fourth Round, Ice Morganthe will cast a Shadow Spell that summons Shadow Morganthe (the giant spider in the background) to hit all Wizards for 600 Shadow Damage at the same time.
**If Ice Morganthe is defeated, Death Morganthe will take over casting the Shadow Morganthe cheat. If Death Morganthe is defeated, Storm Morganthe will cast it, and finally Balance Morganthe will cast the cheat if all other forms have been defeated.
*{{Icon|Damage}} Each Morganthe has a Natural Attack, and a special personal attack. The Natural Attack sends spiders to wrap the Wizard in a web. The special attack morphs many spiders into one giant shadow spider (does not do Shadow Damage though).  The following is a list of the special personalized attacks:
**{{Link|Spell|Balance Morganthe Attack}}
**{{Link|Spell|Death Morganthe Attack}}
**{{Link|Spell|Ice Morganthe Attack}}
**{{Link|Spell|Storm Morganthe Attack}}
*{{Icon|Shadow}} When each Morganthe has 2 Shadow Pips, they will use their turn to cast the following Shadow Spells, even if they have existing pacts:
**Balance Morganthe and Ice Morganthe will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Fiend}}.
**Death Morganthe will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Shepherd}}.
**Storm Morganthe will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Nova}}.
***Because of changes to Shadow Creatures and updates, attempting to cast their respective shadow spells (with the exception of Dark Nova) will cause each Morganthe (except Storm) to always pass due to a targeting bug.
