| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Get ready for the powerfulest spell EVER!"'' -At the start of the duel, Spirit of Ignorance (Tier 2) will gain a {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (-99%)}}.
**If a single target Damage Spell breaks the Shield, another Shield will be placed on him.
**All Enemies Damage Spells will not cause this cheat to trigger.
**He will not react if the '''-99%''' Shield is removed, if he has another Shield on.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} will dispel this cheat.
**''"What the what? Like, where'd it go?"'' - If a Wizard uses {{Link|Spell|Pierce}} or {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}}, he will say this line:
**''"I cannot be defeated! I know your schools!"'' - The Round after the cheat above triggers, he will place an Aegis protected '''-100%''' Shield of the Wizard's School, not including Balance.
**''"Not this time!"'' - If a Wizard tries to remove the matching School Shield, he will recast another one.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"You can't Charm your way out of this!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, he will use {{Link|Spell|Disarm (Sun)}} on the receiver.
**Single Blade spells from pets do not trigger this cheat.
**If a multi-blade spell is used, such as Elemental or Spirit Blade, only the first blade (Fire or Life) will be removed.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"I won't fall into your Traps!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap on him, he will use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -1)}} and remove one Trap from himself. 
**''"What? Why didn't that work?!"'' - He says this if a Wizard casts a multi-trap spell on him, such as Elemental Trap, or an {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} protected Trap. 
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Muhahaha! Two rounds 'till you go DOWN!"'' - Every 5 Rounds, he will place a Shadow Deferred Overtime dealing 100,000 (150,000 with his outgoing damage) Shadow Damage after three Rounds on the Wizard
