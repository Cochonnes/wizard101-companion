| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I invoke the power of Ice!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Doctor Jackall will interrupt-cast Balefrost.
*{{Icon|Damage}}''"I must defend myself!"'' - If Doctor Jackall is damaged by a Spell, then he will interrupt-cast a -35% version of {{Link|Spell|Legion Shield}}.
**If Doctor Jackall is damaged more than once by a single Spell, then he will use this cheat for every time that he is damaged.
**Ticks from Damage over Time effects will not trigger this cheat.
**This cheat will not trigger if Doctor Jackall is damaged during the Round that his [[:Category:Polymorphs|Polymorph]] ends.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"I'll take that!"'' - If Doctor Jackall uses his {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack}} on a Wizard, then he will interrupt to use {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on the Wizard that he attacked.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"See my true face!"'' - At the end of the fourth Round, and at the end of every fourth Round after, Doctor Jackall will interrupt-cast a Spell that Polymorphs him into {{Link|Polymorph|Mister Hound}} and applies a +35% Fire Damage Global.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"You dare strike me?"'' - If Mister Hound is damaged by a Spell, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Fireblade}} on himself, then use [[Spell:Natural Attack (Fire 120-235-350)|his Natural Attack]] on the Wizard that attacked him.
**If Mister Hound is damaged more than once by a single Spell, then he will use this cheat for every time that he is damaged.
**Ticks from Damage over Time effects will not trigger this cheat.
**If this cheat triggers while Mister Hound has no Pips, then his Natural Attack will act as though he has one Pip.
**Although his Natural Attack is not cast when used through this cheat, it can still be Dispelled.
**This cheat will trigger if Doctor Jackall is damaged during the Round that his Polymorph ends.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"I'll take that, thank you!"'' - If Mister Hound uses [[Spell:Natural Attack (Fire 120-235-350)|his Natural Attack]] on a Wizard, then he will interrupt to use {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the Wizard that he attacked.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"The weakness returns!"'' - At the beginning of the eighth Round, and at the end of every fourth Round after, Doctor Jackall will interrupt-cast Balefrost.
*{{Icon|Minion}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"My rage boils over!"'' - If all of the Lanyon Scratchers are defeated, then Doctor Jackall or Mister Hound will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Lava Giant}}.
**If there are no Lanyon Scratchers present at the beginning of the duel, then this cheat will trigger whenever the Wizard team casts their first Spell.
***May-Cast Spells will trigger this cheat.
**If Doctor Jackall or Mister Hound is [[Spell:Beguile|Beguiled]], then this cheat will trigger the first time that a Lanyon Scratcher casts a Spell.
***This trigger can only occur once during the duel.
