| cheatnotes = 
*''"Only Dispels can pierce my metal!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, the Metallossus will cheat-cast a version of Mighty Judgement that damages all enemies and applies the same two Dispels on all enemies.
*''"Oof, Smart Move!"'' - If Metallossus is hit, the Metallossus will steal all Dispels from that Wizard and place them onto himself.
*''"Trying to pierce through my defense?"'' - If a Wizard casts an Armor Piercing Charm, Metallossus will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble (Shadow)}} on the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} is ineffective in protecting against this cheat.
*''"Only Dispels can supress my magic!"'' - If a Wizard casts a harmful Charm that is not a Dispel, Metallossus will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm (Shadow)}}, removing all harmful Charms from himself.
*''"Argh- 6 dispels is too much to handle!"'' - At the end of a Round, if the Metallossus has 6 or more Dispels, then he will remove all Dispels on himself to apply a +300% Armor Piercing Ward on himself.
**''"Fragment!"'' - At the beginning of the next Round, the Metallossus will summon a Runic Fragment.
***An additional Runic Fragment is summoned when the Metallossus is defeated.
*''"My Fragment protects me from Dispels!"'' - If a Dispel is placed on then Metallossus while a Runic Fragment is in the duel, then the Metallossus will push all Dispels to the Runic Fragment.
