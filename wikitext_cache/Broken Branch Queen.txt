'''Broken Branch Queen''':
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"The Queen protects her King!"'' - Starting at the beginning of the first Round, and then every sixth Round, Broken Branch Queen casts a -100% Incoming Damage Aura on Broken Branch King that lasts for six Rounds.
**''"Not while I still stand!"'' - If a Spell is cast to remove the Aura, such as {{Link|Spell|Supernova}}, Broken Branch Queen will heal Broken Branch King to 100% Health, and recast the Aura. **The Broken Branch King can be defeated on the turn that the Aura expires, if the Bosses have the first turn in the battle.
