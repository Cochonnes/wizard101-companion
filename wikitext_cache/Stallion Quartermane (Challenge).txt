'''Advanced Combat Rules:'''
*Spell Decks cannot be changed while in this instance. (Make sure to set the spells in a deck before entering this instance).
*PvP-Only Spells are allowed in this fight.
*Wizards cannot re-join combat after fleeing mid-combat.

'''Duel Circle Notes:'''
*Each team takes a Round to cast their Spells.
**For example: Team 1 takes first Round, Team 2 takes second Round.
*Rounds in this battle are counted only when a team has their turn.
*Effects that last over a few Rounds will last twice as long.
*Gaining Pips or Power Pips are only granted when the First Team has their turn.
*All Enemy Spells only affect 1 of the 2 teams at a time. Thus all enemies Spells will become targeting Spells when choosing which team to use them on.
*All Ally Spells used by the bosses only affect 1 team.

{{Icon|Late}} '''Late Cheats''' - When a Wizard joins after the 2nd Round, the following cheats will occur:
*''"I find your etiquette lacking"'' - Solomon Crane casts {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju}} on the Wizard.
*''"More of you? No problem!"'' - Buck Gordon casts {{Link|Spell|Smoke Mantle}} on the Wizard.
*''"You're never too late"'' - The Shadoe casts Shadow Spell to give the late Wizard the following Spells:
**{{GivesSpell|cards=3|size=x150px|Card Limit (0 Pip)|Card Limit (7 Pip)|Card Limit (2 Shadow Pip)}}
*''"Late to the party, ain't ya?"'' - Dog Tracy casts a Moon version of {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}}, dealing '''1,500''' Moon Damage.
*''"Ha-ha! The more the merrier!"'' - Stallion Quartermane casts Mana Burn on the Wizard.
*''"More foes? HA! I am ready for you!"'' - Mandar casts a Myth Spell to Confuse the Wizard for 2 Rounds.
*''"Ha! I saw your ambush coming!"'' - Duck Savage casts {{Link|Spell|Stun}} on the Wizard.
*''"Tsk, tsk. You're late!"'' - The Bantam casts {{Link|Spell|Infection}} and Angel casts {{Link|ItemCard|Entangle}} on the Wizard.

{{Icon|Balance}} '''Stallion Quartermane'''
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Good show!"'' - Stallion will say this when casting his Natural Attack.
*{{Icon|Health}} ''"Ah-ah-ah, Never say die!"'' - If any Boss is low on health, there's a chance he'll cast a Life version of Blessing that restores '''10%''' of the target's max Health.
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"I claim these pips for the queen!"'' - Stallion Quartermane will use {{Link|Spell|Steal Pip}} whenever a Wizard has a bunch of Pips.
**Stallion can use a version of Steal Pip that steals 1 or 2 Power Pips.

{{Icon|Myth}} '''Mandar the Barbarian'''
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Flee! Flee from the savage sword of Mandar!"'' - At the end of Round 2, Mandar will cast {{Link|Spell|Frenzy (+40%)}}.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"By Grom, you'll pay for that!"'' - If Mandar's Aura is replaced by Stallion Quartermane's {{Link|Spell|Quicken}}, Mandar will replace it with {{Link|Spell|Frenzy (5 Pip)}} that applies a +40% outgoing Damage and Quicken Aura for 4 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Is that the best you can do, Dog?"'' - At the beginning of every 7th Round, Mandar will cast a version of Earthquake that deals '''145''' Damage and removes 1 positive Charm and Ward.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Grom, give me strength!"'' - Mandar will cast a version of Blessing that restores 5% of his max HP, ('''652''' Health).

{{Icon|Ice}} '''Duck Savage'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Feel my Fists of Bronze!"'' - Duck Savage will say this when using the 5-Pip version of her Natural Attack and will apply a '''+75%''' protected Damage Trap on the target.
*{{Icon|Protected Absorb}} ''"I am the duck of Bronze!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Duck Savage will interrupt-cast a protected '''5,000''' Damage Absorb version of Frozen Armor on herself.
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''No, I don't think so"'' - If a Wizard casts Steal Ward on her '''5,000''' Damage Absorb, she'll cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward (Star, -All)}} to steal all positive Wards from the caster.
*{{Icon|Heal over Time}} ''"Don't worry. I'm a doctor"'' - If a Heal over Time effect is cast by the opponents, such as Buck Gordon casting Power Link, Duck Savage will extend the Heal over Time effect by 1 Round.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"I can formulate an antidote!"'' - If a Damage over Time effect is cast she will interrupt-cast either Cooldown, or a single-target version of Cooldown, on the receivers.
**Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"My stamina has no limits!"'' - When Duck Savage's '''5,000''' Damage Absorb is gone, she'll interrupt cast a 99 Round '''-50%''' version of Fortify on herself.
**This Aura will be recast if it is removed.

{{Icon|Life}} '''The Bantam'''
*{{Icon|Pacify}} ''"I need a minute. Talk amongst yourselves."'' - At the beginning of the first Round, The Bantam will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Pacify}} that lasts 3 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"I'll be taking that, thanks!"'' - Whenever a Blade is cast on another Wizard or summoned Minion, The Bantam will interrupt-cast a Life Spell on the receiver, replacing it with a Balance Damage Blade equal to half the percent of the original Blade.
**For multi-Blade Spells, such as Elemental and Spirit Blade, only the first Blade is removed.
**For Blade Spells that target all allies, such as Bladestorm, only the caster's Blade is removed.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Get ready, Angel"'' - The Bantam will shout this line at the end of the Round in which he'll cast Mass Sacred Charge next Round.
*{{Icon|All Friends}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Angel to the rescue!"'' - The Bantam will cast Mass Sacred Charge (840).

{{Icon|Death}} '''Solomon Crane'''
*{{Icon|Harmful Aura}} ''"Curses be upon thee!"'' - At the beginning of Round 1 and every 11th Round following, Solomon Crane casts a Damage Aura on all Wizards, starting at -10% Damage and decreasing by 10% each time it is cast, up to a maximum of -50%.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Steel your resolve"'' - At the end of Round 6 and every 12th Round following, Solomon Crane casts a Resist Aura on his Team, starting at -10% Damage and decreasing by 10% each time it is cast, up to a maximum of -50%.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"May your wards crumble upon you"'' - If a Shield is cast, Solomon Crane will cast a Shadow Spell to convert one Ward to half of the original percent as a Tower Shield.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} is ineffective in preventing this cheat.
**For Shields Spells targeting all allies, such as Legion Shield, he will convert all Shields for half the percent.
**Absorb Wards trigger this cheat, but the Spell doesn't affect them.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"A crude tactic"'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap, he'll convert the Trap into half the percent as a universal Trap.
**Traps placed on all enemies will also be converted.
*{{Icon|Shield}}{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Your end is nigh!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Shield and has a Damage over Time, Solomon Crane will cast Detonate on them.

{{Icon|Fire}} '''Buck Gordon'''
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"All right!"'' - Whenever a Blade is cast on Buck Gordon, he will convert it to a Fire Blade with double the percent.
**This cheat sometimes activates when Stallion Quartermane casts Bladestorm, but it doesn't do anything as Buck Gordon didn't get the Blade. However, this cheat will activate the second Blade from Solomon's Dark Pact.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} '''Rain of Fire Cheat Cycle:'''
**''"You're going down!"''  - Warning call for the {{Link|Spell|Rain of Fire}} cheat.
**''"Oh, come on!"'' - If Buck Gordon takes Damage, he'll refrain from casting Rain of Fire.
**''"Here it comes..."'' - Buck Gordon will cheat-cast Rain of Fire.

{{Icon|Shadow}} '''The Shadoe'''
*{{Icon|Round}} '''Round 1 Cheats:''' - Beginning of Round 1 The Shadoe will cast the following cheats.
**{{Icon|Damage}} The Shadoe will cast a version of Dark Fiend from {{Link|ItemCard|The Shadoe}}.
**{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Mind Talisman... Activate?"'' - The Shadoe will cast a Shadow spell giving wizards {{Link|Spell|Card Limit (0 Pip)}} and {{Link|Spell|Card Limit (2 Shadow Pip)}}.
**''"I'll shoulder some of the burden"'' - The Shadoe will cast a version of Shadow Sentinel which intercepts 25% of Damage.
**{{Icon|Global}} ''"Synthesizer's on the blink!"'' - The Shadoe will cast a Balance Equalize Global which sets the Damage limit to '''9,999'''.
***''"That solves nothing"'' - If a Global is cast, The Shadoe will cast a Balance version of {{Link|Spell|Equalize}} which limits the Damage cap to '''9,999'''.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"I believe in you!"'' - Randomly The Shadoe will cast {{Link|Spell|Donate Shadow}} on a random ally of either team.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"The Shadoe says 'No.'"'' - Shadoe will cast a Shadow Spell to steal a Trap from an ally to herself.
**If Traps are placed on multiple enemies at once, she'll only move the Trap from the first recipient.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"I can shoulder no more!"'' - The Shadoe will occasionally cast the Damage attack from {{Link|Spell|Shadow Seraph}}.
**''"Hahahahahahahaha!"'' - After this cheat, she will cast a version of Dark Feind from {{Link|ItemCard|The Shadoe}}.

{{Icon|Storm}} '''Dog Tracy'''
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Hey, don't quit your day job"'' - Dog Tracy will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Healing Current (Shadow)}} that affects all allies.
*{{Icon|Pacify}} ''"You're now in witness protection"'' - Dog Tracy will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Mega Soothe}} on an ally that lasts 3 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"That's our prime suspect. Get 'em!"'' - Dog Tracy will say this when casting his Natural Attack. The Natural Attack will taunt himself after dealing Damage.
