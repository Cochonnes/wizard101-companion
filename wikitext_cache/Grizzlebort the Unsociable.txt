| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"All are weak against Grizzlebort!"'' – At the beginning of every Round, Grizzlebort interrupt-casts {{Link|Spell|Plague}}.
**''"Ha-ha! Puny human!"'' - Grizzlebort casts Plague after a Wizard casts a Spell, even if they fizzle. He will cast it twice if both he and Grizzletroll are hit by the Spell. He will also cast it on a Wizard every Round they have a Damage over Time effect on them.
***Pet casts will trigger this cheat.
***Shields, and Spells that create multiple Charms or Traps, like {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}} will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Healing}} If Grizzlebort is defeated while his Plague cheat is still active, he will revive with full Health.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Sticks and stones may break my bones, but something, something"'' – At the beginning of the fourth Round (after spells are chosen), or when he revives, Grizzlebort will give each Wizard the following Spell:
**{{GivesSpell|cards=1|size=x150px|Stone Colossus (Tier 2b)}}
*{{Icon|Myth}} ''"I feel woozy. Where did that mountain come from?"'' - When hit by Stone Colossus (Tier 2b), Grizzlebort will remove all harmful Charms from Wizards, and stop his Plague cheat.
**''"Wait... you aren't supposed to do that!"'' - If a Wizard casts another Myth Damage Spell that removes harmful Charms on Grizzlebort, such as {{Link|Spell|King Artorius (Myth) (Tier 2b)}}, he will remove all harmful Charms from Wizards, and stop his Plague cheat.
