*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Fool! You dare to sneak in late?"'' - If a wizard joins the battle late, Balaur (Ice-Storm) will cheat-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} that removes all of the wizard's pips.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Let the world turn to my will!"'' - At the start of combat, Balaur (Balance) designates himself as "active" and all other Balaurs as "inactive".
**All spells can only target the "active" Balaur, including AOE spells.
**"Inactive" Balaurs will always pass until they are able to use a 7 pip or stronger spell.
**Once the "active" Balaur is defeated, the next Balaur in sequence will become "active". The order of Balaurs is as follows:
***{{Icon|Balance}} {{Link|Creature|Balaur (Balance, Standard)}}
***{{Icon|Life}} {{Icon|Myth}} {{Link|Creature|Balaur (Life-Myth, Standard)}}
***{{Icon|Death}} {{Icon|Fire}} {{Link|Creature|Balaur (Death-Fire, Standard)}}

*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Even death cannot fell me!"'' ''"The wheel of fortune turns without you"'' - When the "active" Balaur dies, the next Balaur in sequence will become "active" and begin its cheats. All cheats from the previous defeated Balaur will be removed.
*{{Icon|Feint}} At the start of the round, if Balaur has a feint on it, it will remove the feint. {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} does not stop this cheat. Other traps are not affected.
*{{Icon|Global}} ''"I am the emperor of this realm. I decide its state."'' - If a wizard casts a Global spell, the "active" Balaur will replace it with its own Global.

{{Icon|Balance}} '''{{Link|Creature|Balaur (Balance, Standard)}}'''
*''"Let Spring rejuvenate our strength"'' - Casts {{Link|Spell|Balance of Power}} upon becoming "active".
*''"The star itself will replenish my pips"'' - At the end of the first round and every 4 rounds thereafter while it is "active", Balaur (Balance) gains 7 power pips.

{{Icon|Life}} {{Icon|Myth}} '''{{Link|Creature|Balaur (Life-Myth, Standard)}}'''
*''"Let Summer sear with the sun"'' - Casts {{Link|Spell|Circle of Thorns}} upon becoming "active".
*At the end of each round while it is "active", Balaur (Life-Myth) places a 20% shield on itself. This shield increases by 20% potency after each cast. Once it reaches 100%, this cheat stops. 

{{Icon|Death}} {{Icon|Fire}} '''{{Link|Creature|Balaur (Death-Fire, Standard)}}'''
*''"Let Autumn balance the scales of justice"'' - Removes all blades and shields from all wizards and casts {{Link|Spell|Deadzone}} upon becoming "active".

{{Icon|Ice}} {{Icon|Storm}} '''{{Link|Creature|Balaur (Ice-Storm, Standard)}}'''
*''"Let Winter's temperance wither you all"'' - Removes all pips from all wizards and casts {{Link|Spell|Balefrost}} upon becoming "active".
*''"My power is towering, now for the destruction"'' - At the end of each round while it is "active", Balaur (Ice-Storm) grants itself 2 power pips.
