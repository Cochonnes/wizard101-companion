| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Time Butterfly (Tier 2) will cast a Shadow version of Empower on itself to gain 1 Power Pip and hidden-casts a Spell that switches the first turn of the duel.
*{{Icon|Feint}} If a Feint is placed on Time Butterfly, he will cast Swap Ward to switch the Traps so that the higher percentage is on the Wizard.


{{Icon|Minion}} '''Minions:'''
*Time Butterfly (Tier 2) will summon Minions starting with {{Link|Creature|Young Morganthe (Tier 2)}} on the first turn. If Young Morganthe (Tier 2) is defeated before Time Butterfly (Tier 2), both Young Morganthe (Tier 2) and {{Link|Creature|Roberto (Tier 2)}} will be summoned on the following turn. If Young Morganthe (Tier 2) and Roberto (Tier 2) are defeated, Time Butterfly (Tier 2) will resummon both of them, and also {{Link|Creature|Broodmother (Tier 2)}} on the following turn.
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You're Late, or Are You Early?"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle late, Time Butterfly (Tier 2) will use Stun that will last two turns.

*'''Variation:'''
**On the first round, three Young  Morganthe (Tier 1) will be summoned instead of just one. In this case, the Stage 2 summon will be omitted and the round after the three minions are defeated, the Stage 3 summon will occur.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Young Morganthe:'''
*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"Time for a New Strategy"'' - Young Morganthe (Tier 2) will cast {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} on a Wizard who collects more than 7 Pips.
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"No More of That!"'' - If a Ward is used, she'll use Power Nova, followed by Earthquake dealing 400 Myth damage to all enemies, and 100 Fire Damage to self.

{{Icon|Fire}} '''Broodmother:'''
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Give me your health, drone!"'' - Broodmother (Tier 2) will cast {{Link|TreasureCard|Cheat}} to steal 15% max Health from Time Butterfly and restores 15% of its max Health
**Sometimes after this cheat, a {{Link|Spell|Dragonblade}} is cast on a random ally, and {{Link|Spell|Mass Tower Shield (Shadow)}} (-55%) to all allies. ''"Need to verify which Round this trigger happens."''
