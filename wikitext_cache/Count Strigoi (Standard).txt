| cheatnotes = 
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"They... ascended"'' - At the beginning of Round 1, Strigoi will summon 3 Ascended Guards.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"I told you child... I protect you from the dangers beyond"'' - At the beginning of the round, Count Strigoi will cast Confusion on on a random wizard and heal himself for 6600 HP.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"And now my hunger returns!"'' - When {{Link|Creature|Ascended Guard}} dies, Strigoi will cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} to revive them with 20% of their max HP at the cost of 2000 of his HP. If multiple Ascended Guards are defeated in 1 spell, he will cast Sacrifice multiple times. 
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"We turn our pain into the master's boon!"'' - When {{Link|Creature|Ascended Guard}} is hit with an attack, if they are not defeated, they will heal Count Strigoi for 5000 HP.
*{{Icon|Health}} ''"Eh, I'm feeling a little overstuffed"'' - Count Strigoi will say this when he is at full HP at the start of a turn, allowing the Wizards to defeat him.
*{{Icon|Health}} ''"The only thing I feel is hunger pangs!"'' - Count Strigoi will say this when he is not at full HP at the start of a turn. Defeating him in this state will result in him casting Scarecrow and reviving.
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Are you alone? Let me fix that!"'' - If Count Strigoi is not defeated from full HP in a single attack, he will cast Scarecrow and revive himself.
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"You don't drain us, we drain you!"'' - If either Count Strigoi or an Ascended Guard is hit with a Drain spell, the enemy that was hit will cheat-cast one of the following spells on himself:
** If he is not defeated by the attack, he will cast an {{Link|Spell|Aegis}}-protected +100% {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}} on himself.
** If he is defeated by the attack, he will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Minor Blessing}} on himself that heals for 100% of his max health.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Is that the sun?!"'' - If either Count Strigoi or an Ascended Guard is hit by a spell with a sun in it (such as {{Link|Spell|Sun Serpent}}, {{Link|Spell|Ra}}, or {{Link|Spell|Climaclysm}}) and are not defeated by it, they will cast a protected universal dispel on themself.
