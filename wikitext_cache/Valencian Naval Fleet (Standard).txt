'''{{Link|NPC|Doom Moon}}'''
*The Doom Moon will charge its laser for each Hanging Effect, and will shoot a laser beam on all enemies after a condition is met.
*This laser will use the Balanceblade from Marleybonian Naval Fleet's Justiceblade.
*The laser will be fired per condition met, and then all hanging effects used to meet the condition will be removed.
**Protections can not prevent removal of any hanging effects.

*'''Gambit Conditions:'''
**{{Icon|Charm}} - 4 Helpful Charms on Wizards -> Dog Tracy
**{{Icon|Curse}} - 4 Harmful Charms on Enemies -> Solomon Crane
**{{Icon|Ward}} - 4 Helpful Wards on Wizards -> Duck Savage
**{{Icon|Jinx}} - 4 Harmful Wards on Enemies -> Mandar the Barbarian
**{{Icon|Damage over Time}} - 2 Damage over Time effects on Enemies -> Buck Gordon
**{{Icon|Heal over Time}} - 2 Heal over Time effects on Wizards -> The Bantam
**{{Icon|Charm}}{{Icon|Curse}} - 2 Helpful Charms on Wizards and 2 Harmful Charms on Enemies  -> Stallion Quartermane
**{{Icon|Ward}}{{Icon|Jinx}} - 2 Helpful Wards on Wizards and 2 Harmful Wards on Enemies -> Stallion Quartermane
**{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} - 2 Uses of Shadow-Enhanced Spells by Wizards -> The Shadoe

Notes: 
*Charge Up cheats have no function. In order to shoot the Doom Moon laser, the Gambit Conditions must be met.
*If any Wizard has 4 or more {{Icon|Curse}} or {{Icon|Jinx}}, or 2 or more {{Icon|Damage over Time}} on them, the respective Gambit cheat will activate when another Wizard casts any Hanging Effect on that Wizard. (Needs Confirmation)

*''"Need some help? Your shields need reinforcement..."'' - Charge Up.

'''Mandar the Barbarian''' - ('''4''' {{Icon|Jinx}})
*''"Bah, only fools fall into traps..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"By the power of Mandar!"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to and removes all harmful Wards from all enemies.

'''Buck Gordon''' - ('''2''' {{Icon|Damage over Time}})
*''"Sorry I'm late. You'll see why..."'' - Charge Up.
*''"Took a detour for some sabotage."'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to and removes all Damage over Time effects from all enemies.

'''The Bantam''' - ('''2''' {{Icon|Heal over Time}})
*''"Damage reports from all decks..."'' - Charge Up
*''"Oh, what's this button do?"'' - Deals 6,000 Moon Damage to all enemies to and removes all Heal over Time effects from all Wizards.
**Heal over Time effects do NOT have to be on the same Wizard for the condition to be met.
