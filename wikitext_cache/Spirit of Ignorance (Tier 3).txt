| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I... Am... INVINCIBLE!!!"'' - At the start of the Round, a '''-300%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Brace}}, lasting 99 Rounds, will be placed on Spirit of Ignorance (Tier 3).
**''"YIKES! What was that?!"'' - Spirit of Ignorance will say this if his Aura is removed, but will not recast it.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"You can't Charm your way out of this!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, he will use {{Link|Spell|Disarm (Sun)}} on the receiver.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"I won't fall into your Traps!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap on Spirit of Ignorance, he will use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -1)}} and remove one Trap from himself. 
**''"What? Why didn't that work?!"'' - He says this if a Wizard casts a multi-Blade or multi-Trap Spell on him, such as Elemental Blade or Elemental Trap, or an {{Link|Spell|Aegis}} protected Blade or {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} protected Trap. 
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I cannot be defeated! I know your schools!"'' - The Round after his Aura is removed, Boss will use a '''-100%''' protected Shield of the Wizard's School, not including Balance.
**Each time a Damage Spell is used on the Boss, he'll recast all Shields based on the Wizard's School.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"No you don't!"'' - If a Wizard uses {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} on his Shield, he'll recast all Shields based on the Wizard's School.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Muhahaha! Two rounds 'till you go DOWN!"'' - Every 5 Rounds, he will cast a Shadow Spell that deals 100,000 Shadow Damage after two Rounds on the Wizard.
**''"Let's try that again..."'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Triage}} to remove the Damage over Time effect, he will recast it and then cast {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} on all Wizards.
