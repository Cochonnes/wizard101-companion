Originally from Zafaria, Kravenly rose to become a Warlord in Morganthe's Shadow Army, overseeing operations in Tyrian Gorge from the top of Banyan Tower. He is a parody of General Zaroff, the antagonist of the short story "''The Most Dangerous Game''."

**{{Link|Spell|Spirit Blast}} dealing '''1,120''' Death Damage, '''1,095''' Life Damage, or '''1,190''' Myth Damage to all enemies.
**{{Link|Spell|Spectral Blast}} dealing '''1,320''' Fire Damage, '''1,095''' Ice Damage, or '''1,650''' Storm Damage to all enemies.
***''"Health at 75%"'' - Once his health reaches 75% (11,280 remaining), he will interrupt cast his Spirit Blast.
***''"Health at 50%"'' - When his health reaches 50% (7,520 remaining), he will interrupt cast his Spirit Blast.
****His {{Link|Spell|Spectral Blast}} may trigger from this instead.
***''"Health at 25%"'' - When his health reaches 25% (3,760 remaining), he will interrupt cast his Spirit Blast.
****If multiple health cheats are triggered at once, the Spirit Blasts may not occur immediately together. One will trigger the same round and the others on following rounds.
