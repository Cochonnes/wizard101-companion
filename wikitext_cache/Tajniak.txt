| cheatnotes =
*The goal of this battle is to defeat the untruthful bear before the honest bear. ''Jawniak is the liar.''
*This Boss will shout statements about Jawniak's attacks every few Rounds.
*Jawniak will shout statements about Tajniak's attacks every few Rounds.

'''Tajniak'''
*{{Icon|Charm}} Tajniak will always cast Cloaked versions of positive [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]].
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Why don't we trade!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on Tajniak, he will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} that switches the [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]] between himself and the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} will not trigger this cheat.
'''Jawniak'''
*{{Icon|Curse}} Jawniak will always cast Cloaked versions of negative [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]].
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} If Tajniak is defeated first, Jawniak will cast {{Link|Spell|Poison}} on the Wizard in the first position, and then detonate it immediately for ~33,000 Death Damage.
**Every Round, he will cast Poison on another Wizard, going in order of battle position.
**Fleeing or porting will not stop this cheat.
