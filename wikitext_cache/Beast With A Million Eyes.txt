*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Wrong!"'' - If Beast With A Million Eyes is killed while the cheat is active, he will revive himself to 100% HP. Depending on the Aura that Beast with a Million Eyes has, and what School's damage is dealt to it, Beast with a Million Eyes will retaliate with various Spells:
**While {{Link|Spell|Sleet Storm}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Fire and Myth Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Lord of Winter}}.
**While {{Link|Spell|Devotion}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Death and Storm Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Spinysaur}}.
**While {{Link|Spell|Furnace}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Life and Storm Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Sun Serpent}}.
**While {{Link|Spell|Reliquary}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Fire and Life Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Celestial Calendar}}.
**While {{Link|Spell|Galvanic Field}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Death and Ice Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Storm Owl}}.
**While {{Link|Spell|Virulence}} is active, the Beast can be safely damaged with Ice and Myth Spells, or else it will cast {{Link|Spell|Avenging Fossil}}.
***For Spells that do multiple Schools of damage, such as {{Link|Spell|Hydra}}, each school will be counted separately, and if ''any'' of them would cause this Cheat to trigger, it will trigger once.
