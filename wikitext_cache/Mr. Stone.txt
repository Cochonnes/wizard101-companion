| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"By sun's light, I am as stone"'' - At the end of the first and fourth Round, and every fifth Round after (1, 4, 9, 14, etc.), Mr. Stone will cast {{Link|Spell|Power Nova}} on himself. This deals no damage and Polymorphs him into his Statue form for 2 Rounds.
**When in this Statue form, Mr. Stone is '''immune to all damage.'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"*Low unhappy growl*"'' - If Mr. Stone is attacked while in his statue Polymorph, he will unpolymorph himself, making him vulnerable to damage again.
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"You sat idle like stones, so stones you shall become!"'' - If Mr. Stone’s Statue Polymorph expires naturally (meaning he was not attacked during the Statue phase), he will cast {{Link|Spell|Medusa}} on the Wizard. This deals no damage but Polymorphs the Wizard into a {{Link|Polymorph|Statue}} for 99 Rounds.
**Polymorph has a deck of 77 Cards, all being {{Link|Spell|Pass}} Cards, except for one {{Link|Spell|Break Free (Myth)}} Card. The Pass Spells can be discarded and the Wizard may Pass normally to avoid a spell casting animation. The Wizard can be healed while polymorphed.
**If all Wizards are already a {{Link|Polymorph|Statue}}, Mr. Stone will not cast {{Link|Spell|Medusa}} after his Statue Polymorph expires naturally.
**This cheat can be dispelled with {{Link|Spell|Vaporize}}.
