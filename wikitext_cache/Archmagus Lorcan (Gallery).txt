| cheatnotes =
'''Archmagus Lorcan'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Serve your Archmagus!"'' - At the end of the first Round or any Round that he casts Shadow Sentinel, Archmagus Lorcan will summon two [[Creature:Elder Magus|Elder Magi]] Minions which becomes active two Rounds later.
*{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} Once Archmagus Lorcan receives a Shadow Pip, he will begin to cast [[Spell:Shadow Sentinel MOB|Shadow Sentinel]] every 3 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Caster}}{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Your sacrifice feeds the Shadow Web!"'' - After Archmagus Lorcan's Shadow Sentinel expires, the Elder Magi will interrupt-cast a Fire version of {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} that deals 10,000 Fire Damage to themselves and heals Archmagus Lorcan for 1,500 Health.
**Defeating Archmagus Lorcan before the Elder Magi will stop this cheat.

'''Lord Ombra'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Your power belongs to me!"'' - Every 4 Rounds, Lord Ombra will interrupt-cast a Balance version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Pips}} on the Wizard with the most pips, which steals 4 Pips and gives him 3 regular Pips.
**He will not cast this cheat if all Wizards have 4 Pips (in any combination of Power Pips and regular Pips) or less.
***School Pips do not count as any kind of Pip for the purposes of this cheat.
*{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} When Lord Ombra has 2 Shadow Pips, he will use his turn to cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Nova}}.
