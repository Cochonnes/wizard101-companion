| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"More souls for Sugarr"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, Sugarr Eater of Souls will cast a Shadow version of S'more Machine which deals 1,020 Shadow Damage, removes all Pips and places an {{Link|Spell|Impede}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Sugarr still has some bite left!"'' - If a Wizard defeats Sugarr Eater of Souls while a Sugar Cube minion is still alive, he'll be revived with 75% of his total health, and cast a Shadow {{Link|Spell|S'more Machine}} that removes 100% of the Wizard's Health defeating the Wizard who defeated the boss.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Beware the aftertaste!"'' - If a Wizard defeats a Sugar Cube, the defeated Sugar Cube will cast the 1,020 Damage Shadow S'more Machine on the Wizard that defeated them, removing their pips, and placing an {{Link|Spell|Impede}} on them.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Sugarr Rush!"'' - If Sugarr Eater of Souls takes damage, the Boss will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Sugar Rush}} that gives all allies 5 Pips, first giving 2 Power Pips then 1 Pip without an Impede aura, then places {{Link|Spell|Quicken}} on all allies.
