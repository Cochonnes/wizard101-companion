| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"For those of you arriving late, TAKE THIS!"'' - If a Wizard joins the Duel late, then the Sphinx will interrupt-cast Desert Hound (Sun).
*{{Icon|Round}} Every second Round, the Sphinx will ask a riddle. The answer to the riddle will be a type of Spell. Casting a Spell of the correct type to answer the riddle will not cause any response from the Sphinx. However, giving the incorrect answer will cause the Sphinx to cheat cast a Spell in response. Once all riddles have been asked, they will then be asked in random order. Players must wait until the next riddle before casting a different type of Spell.
**Pet "May Cast" talents can activate this cheat.
**Passing will not activate this cheat.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"What do swords and grass have in common?"''
**The correct answer is Blade.
**''"*Buzzer* Awwwwwww!"'' - If answered incorrectly, then the Sphinx will cast {{Link|Spell|Orthrus (Sun)}}.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Sticks and stones will never break my bones but names will never..."''
**The correct answer is "hurt me", given by casting a Damage Spell.
**''"*Buzzer* Awwwwwww!"'' - If answered incorrectly, then the Sphinx will cast a single-target version of {{Link|Spell|Power Nova (Shadow)}} on the Wizard that answered incorrectly.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Mice fear them, villains spring them!"''
**The correct answer is Trap.
**''"*Buzzer* Awwwwwww!"'' - If answered incorrectly, then the Sphinx will cast a '''+200%''' Shadow version of Feint with no self Trap.
*{{Icon|Weakness}} ''"The opposite of strength"''
**The correct answer is Weakness.
**''"*Buzzer* Awwwwwww!"'' - If answered incorrectly, then the Sphinx will cast an Indemnity-protected {{Link|Spell|Weakness (Shadow)}}.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"If your health they steal, you must..."''
**The correct answer is Heal.
**''"*Buzzer* Awwwwwww!"'' - If answered incorrectly, then the Sphinx will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Blessing}} that heals 10% of his total Health.
