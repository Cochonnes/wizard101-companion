Osseus Nightreaver possesses the last known set of Drake bones needed to make the {{Link|Spell|Skeletal Dragon}} spell for the level 58 Necromancer spell quest, ''Don't Be Scared''.  Osseus is located in the southwest corner of The Necropolis, in Nightreaver's Lair, which is a solo dungeon.  Wizards entering together will zone in to separate instances, alone.  Other Wizards will not be able to port into the dungeon.  This Boss is not repeatable.
