| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Death}} '''Rattlebones'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, Rattlebones will cast a Mana Burn at the Wizard.
:*{{Link|Spell|Unbalance}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*The Fairy Queen will cast this cheat if Rattlebones has been defeated.
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"Lets dampen the Heals!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell, he will cheat-cast Doom and Gloom.
:*{{Link|Spell|Strangle}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*Pet heals will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Why don't we trade?"'' - When a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on Rattlebones, he will cast Swap Ward to switch the Traps so that the higher percentage is on the Wizard.
:*{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent this cheat.

{{Icon|Life}} '''Fairy Queen (Rank 7)'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Fairies! Protect your Queen!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round (and every four Rounds after a Minion is defeated), the Fairy Queen will summon two [[Creature:Fairy|Fairies (Rank 7)]] of random Schools, which become active the following Round, and have 1,750 Health.
:*{{Link|Spell|Entangle}} will not dispel this cheat, but instead will cause the Minions to become active two Rounds later.
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}} ''"You'll never be as charming as Rattlebones!"'' - If a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spell is cast, there is a random chance that the Fairy Queen will cast {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} on the Wizard who received the Blade or cast the Global Spell.
:*{{Link|Spell|Dissipate}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*If a multi-target Blade spell is cast (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}), then she will only remove the Blade from the Wizard who cast the Spell.
*{{Icon|Feint}}''"Why don't we trade?"'' - When a Feint is placed on the Fairy Queen, she will cast Swap Ward to switch the Traps so that the higher percentage is on the Wizard.
:*Melt can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*Indemnity can be used to prevent this cheat.
