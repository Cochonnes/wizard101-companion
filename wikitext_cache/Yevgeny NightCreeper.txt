:*Harmful Charms that target all enemies (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Plague}}) will not trigger this cheat.

'''Interrupt Cycles'''

:This battle revolves around two different cheat cycles; a "Primary Interrupt Cycle" and a "Secondary Interrupt Cycle." Both cycles overlap each other throughout the battle.

:'''Primary Interrupt Cycle:'''
::The primary interrupt cycle consists of three active five Round sub-cycles. The entire cycle repeats itself after fifteen Rounds. This cycle removes one Shadow Pip from Yevgeny Nightcreeper but he can still cast Shadow Enhanced Spells after the cycle.
:{{Icon|Ice}} '''Ice Interrupt Cycle''' (Rounds 1-5)
::*''"I wrap you in armor!"'' - At the beginning of every Round in this sub-cycle, Yevgeny will cast {{Link|Spell|Frozen Armor (185)}} on himself or one of his Minions. 
::*''"Let the cold wind destroy you!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this sub-cycle (and every fifteen Rounds after), he will cast {{Link|Spell|Katabatic Wind}}, {{Link|Spell|Mass Ice Trap}}, {{Link|Spell|Iceblade (+45%)}}, and {{Link|Spell|Dark Weaver}}. This Spell will target the Wizard with the lowest current Health.
:{{Icon|Myth}} '''Myth Interrupt Cycle''' (Rounds 6-10)
::*''"I empower you!"'' - At the beginning of every Round in this sub-cycle, Yevgeny will cast {{Link|Spell|Buff Minion}} on himself or one of his Minions.
::*''"Crush my enemies!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this sub-cycle (and every fifteen Rounds after), he will cast {{Link|Spell|Saga of Heroes}}, {{Link|Spell|Mass Myth Trap}}, a {{Link|Spell|Mythblade (+40%)}}, and {{Link|Spell|Cursed Colossus}}.
:{{Icon|Death}} '''Death Interrupt Cycle''' (Rounds 11-15)
::*''"I decimate you!"'' - At the beginning of every Round in this sub-cycle, Yevgeny will cast a {{Link|Spell|Feint (+75%)}} on a random Wizard.
::*''"Klaatu Barada Nikto!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this sub-cycle (and every fifteen Rounds after), he will will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}, a {{Link|Spell|Deathblade (+45%)}}, {{Link|Spell|Mass Death Trap}}, and {{Link|Spell|From Beyond}}.

:'''Secondary Interrupt Cycle:'''
:The secondary interrupt cycle activates at the end of every third Round, repeating itself every nine Rounds.
::*''"Fear the howl of the moon!"'' - (Round 3) At the end of the third Round in this cycle, Yevgeny will cast {{Link|Spell|Shift Chaney (3 Pip)}}, dealing 800 Life Damage.
::*''"Fear the bite of Dragonspyre!"'' - (Round 6) At the end of the sixth Round, he will cast a Fire version of {{Link|ItemCard|Shift Akhtang}}, which deals 1,000 Fire Damage.
::*''"Fear the Blackwater!"'' - (Round 9) At the end of the ninth Round, he will cast a Moon version of {{Link|ItemCard|Shift Blackwater}}, which  deals 1,200 Moon Damage.
