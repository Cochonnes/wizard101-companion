Hades the Unseen is the final Boss of Tartarus. Defeating him is the final step in completing the last trial of the Immortal Games, and becoming the [[Basic:Aquila Badges| Glorious Golden Archon]]. All drops received during this battle should be recorded on this page.
*For the Challenge version of this battle, see {{Link|Creature|Hades the Unseen (Tartarus, Challenge)}}.

| speech =

:'''Poseidon:''' ''"You have come far, little Wizard, since last we battled.  Are you ready to face the full fury of the sea?"''
:'''Zeus:''' ''"Do not fear, Wizard.  We set the bar so high to ensure that only the truly heroic earn our greatest favors.  We will not hold back this time.  If you fall, you will live in the golden Elysian Fields for blessed eternity."''

| cheatnotes =
*Each of the Immortals may gain an extra Pip or Power Pip at the start of each Round.

:'''Hades the Unseen'''
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"The sands of your time are running out!"'' - Hades will use his turn to cast Impending Doom on a random Wizard.
**If a Spell is used to push the Damage over Time, such as {{Link|Spell|Shift}}, Hades will use {{Link|Spell|Triage}} to remove it.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Ahhh, the glorious taste of defeat!"'' - If any Wizard is defeated, Hades will cast a Death Spell that restores '''1,890''' Health over 3 Rounds to all Allies.
**This Spell will be cast up to 3 times, once for each Immortal present in the Battle.
***This cheat will '''not''' trigger if a Wizard is not defeated during the same Round they were attacked, or if a Wizard is defeated by a Damage over Time.
***This cheat '''will''' trigger if a Wizard defeats themselves, even if the Wizard is subsequently revived.
*The above cheat is also noted to trigger if Hades is present, and Zeus is defeated.

:'''Poseidon Earth-Shaker'''
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"We Immortals are infallible!"'' - During the first Round, and any other Round when he does not have an Aura, Poseidon will use his turn to cast {{Link|Spell|Infallible}}.
*{{Icon|Jinx}} {{Icon|Curse}} ''"You seek to weaken me? Drink from the depths!"'' - If a Charm or Trap is cast on Poseidon, he will use his Natural Attack on the caster, which removes all Traps on him.
**Fizzling will trigger this cheat.
**Single-target Prisms will trigger this cheat.
**Multi-target Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}} or {{Link|Spell|Mass Storm Prism}}, will not trigger this cheat.
**Spells with secondary effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Efreet}} or {{Link|Spell|Dr. Von's Monster}}, will trigger this cheat.
**Single-target Spells which have secondary effects that affect the Wizard, such as {{Link|Spell|Gnomes!}}, will trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to protect Charms and Wards from removal.

:'''Zeus Sky Father'''
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"YOU HESITATE TOO MUCH!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Spell that costs 6 or more Pips, Zeus will cast a {{Link|Spell|Minotaur}} on the Wizard.
**Healing Spells will trigger this cheat.
**Shadow Spells will trigger an additional Minotaur.
