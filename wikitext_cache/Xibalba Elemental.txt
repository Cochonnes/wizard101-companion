Xibalba Elemental is an optional Boss in the Crying Sky Raid. It is summoned on the top of the pyramid in Xibalba Wastes (North) by defeating a {{Link|Creature|Xibalba Sprite}}.
*Summoning Xibalba Elemental grants the "[[Basic:Badges|Foe of the Cometborn]]" Badge.

| speech =

| cheatnotes =
*''"What is death can never die!"'' - At the beginning of Round 1, Xibalba Elemental casts an Equalize Global, setting the Damage cap to '''1'''.
*After the first cheat, Xibalba Elemental casts one of the following Invunerability Auras:
**{{Icon|Balance}} - ''"Xibalba brings balance!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Balance''' for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Fire}} - ''"Xibalba burns!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Storm''' for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Ice}} - ''"Xibalba freezes!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Fire''' and for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Storm}} - ''"Xibalba crackles!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Ice''' for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Death}} - ''"Xibalba is death!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Myth''' for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Life}} - ''"Xibalba is life!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Death''' for 99 Rounds.
**{{Icon|Myth}} - ''"Xibalba is legend!"'' - Immune to all schools except '''Life''' for 99 Rounds.

*''"No!"'' - If a Wizard removes Xibalba Elemental's Aura, it will deal 1 Damage. Xibalba Elemental will then use Blessing on himself, healing for 100% of his max Health.
**Because this cheat deals Damage, the next cheat will be activated.
*"''Aaaahhhhhh!"'' - If Xibalba Elemental takes any Damage, he will use a no-Damage version of Supernova on himself, then use {{Link|TreasureCard|Exit Combat}} on the caster, and change to a new Immunity Aura.
*''"Death comes for you all!"'' - At the end of every Round, Xibalba Elemental casts a version of Deer Knight that deals 20% max Health Damage on all Wizards.
*''"I feel... MORTALITY."'' - After 7 Aura cycles, Xibalba Elemental will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom}}, removing the Damage cap.
**(Needs confirmation on Doom and Gloom version).
