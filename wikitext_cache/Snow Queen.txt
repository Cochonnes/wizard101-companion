| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Do you want to battle a Snowman?"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, another Awful Snowman will be summoned.
**{{Icon|Global}} This cheat also activates at the beginning of the first Round, attempting to spawn an Awful Snowman regardless of if the battle has room to accommodate it, though it will only enter battle if the minions that were initially present were defeated in a previous battle where the fight was not fully completed.
***If a Wizard attacks Snow Queen on the first Round before these minions enter battle, Snow Queen will activate her {{Link|Spell|Celestial Intervention}} cheat, which prevents the minion from entering combat, disappearing entirely.   
*{{Icon|Random}}{{Icon|Friend}}{{Icon|Aura}} ''"You are the Chosen One!"'' - The Snow Queen will cast a '''+15%''' Damage, '''+15%''' Armor Piercing {{Link|Spell|Icewind}} Aura lasting 1 Round on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"No feinting spells here!'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}}, a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (-2)}} will be cast on her twice
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} cannot be used to protect against this cheat.
**The balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"I am insulting you!"'' - The Awful Snowman Minions will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Mega Taunt}} that lasts 5 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"I didn't choose you!"'' - If a non-Chosen Wizard attacks her, she will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Celestial Intervention}} that restores 500 Health and removes 6 Shields to restores 180 Health per Shield removed.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"You hit me...I'll let it slide"'' - If the chosen Wizard attacks the Snow Queen, she will provide the Wizard with a protected {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade (+100%)}}.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Focus on me and me alone!"'' - If a Wizard uses an attack on an Awful Snowman, the Snow Queen will cast a protected {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (Moon)}} on herself.
**This also happens if any attack all enemy spell is used even if there are no minions present.
**Multi-target attacks will also trigger this cheat
**{{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute}} will also trigger this cheat
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Let it Snow!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Damage over Time effect on her, she will use {{Link|Spell|Cooldown}}.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Bad choice, Chosen One!"'' - If the chosen Wizard does anything other than hit, or a non-Chosen Wizard does anything, the Snow Queen will cast a protected Tower Shield (Moon) on herself. 
**Drain Spells will not trigger this cheat.
**Passing will not trigger this cheat.
**Pet and Weapon May Cast Spells can also trigger the cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Let the storm rage on!"'' - The Snow Queen will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Blizzard}} that targets all allies (the Boss's Team), removing all Wards, or targets all enemies (the Wizard's Team) removing all Charms.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} will prevent the removal from this Spell.
