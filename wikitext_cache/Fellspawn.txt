*{{Icon|Late}} If a Wizard joins the battle after the second Round, Fellspawn casts Call of Khrulhu (Shadow).
*{{Icon|Minion}} At the start of the first Round, Fellspawn summons any 3 of the following Minions:
**{{Link|Creature|Balance Tentacular Horror}}
**{{Link|Creature|Death Tentacular Horror}}
**{{Link|Creature|Fire Tentacular Horror}}
**{{Link|Creature|Ice Tentacular Horror}}

**{{Link|Creature|Myth Tentacular Horror}}
**{{Link|Creature|Shadow Tentacular Horror}}
**{{Link|Creature|Storm Tentacular Horror}}
*{{Icon|Minion}} At the end of the third Round, after a Minion is killed, a random school Minion is summoned. After this, another Minion will be summoned at the end of every fourth Round.
*{{Icon|Global}} At the start of the first Round, Fellspawn casts an {{Link|Spell|Equalize}}, which sets the Damage cap to '''9,999'''. Damage cannot go above this limit while Global is present. If the Global is replaced, it will be recast.
*{{Icon|Minion}} At the start of the fifth Round, Fellspawn summons an {{Link|Minion|Unhelpful Spawn}} on the Wizard's team if all duel slots are not filled.
**Sacrificial Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Sap Power}}, will not remove the Minion from the duel slot.
**If the Wizard in the first duel slot flees, this summon will disappear.
**Fellspawn will resummon the Minion soon after it is defeated.
*{{Icon|Round}} At the end of every fifth Round, Fellspawn will cheat-cast a {{Link|Spell|Black Mantle}}, {{Link|Spell|Hex}}, and {{Link|Spell|Weakness}}, each on a random Wizard, and cast {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}} and {{Link|Spell|Mass Tower Shield (Ice)}}.

*{{Icon|Polymorph}} Every six Rounds, Fellspawn will Polymorph into a Moon form lasting for three Rounds, starting on Round 3, with different cheats.

'''{{Link|Creature|Fellspawn}}''': {{Icon|Shadow}}{{Icon|Storm}}
*{{Icon|Blade}} If a Wizard casts a Blade, Fellspawn will {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn (Shadow)}} the caster, dealing 600 Shadow Damage per Pip, and {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble (Shadow)}} the receiver of the Blade.
**If an all-allies Spell such as Bladestorm is cast, only the caster's Blade will be removed.
**Non-Blade Charms such as {{Link|Spell|Guiding Light}} will not activate this cheat.
*If a Wizard casts a Dispel Charm on Fellspawn, he will cast a Sun version of Cleanse Charm, removing all harmful Charms from himself.

'''[[:Polymorph:Fellspawn|Moon Polymorph Form]]''': {{Icon|Moon}}
*While in Polymorph form, Fellspawn loses all stats and cannot gain Power Pips or Shadow Pips.
*{{Icon|Trap}} If a Wizard casts a Trap, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)}} to remove all Traps from the target of the Trap, and cast {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} that Stuns the caster for two Rounds.
**Spells with a Trap after effect, such as {{Link|Spell|Brimstone Revenant}}, will trigger this cheat.
**Prisms will not trigger this cheat.
