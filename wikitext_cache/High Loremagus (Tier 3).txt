| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Only a true master of lore can defeat me!"'' - At the start of the First Round, and every round thereafter for any Wizard or Minion lacking an Aura, High Loremagus (Tier 3) will cast a Star School Spell on that Wizard or Minion, applying a '''-200%''' Accuracy Aura of the target's School that lasts for 99 turns.
**Summoned Monstrology Minions that are Astral or Shadow School will be targeted by this cheat, but no Aura will be applied to them as there's no Aura for those Schools.
**Summoned Monstrology Minions that are dual schooled will get a '''-200%''' Accuracy Aura for their second school.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"You know nothing of lore. Do your research!"'' - If a Wizard or Minion replaces the given Aura, High Loremagus (Tier 3) will recast the Aura on them.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Uh oh... Watch out for vermin!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} or {{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} is cast on her, she will cast {{Link|Spell|Camp Bandit (Tier 4a)}}.
** The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
