| cheatnotes =
'''Takanobu the Masterless''' {{Target|Spiral}}
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"No! I disarm you."'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade Spell, then Takanobu the Masterless will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the recipient of the Blade.
**If a multi-target Blade is cast, then Takanobu will only remove the Blade from the caster.

*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"My katana dances around your shield."'' - If a Wizard casts any Storm Shield Spell, then Takanobu will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Storm Prism}} on the recipient of the Shield.

*{{Icon|All Enemies (Squared)}} ''"Control your Energy!"'' - If a Wizard casts an [[:Category:All Enemies Spells|All Enemies Spell]], then Takanobu will interrupt-cast a version of [[Spell:Tempest (6 Pip)|Tempest]] that always deals '''560''' Storm Damage.

*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"This wind is an omen!"'' - At the beginning of any Round, if Takanobu has at least 6 Pips, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Darkwind}}.

*{{Icon|Pip}} ''"KIAI!"'' - At the end of any Round, if Takanobu has at least 5 Pips, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Supercharge}} on himself.

'''Bladeless Ronin'''

*{{Target|Dagger}}{{Icon|Balance}}{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"We must stay centered!"'' - If any enemy receives Balance Damage, then this Bladeless Ronin will interrupt-cast an all-enemies version of [[Spell:Unbalance (All Enemies)|Unbalance]].

*{{Target|Key}}{{Icon|Elemental}}{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"Guard against the elements!"'' - If any enemy receives Fire, Ice, or Storm Damage, then this Bladeless Ronin will interrupt-cast an all-enemies version of [[Spell:Quench (All Enemies)|Quench]], [[Spell:Melt (All Enemies)|Melt]], or [[Spell:Dissipate (All Enemies)|Dissipate]] based on the School of the Damage that was received.

*{{Target|Gem}}{{Icon|Spirit}}{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"Spirits, guard us!"'' - If any enemy receives Myth, Life, or Death Damage, then this Bladeless Ronin will interrupt-cast an all-enemies version of [[Spell:Vaporize (All Enemies)|Vaporize]], [[Spell:Entangle (All Enemies)|Entangle]], or [[Spell:Strangle (All Enemies)|Strangle]] based on the School of the Damage that was received.

*Damage over Time effects do not trigger the Bladeless Ronins' cheats.

*If a Spell deals Damage more than once, then the Bladeless Ronin will cast an all-enemies Dispel matching the School of the Damage that were received in the order that they were received.
**Casting a Spell that hits multiple enemies will result in the respective Bladeless Ronin repeating their cheat per enemy hit.
**Casting Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Hydra}} on any enemy will result in the {{Target|Key}} Bladeless Ronin casting Quench, Melt, and Dissipate, in that order.
**Casting spells such as {{Link|Spell|Scales of Destiny}} on any enemy will result in the {{Target|Gem}} Bladeless Ronin casting Entangle, the {{Target|Key}} Bladeless Ronin casting Melt, and the {{Target|Dagger}} Bladeless Ronin casting Unbalance, in that order.
**Dealing Damage through a Prism will result in the Bladeless Ronin casting a Dispel that matches the School of the Damage after the Prism is applied.
