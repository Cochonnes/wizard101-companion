*{{Icon|Late}} If a Wizard joins the duel late, Aberrant Paradox will cast a single-target version of Power Nova for that Wizard's maximum Health, ignoring all Resist and all Wards. This occurs at the beginning of the Round.
*{{Icon|Global}} At the beginning of the first Round, the Divine Paradox in the background will cast a random School Global Spell. These Global Spells boost the Damage of their School and weaken the Damage of the opposite School. The only exception is the Balance School, which weakens the damage of all other Schools. The first Global Spell lasts two Rounds. All others last three Rounds.
**When this Global Spell is cast, all Wizards will receive a card related to the School of the Global Spell in their hand that cannot be discarded. Reshuffle does not remove them. These cards either help the enemy team or hurt the Wizard's team.
**Changing the Global Spell will make the Aberrant Paradox recast the previous Global Spell that was in effect.
**If the Global spell is changed by an enemy spell, such as {{Link|Spell|Gaze of Fate}}, the Global will not be recast.
