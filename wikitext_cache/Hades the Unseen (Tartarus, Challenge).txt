| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"The sands of your time are running out!"'' - At the beginning of every Round, Hades will cast a +70% incoming Death Damage Aura for 4 Rounds on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"We Immortals are Infallible"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Poseidon will cast {{Link|Spell|Infallible}}.
*{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Death is Eternal"'' - Whenever a Wizard casts a helpful Charm or helpful Aura, Hades will cast {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on himself.
*{{Icon|Harmful Aura}} ''"Death is Vengeful"'' - Whenever a Wizard casts a harmful Aura, Hades will cast his Natural Attack on the caster.
**Auras that increase incoming Damage such as {{Link|Spell|Berserk}} or {{Link|Spell|Galvanic Field}} will also trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"Death is Patient"'' - Whenever a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, Hades will cast Deathblade on himself.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Your punishment shall be swift!"'' - Whenever a Wizard casts a helpful Ward, Zeus will cast Minotaur on the recipient.
*{{Icon|Death}} ''"Ahhh, the glorious taste of defeat!"'' - Whenever a Wizard, Poseidon, or Zeus are defeated, Hades will cast a protected +40% version of Deathblade on himself.
