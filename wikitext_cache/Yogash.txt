| cheatnotes =
*''"Too small! Try again!"'' - Spells that cost less than 3 Pips will result in Yogash interrupt-casting a Tower Shield on himself.
**[[:Category:Damage Spells|Damage]] Spells costing less than 3 Pips, result in Yogash interrupt-casting a version of Satyr on himself that restores '''5,000''' Health, then casts a Tower Shield on himself.
**X Pip spells only trigger this cheat if the Spell costs less than 3 Pips.
**[[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spells costing less than 3 Pips, result in Yogash interrupt-casting Tower Shield on himself and a '''-5%''' version of Doom and Gloom.
**If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] or [[:Category:Manipulation Spells|Manipulation]] Spell, then Yogash will interrupt-cast Wraith on the caster.
**[[:Category:Steal Spells|Steal]] Spells result in Yogash interrupt-casting a Tower Shield on himself. (Example:Ghoul)
**[[:Category:Polymorph Spells|Polymorph]] Spells will cause Yogash to interrupt-cast Orthrus on the caster.
***{{Link|Spell|Taco Toss}} and {{Link|Spell|Stinky Salute}} will result in Yogash casting Satyr on himself, and Wraith at the Wizard.

*Unknown Cheat Trigger:
**If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Steal Spells|Steal]] Spell, then Yogash will interrupt-cast a '''-90%''' version of Tower Shield on himself. (Ghoul does not trigger this cheat) Needs confirmation if this cheat still triggers.

*'''Spells that don't trigger this cheat:'''
**Manipulation Spells that steal hanging effects do not trigger this cheat.
***(Example:Steal Charm, Steal Ward, Snow Drift)
**{{Link|Spell|Cooldown}} does not trigger this cheat.
**X Pip [[:Category:Minion Spells|Minion Spells]] Spells do not trigger this cheat.
**[[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spells that cost more than 2 Pips do not trigger this cheat.
**[[:Category:Aura Spells|Aura]] Spells, Shadow Self Spells, Shadow Creature Spells, and Pet May Cast Spells will not trigger this cheat.
**Charms and Wards that are cast by active [[:Category:Shadow Creature Spells|Shadow Creatures]] will not trigger this cheat.
