| cheatnotes = 
'''The Prophet'''
*''"No Sanctuary in the Night."'' - At the beginning of the first Round and if a Wizard joins the duel late, The Prophet casts a negative version of {{Link|Spell|Acrimony}} on all Wizards that turns all incoming Healing into Damage for 99 Rounds. The Aura is applied twice, but the effects do not stack.
**If a Wizard casts an Aura Spell on the first Round, one of the negative Auras will be replaced, and the Wizard will receive the effects of the remaining one, as well the one they cast, at the same time.
**Casting any Aura on any other turn will cause The Prophet to cast a no-Damage version of {{Link|Spell|Supernova}}, removing the Wizard's Aura, and then will recast this cheat on all wizards.
'''Enki the Heretic'''
*''"To face the wolf, bare the claws."'' - At the beginning of every second Round, Enki the Heretic will cheat-cast a +25% {{Link|Spell|Fireblade}} or +25% {{Link|Spell|Deathblade}} on each Wizard.
*''"The Light burns!"'' - At the beginning of the every fifth Round, Enki will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Incindiate}}.
*''"The Light blesses you."'' - If a Wizard casts a positive Charm, Enki will cheat-cast an all enemies version of {{Link|Spell|Sprite}}, restoring 500 Health over 4 Rounds on each Wizard.
*''"Feel the bite of Raven!"'' - After Enki attacks, or if a Wizard joins the duel late, Enki will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Scald}}.
