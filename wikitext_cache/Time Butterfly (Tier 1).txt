*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You're late! Or are you early?"'' - Casts a {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} on any Wizard who joins the duel after the first Round.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Every two Rounds, Time Butterfly (Tier 1) will cast a Shadow version of Empower to give 1 Power Pip to all allies, and hidden-casts a Spell that switches the first turn of the duel.
*{{Icon|Feint}} If a Feint is placed on Time Butterfly (Tier 1), then it will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} to switch the Traps with the Wizard who cast it.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} If Time Butterfly (Tier 1) is defeated before or on the same turn that all of the Minions are defeated, then it will cast a Sun version of Blessing to restore 100% Health on all allies.
** This Spell will revive any Minions defeated during the Round.

{{Icon|Minion}} '''Minion Summon Cycle:'''
*'''Stage 1:'''
**At the start of the battle, Time Butterfly (Tier 1) will cast a Shadow Spell to summon '''{{Link|Creature|Young Morganthe (Tier 1)}}'''. Occasionally, three of the Minions will be summoned.

*'''Stage 2:'''
**On the following Round after '''Young Morganthe (Tier 1)''' has been defeated, Time Butterfly (Tier 1) casts a Shadow Spell to summon Young Morganthe (Tier 1) and '''{{Link|Creature|Roberto (Tier 1)}}'''.

*'''Stage 3:'''
**On the following Round after '''Young Morganthe (Tier 1)''' and '''Roberto (Tier 1)''' have been defeated, Time Butterfly (Tier 1) casts a Shadow Spell to summon Young Morganthe (Tier 1), Roberto (Tier 1) and '''{{Link|Creature|Broodmother (Tier 1)}}'''.

*'''Variation:'''
**On the first Round, three Young  Morganthe (Tier 1) will be summoned instead of just one.  In this case, the Stage 2 summon will be omitted, and the Round after the three Minions are defeated, the Stage 3 summon will occur.

{{Icon|Fire}} ''Broodmother:'''
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Give me your health, drone!"'' - Broodmother (Tier 1) will cast {{Link|TreasureCard|Cheat}} to steal 15% max Health from Time Butterfly and restores 15% of its max Health, then a {{Link|Spell|Dragonblade}} to a random ally, and {{Link|Spell|Mass Tower Shield (Shadow)}} (-55%) to all allies.
