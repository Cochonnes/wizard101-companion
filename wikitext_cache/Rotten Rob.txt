| cheatnotes =
*During the first Round, Rotten Rob will use his turn to cast {{Link|Spell|Scald}}.
**{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to prevent this spell.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Oh, he's doing that Fire school thing, I think Storms can counter it!"'' - At the end of every Round, Rotten Rob will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Efreet (Tier 2b)}} that deals 100 Fire Damage and deals 450 Fire Damage over 6 Rounds if target has a Damage over Time effect.
**{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to prevent this cheat.
**If Rotten Rob's initial {{Link|Spell|Scald}} fizzles, or is prevented with {{Link|Spell|Quench}}, he will continue to cast Efreet without any Damage over Time effect. 
** If the Wizard is second to cast, {{Link|Spell|Triage}} can be used to remove the Damage over Time and prevent additional Damage over Time effects when Efreet is cast.
*{{Icon|Healing}} If Rotten Rob is defeated while his Efreet cheat is still active, then he will revive with full Health.
*{{Icon|Spell}} *''"Hang on! Try this on him!"'' - At the beginning of the 4th Round, or after Rotten Rob has revived and then randomly at the end of subsequent Rounds, Daisy Willowmancer will give all Wizards the following Spell:
**{{GivesSpell|cards=1|size=x150px|Stormzilla (Tier 2b)}}
*{{Icon|Storm}} ''"Wowzer, Storm douses Fire, huh?"'' - If a Wizard casts {{Link|Spell|Stormzilla (Tier 2b)}}, Rotten Rob will remove all Damage over Time effects on all Wizards and stop his Efreet cheat.
**''"How'd you do that?"'' - If a Wizard uses another Storm Spell that removes Damage over Time effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Lightning Bats (Tier 2b)}}, Rotten Rob will remove all Damage over Time effects from all Wizards and stop his Efreet cheat.
