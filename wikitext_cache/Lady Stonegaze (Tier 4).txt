*''"Mind your gaze, lest ye be turned to stone!"'' - Lady Stonegaze (Tier 4) gives a warning at the beginning of the first Round. 
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"Haha! Another statue for my garden!"'' - If a Wizard casts any Spell on Lady Stonegaze (Tier 4), including AoE Spells, Traps, or Charms, she will Polymorph the Wizard into {{Link|Polymorph|Statue}} for 99 Rounds.
**Polymorph has a deck of 77 Cards, all being {{Link|Spell|Pass}} Cards, except for one {{Link|Spell|Break Free (Myth)}} Card. The Pass Spells can be discarded and the Wizard may Pass normally to avoid a spell casting animation. The Wizard can be healed while polymorphed.
**Stonegaze's Shadow-Enhanced Spells will remove Polymorph effects from the Wizard.
**If Lady Stonegaze (Tier 4) is damaged by a Spell that is not from the Blindfolded Hits, she will use a Moon version of {{Link|Spell|Blessing}} to restore 100% max Health on herself.
