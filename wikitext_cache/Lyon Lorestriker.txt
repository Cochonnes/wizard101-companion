| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Minion}} '''Minion Cycle:'''
*''"Welcome to the party!"'' - At the end of every Round, he will summon one Lyon Lorestriker (Minion) starting with 1,200 Health.
**''”Friends, stay a bit longer!”'' - If Lyon has 50% or less of his total health remaining, the new minions summoned start with 2,000 Health and an extra Power Pip. Any existing minions will not be replaced until they are defeated.
**Rarely, Lyon Lorestriker may summon multiple minions, or not summon a minion at all.
**''"Tsk tsk. Another friend for you!"'' - Lyon Lorestriker will summon an additional minion during the same round if the Wizard casts any following spells:
***If a Wizard uses a Heal Over Time spell (e.g. Regenerate, Sprite), he will cast a Life Spell that removes the HoT on the Wizard who received it.
****Pet spells do not trigger this cheat.
****{{Link|Spell|Availing Hands}} does not trigger this cheat.
***If a Trap or Prism is placed on him, he will use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}}.
****Multi-target Traps or Prisms such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}} and {{Link|Spell|Mass Storm Prism}} do not activate this cheat.
****This cheat can be avoided using {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} or {{Link|Spell|Vaporize}}.
**{{Icon|Ice}} ''"Crowds just warm my icy heart!"'' - Once Lyon Lorestriker acquires three minions, he will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Blizzard}}  dealing '''750-790''' Ice Damage at the beginning of every Round.
**''"We need a bigger crowd!"'' - If all Minions are defeated, he will once again summon them at the end of each Round.
