*At the beginning of every Round, Hunhau will cast one of the following on a random Wizard:
**{{Icon|Fire}} ''"Let all flesh burn away!"'' - Deals '''300''' Fire Damage over 10 Rounds to the Wizard, applies a '''-250''' Healing Ward on the Wizard, and restores '''400''' Health over 10 Rounds to self
**{{Icon|Ice}} ''"Accept Death's cold embrace."'' - Applies a '''-250''' Healing Ward on the Wizard, a '''-80%''' Life Shield on self, and a '''-50%''' Healing Charm on the Wizard
**{{Icon|Storm}} ''"Thunder gives way to silence."'' - Applies a Stormblade on self, restores '''400''' Health over 10 Rounds to self, and applies a '''-50%''' Healing Charm on the Wizard
**{{Icon|Myth}} ''"Even your story will wither."'' - Deals '''300''' Fire Damage over 10 Rounds to the Wizard, applies a '''-250''' Healing Ward on the Wizard, and a '''-80%''' Life Shield on self
**{{Icon|Life}} ''"Life consumes Life."'' - Applies a Stormblade on self, deals '''300''' Fire Damage over 10 Rounds to the Wizard, and restores '''400''' Health over 10 Rounds to self
**{{Icon|Death}} ''"Death is inexorable."'' - Applies a Stormblade on self, a '''-80%''' Life Shield on self, and a '''-50%''' Healing Charm on the Wizard
*''"And now... your suffering ends."'' - At the end of any Round, if any of the following conditions are met, then Hunhau will cast a Death version of Meteor Strike that deals 100% max Health Damage on all Wizards.

***{{Icon|Charm}} 4 Helpful Charms
***{{Icon|Ward}} 4 Helpful Wards
***{{Icon|Heal over Time}} 4 Heal over Time effects
**'''Condition on Wizards:''' If any one Wizard has at least four of any harmful hanging effect:
***{{Icon|Curse}} 4 Harmful Damage Charms OR 4 Harmful Healing Charms
***{{Icon|Jinx}} 4 Harmful Damage Wards OR 4 Harmful Healing Wards
***{{Icon|Damage over Time}} 4 Damage over Time effects
**'''Global Condition:''' If Hunhau's and the Wizards' team collectively have at least one of each Helpful and Harmful hanging effect, respectively:
***{{Icon|Charm}}{{Icon|Ward}}{{Icon|Heal over Time}} Hunhau's team has at least one Helpful Charm, Helpful Ward, and a Heal over Time effect
***{{Icon|Curse}}{{Icon|Jinx}}{{Icon|Damage over Time}} The Wizards' team has at least one Harmful Charm, Harmful Ward, and a Damage over Time effect
