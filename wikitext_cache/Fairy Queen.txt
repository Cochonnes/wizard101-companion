'''Note:''' With the FTUE revamp in Wizard City, the Fairy Queen has been retired from the game.<br/><br/>
The Fairy Queen was a semi-rare Boss on Unicorn Way that was different from most Bosses by the fact that she roams the streets like a common Creature. Also, she was the only Rank One Boss in the entire game. Her appearance is similar to a {{Link|Creature|Dark Fairy}}, with a slightly different color scheme: her wings are purple instead of red, and she has a crown instead of a hair band. Her dress and shoes are also dark purple. This allows you to tell the difference between a Dark Fairy and the Fairy Queen from a distance, with no visible name tags. She does count as a Dark Fairy for quest purposes. The Fairy Queen tends to stay around the far end of Unicorn Way near the {{Link|Location|Hedge Maze}}, where the Dark Fairies patrol. She used to also be found roaming by the {{Link|Creature|Lost Soul}}s, though much less commonly. The Fairy Queen appears to have had multiple spawn points. One was near the entrance to the Hedge Maze, while another was near the corner where {{Link|NPC|Private O'Ryan}}'s house is located.
