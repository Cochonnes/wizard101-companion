| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Your tardiness is disgusting!"'' - If any Wizards join the duel after the second Round, King Detritus will summon an additional Minion as well as cast Steal Health on one of his Minions and move up to the next Polymorph stage.
**If there are no Minions present, King Detritus will cast Steal Health on a random Wizard.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Ah ah ah!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is cast on King Detritus, one of his Minions will cast a Shadow Spell twice to move the Trap onto itself. Feints can be safely cast during the first Round, while the Minions are not yet active.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Refuse, unite!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, King Detritus will cast a Sun Spell that summons random {{Link|Creature|Trash}} Minions equal to the number of Wizards present.
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Your sacrifice is for the greater garbage good!"'' - King Detritus will cast a Sun version of Steal Health on his Minion to heal himself 100% of the Damage dealt to the Minion.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"The trash is piling up!"'' - King Detritus will summon another random Trash Minion if he is out of Minions.

*''"This fight has become quite the dumpster fire"'' - Gives '''+20%''' Damage, '''+20%''' Armor Piercing, and '''+20%''' Resistance, for 99 Rounds.
*''"Time for the trash to take you out!"'' - Gives '''+25%''' Damage, '''+25%''' Armor Piercing, and '''+25%''' Resistance, for 99 Rounds.
**After these 5 cheats, lines will recycle giving King Detritus the following Auras:
***''Stage 6'' - '''+30%''' Damage, '''+30%''' Armor Piercing, and '''+30%''' Resistance, for 99 Rounds.
***''Stage 7'' - '''+35%''' Damage, '''+35%''' Armor Piercing, and '''+35%''' Resistance, for 99 Rounds.
