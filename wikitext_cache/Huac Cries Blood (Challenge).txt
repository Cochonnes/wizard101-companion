| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Let the Old Day Die!"'' - If anyone on the Wizard's team is defeated, the Boss that defeated them will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Meteor Strike}} that deals 100% max Health Damage to all Wizards.

*{{Icon|All Enemies (Squared)}} ''"Together, we return!"'' - If more than one enemy is defeated on the same turn, including if all enemies are defeated at the same time, all enemies will cast a version of Blessing, restoring themselves to 100% max Health.

*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"No defense against the inevitable"'' - If a Wizard casts a Shield, Huac Cries Blood will cast {{Link|Spell|Helephant (Tier 5b)}} on the Wizard with the lowest Health.

*{{Icon|Heal over Time}} ''"All succumb to decay"'' - If a Wizard casts a Heal over Time, Skurkis Screaming Moon will cast {{Link|Spell|Skeletal Pirate (Tier 5b)}} on the Wizard with the lowest Health.

*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"No curse can snare me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a harmful Charm on Yacate Farwalker, Yacate Farwalker will cast {{Link|Spell|Stone Colossus (Tier 5b)}} on the Wizard with the lowest Health.
:*Dispels and harmful Accuracy Charms do not trigger this cheat.

*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"No curse can snare me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a harmful Charm on Huac Cries Blood or Skurkis Screaming Moon, Yacate Farwalker will cast {{Link|Spell|Glacial Fortress}} on the Boss who receives the harmful Charm. 
:*{{Icon|Dispel}} Dispels will trigger this cheat. If the harmful Charm is a Dispel cast on Huac Cries Blood, he will cast a protected +35% Fireblade on himself. If the harmful Charm is a Dispel cast on Skurkis Screaming Moon, she will cast a protected {{Link|Spell|Deathblade (+40%)}} on herself.
:*Harmful Accuracy Charms do not trigger this cheat.
:*If a Spell is cast that applies harmful Charms on multiple enemies, such as {{Link|Spell|Plague}}, Yacate Farwalker will only cast Stone Colossus and will not cast Glacial Fortress.
:*Yacate Farwalker's Glacial Fortress can be dispelled with {{Link|Spell|Melt}}.
