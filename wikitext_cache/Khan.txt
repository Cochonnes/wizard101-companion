*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"I'm afraid it's more difficult than you think, Wizard!"'' - At the end of each Round, if Khan does not have an equal number of Minions to the number of Wizards, he will summon another.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"From Tartarus' heart I will grapple with thee!"'' - At the end of every 5th Round:
**A Minion will cast a Moon version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Health}} that sacrifices themselves to heal Khan for '''4,000''' Health.
**Then, Khan casts an all-enemies version of {{Link|Spell|Wraith}} that gives him a '''+500%''' [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] of the Minion's School, and deals '''500''' Damage of that School (using the Blade).
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Is that the best you can do? I'm laughing at your superior magic!"'' - If a Trap is placed on Khan while a Minion is present, a Minion will interrupt-cast an Ice spell to steal one Trap.
**This can be avoided with {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}}.
