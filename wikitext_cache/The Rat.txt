The Rat, also known as {{Link|NPC|Rasputin}}, is the final Boss of {{Link|Location|Polaris}}. He is the firstborn Child of [[NPC:Old Cob|Grandfather Spider]].
*The strategy of this battle is to defeat The Rat in less than 30 rounds.

| speech =
:'''{{Link|NPC|Rasputin}}:''' ''"Oh, what a pleasant surprise! You decided to attend the ceremony after all. The final moments of this twisted experiment called the Spiral. For centuries, we lay in the darkness and torment, but we dreamed of this day, when shadows would rise and pure Chaos would be properly restored. Now that we've reached this fateful moment, I can finally shed this silly skin..."''
:'''The Rat:''' ''"Behold my true form, you insignificant mortal. It is much more comfortable... and immensely more powerful. Come on and challenge me. I welcome the amusement. With my Titan, I will destroy you and your world at the same time."''

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - Wizards who join the duel after the battle has started, will be punished with a '''+60%''' Fire, Ice, and Global [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]], as well as a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that transfers all negative hanging effects on The Rat to the late Wizard.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Fool me once?"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on The Rat, he will shout this as a warning. 
**''"Can't get fooled again!"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} has already been placed on The Rat, any subsequent attempts to Feint him will trigger a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that removes all negative hanging effects on him, and places them onto the Wizard who cast the {{Link|Spell|Feint}}.
***{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint}} will not trigger this cheat.
*** The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} will not be removed, but will instead summon a {{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 07}} minion as per The Rat's ''"The Spiral is a disease, we have the cure!"'' cheat.

'''Minions'''

As long as there are Minions occupying all three enemy spots, the Rat will be unable to summon additional Minions in response to a trigger cast until a spot opens up.
:{{Icon|Trap}} '''{{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 07}}'''
:*''"The Spiral is a disease, we have the cure!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] spell, The Rat will cast a Death Spell to summon a {{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 07}} Minion with 1 Health 103% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 3% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*This can be dispelled with {{Link|Spell|Strangle}}, preventing the Minion from being summoned.
::*On its first turn, the Minion will cast a {{Link|Spell|Plague (-25%)}}, then polymorph to have 10 Health and 105% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 5% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its second turn, the Minion will cast a {{Link|Spell|Mass Infection}}, and polymorph to have 100 Health and 107% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life,  Death, and Balance, and 7% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its third turn, the Minion will cast a multi-target all enemies {{Link|Spell|Bad Juju (2 Pip)}}, then self-destruct.
::*{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint}} will trigger these cheats.
::*{{Link|Spell|Mockenspiel}} will trigger these cheats.
::*Spells that place negative charms will also trigger these cheats.

:{{Icon|Heal}} '''{{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 34}}'''
:*''"You call that healing? Behold my creation!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Healing Spells|Healing]] Spell, The Rat will summon a {{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 34}} Minion with 1 Health and 103% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 3% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its first turn, the Minion will cast {{Link|Spell|Guiding Light}} and {{Link|Spell|Sanctuary}}, then polymorph to have 10 Health and 105% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 5% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its second turn, the Minion will either cast {{Link|Spell|Regenerate (225+1,100)}}, if The Rat has been injured, or pass if he is uninjured or fully defeated. It will then polymorph to have 100 Health 107% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 7% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its third turn, the Minion will either cast {{Link|Spell|Rebirth (730, 100%)}} (if The Rat has been injured), or {{Link|Spell|Spinysaur}} on a random Wizard (if The Rat is uninjured or fully defeated), then self-destruct.
:::*''"Say bye-bye to my friend, and hello to your doom!"'' - If the Minion used {{Link|Spell|Rebirth}}, The Rat will also cast a {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}.
:::*When a defeated Minion is affected by {{Link|Spell|Rebirth}}, it will be revived for an additional Round. The revived Minion will always cast the same spell it used previous to its defeat.
::*Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
::*Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Link}} and {{Link|Spell|Power Link}} will not trigger this cheat.

:{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}} '''{{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 291}}'''
:*''"You have blades, but I have Borealis!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] spell, The Rat will cast a Storm Spell to summon a {{Link|Creature|Titan Experiment 291}} Minion with 1 Health and 103% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 3% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*This can be dispelled with {{Link|Spell|Dissipate}}, preventing the Minion from being summoned.
::*On its first turn, the Minion will cast {{Link|Spell|Windstorm (+25%)}}, and polymorph to have 10 Health and 105% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 5% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its second turn, the Minion will cast {{Link|Spell|Wild Bolt (15-150-1,150)}} on a random Wizard, and polymorph to have 100 Health and 107% Resist to Fire, Ice, Storm, Myth, Life, Death, and Balance, and 7% Resist to Shadow and Astral school Damage Spells.
::*On its third turn, the Minion will cast a multi-target all enemies {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt (1,125)}}, then self-destruct.
:::*''"Borealis is hard to pierce, eh?"'' - The Rat will shout this whenever a Titan Experiment 291 self-destructs.
::*Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.

{{Icon|Round}} '''Titan Timer'''
*''"Down comes the Sky Anchor, and the Spiral with it!"'' - At the beginning of Rounds 2, 10, and 20, the Titan will attack the Sky Anchor, causing 1,043 Shadow Damage to all Wizards. The Damage will increase for Rounds 10 and 20.
*''"My Titan will see you disintegrated!"'' - At the beginning of Rounds 5, 15, and 25, the Titan will deal 1,043 Shadow Damage to all Wizards. The Damage will increase for Rounds 15 and 25.
*''"And alas, the Anchor falls, the Spiral unravels..."'' - At the start of Round 30 (and every Round thereafter), the Titan will deal 1,000,000 Shadow Damage with a higher Armor Piercing to all Wizards, thus ending the fight.
:*If The Rat is defeated by a Damage Over Time on the 30th round, and a Wizard using {{Link|Spell|Guardian Spirit}}, the timer will stop, and the Wizard will survive.
