| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}}''"Tardiness is unacceptable"'' - If a Wizard is late after the second Round, Boss will cast a Moon version of Ship of Fools dealing '''100,000''' Moon Damage.

{{Icon|Global}} '''Equalize Cycle:'''
*''"Your doom approaches"'' - At the beginning of the first round, Boss will cast a Death version {{Link|Spell|Equalize}}, which sets the Damage cap to 250.
*''"Closer"'' - At the beginning of the third round, Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 500.
*''"and closer..."'' - At the beginning of the fifth round, Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 1,000.
*''"It's almost here"'' - At the beginning of the seventh round, Boss will cast a Death version Equalize, which sets the Damage cap to 2,000.
*''"Your doom is NOW!"'' - At the beginning of the tenth round, Boss will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (Moon)}}.
*''"You wish to hasten your doom?"'' - If a Wizard changes the Global, Boss will recast Equalize.

{{Icon|Round}} '''End of Round Cheat'''
*''"Kneel! For I am the harbinger of your demise!"'' - At the end of every Round, Lord Nightshade will interrupt with a Moon version of '''Deer Knight''' dealing '''100,000''' Moon Damage.
