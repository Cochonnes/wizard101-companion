Krokenkahmen is the current ruler of the Nirini family, located at the end of the Throne Room of Fire inside of the {{Link|Location|Pyramid of the Sun}}.  After defeating {{Link|Creature|Prince Manu Nirini}} and obtainined the Serpent Staff to activate the {{Link|Location|Map Room}}, the mechanisms fail to engage because a piece is still missing. {{Link|NPC|Professor Winthrop}} realizes the needed component is the staff's Eye of Krok, which Krokenkahmen has taken in a display of power, and sends the Wizard to retrieve the Eye which, combined with the Staff, will reveal the location of the Order of the Fang.

A {{Link|Creature|Krokomummy}} and {{Link|Creature|Nirini Fire Spirit}} will appear if more than two Wizards are present.
