| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"I can see through you!"'' - If {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is cast on Shane von Shane, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Pact (+30%)}} on himself.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
**The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"I will not bow down to you!"'' - If a harmful Charm is cast on Shane von Shane, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice (Death)}} on himself.
**All-enemies harmful Charms such as {{Link|Spell|Plague}} do not trigger cheat.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"More life for me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, then Shane von Shane will interrupt-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Vampire}} that deals '''1,050''' Death Drain Damage and returns half Health to the caster.
**Pet Maycast Heals will trigger this cheat.
**This cheat will not trigger for [[Spell:Monster Mash (1,170)|Beguiled]] Wizards.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Interrupt Cycle:'''
*''"We feast tonight!"'' - At the beginning of the second Round in this cycle, Shane von Shane will interrupt-cast a version of Vampire that deals '''1,050''' Death Drain Damage to a random Wizard and returns half Health to the caster.
*''"The monster begins to rise!"'' - At the beginning of the third Round in this cycle, Shane von Shane will interrupt-cast a version of [[Spell:Dr. Von's Monster (900)|Dr. Von's Monster]] that deals '''900''' Death Drain Damage to a random Wizard and returns half Health to the caster.
*''"It's a graveyard smash!"'' - At the beginning of the fourth Round in this cycle, Shane von Shane will interrupt-cast a version of [[Spell:Monster Mash (1,170)|Monster Mash]] that deals '''1,170''' Death Damage to and {{Link|Spell|Beguile}}s a random Wizard.
**This cheat repeats at the beginning of every Round until this cycle is reset.
**A wizard joining late will trigger an additional cycle starting at vampire, activating an additional cheat in the same cycle for extra wizards joining late.
'''Cycle Reset:'''
:*''"Hiss!"'' - If Shane von Shane is damaged at any point during the above interrupt cycle, then he will reset back to the start of the cycle.
::*Damage over Time effects will reset this cycle.
::*[[:Category:Steal Spells|Steal Spells]] will not reset this cycle.
::*[[:Category:Manipulation Spells|Manipulation Spells]] that have Damage-dealing effects, such as {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} and {{Link|Spell|Supernova}}, will not reset this cycle.
