| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Blade}} {{Icon|Global}} ''"Burn, Wizard!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] or [[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spell, she will cheat-cast an enhanced version of Scald, which deals '''1000''' Fire Damage over 3 rounds to all Wizards.
**Pet spells will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"I shatter your chains!"'' - If a Wizard places a [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] on the Spirit of Darkmoor, she will cheat-cast a version of {{Link|ItemCard|Assail}}, which deals '''850''' Myth Damage and places two [[Spell:Myth Trap (+35%)|+35% Myth Traps]] on the Wizard who casted a trap.
**Multi-target Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, will not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"The depths of Darkmoor strikes at you!"'' - If a Wizard defeats a Minion, she will cheat-cast a Sun version of {{Link|Spell|Eternal Leviathan (777)}} on the Wizard who defeated the Minion, which deals '''777''' Life Damage to the Wizard and restores '''777''' Health to all allies (reviving the defeated Minions).
**If another Wizard defeats a revived minion within the same round after the cheat, this cheat does not trigger.

'''Interrupt Cycles'''<br/>
All interrupt cycles are five Rounds each, and repeat until the duel is finished.
:{{Icon|Myth}} '''Myth Interrupt Cycle''' (First Cycle):
:*''"This place defeats us all!"'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, the Spirit of Darkmoor will cast Pierce on a random Wizard.
:*''"The stories of lore will defeat you!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, she will cast a {{Link|Spell|Mythspear}}, a {{Link|Spell|Mythblade (+40%)}}, {{Link|Spell|Cursed Colossus}}, and {{Link|Spell|Saga of Heroes}}.  This Round will be the end of the Myth cycle and the beginning of the Life cycle.

:{{Icon|Life}} '''Life Interrupt Cycle''' (Second Cycle):
:*''"Darkmoor holds my spirit."'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, the Spirit of Darkmoor will cast version of {{Link|Spell|Minor Blessing}} on herself for '''750''' healing.
:*''"The world hunger consumes you!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, she will cast a {{Link|Spell|Lifespear}}, a {{Link|Spell|Lifeblade}}, {{Link|Spell|Namaste}}, and {{Link|Spell|Moody Caterpillar}} on the highest threat Wizard.  This Round will be the end of the Life cycle and the beginning of the Fire cycle.

:{{Icon|Fire}} '''Fire Interrupt Cycle''' (Third Cycle):
:*''"We are connected."'' - At the end of every Round in this cycle, the Spirit of Darkmoor will cast {{Link|Spell|Backdraft}} worth the amount of Pips she has on a random Wizard.
:*''"Your passion will consume you!"'' - At the beginning of the fifth Round in this cycle, she will cast a {{Link|Spell|Firespear}}, a '''+40%''' {{Link|Spell|Fireblade}}, {{Link|Spell|Combustion}}, and {{Link|Spell|Night Raid}} to the highest-threat Wizard. This Round will be the end of the Fire cycle and the beginning of the Myth cycle.
