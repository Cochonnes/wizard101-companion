| cheatnotes =
'''Power Draconian'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Oh look, a wizard with attitude!"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle after the second Round, Power Draconian casts {{Link|Spell|Impede}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Power Equalize!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, Power Draconian will cast a Sun Global Spell called Equalize, which sets the Damage limit to '''3,000'''.
:*{{Icon|Global}} ''"Bubble Morph!"'' - If a Wizard tries to replace the Equalize Spell with another type of Global Spell, he will recast Equalize.
*{{Icon|Stun}}{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Power Stunner!"'' - At the beginning of every third Round, Power Draconian casts {{Link|Spell|Power Stunner}}, which Stuns for 1 Round and applies a -40% Accuracy Charm on all enemies.
*{{Icon|All Enemies}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Power Recharge!"'' - If Power Draconian is damaged by an [[:Category:All Enemies Spells|All Enemies]] Spell, Power Draconian casts a Shadow version of Blessing restoring 100% max Health on all allies.
**All Enemies Spells that deal Drain Damage, like {{Link|Spell|Scarecrow}}, do not trigger this cheat.

'''White Tiger'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"*GROWL*"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle after the first Round, White Tiger casts {{Link|Spell|Sabertooth}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"*WARNING ROAR*"'' - White Tiger will say this the turn before their *ROAR* cheat.
*{{Icon|All Friends}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"*ROAR*"'' - At the end of every two Rounds, White Tiger casts a Moon version of {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}} to all enemies dealing '''550''', '''1,050''' or '''1,550''' Moon Damage.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"*WHIMPER*"'' - If White Tiger is hit by an attack, their *ROAR* cheat is delayed, and the two Round cycle is reset.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"*SNARL*"'' - White Tiger will cast {{Link|Spell|Empower (Moon)}} on itself after these following triggers.
**If a negative Charm is cast on White Tiger.
**After White Tiger casts Grim Reader or Power Draconian casts Lord of the Jungle.
