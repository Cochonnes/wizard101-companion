{{Icon|Storm}} '''Krokopatra'''
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Krokopatra will cheat-cast Beguile before a Wizard attacks with an Attack-All-Enemies Spell, causing the Wizard to attack allies that Round.
:*This cheat does not activate if soloing.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"More fodder! Protect your queen!"'' If one of Krokopatra's Minions is defeated before her, she will randomly summon another Minion at the end of the next Round.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"Loose the winds of destruction!"'' She will randomly cast {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Why don't we redirect all that potential?"'' She will cheat-cast Beguile at the beginning of the Round if there is any Blade on a Wizard.  This will repeat every other Round until there are no more Blades on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"I'm crackling with rage!"'' Krokopatra will randomly cheat-cast Supercharge (+12%). Its percentage is still based on her number of Pips, however.

{{Icon|Storm}} '''Queen's Thrall (Storm) (Rank 8)'''
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Stand strong for the Queen!"''If a Wizard casts a negative Charm (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Plague}}), this Queen's Thrall will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Charm}} on the receiver of the Charm.

{{Icon|Life}} '''Queen's Thrall (Life) (Rank 8)'''
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"Our Queen needs her army!"'' Interrupt casts {{Link|Spell|Unicorn}} when any other Thralls are defeated in the same Round. Stuns do not prevent this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"We must protect ourselves to protect the Queen!"'' If a Damage over Time Spell is cast, this Minion will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Mass Triage}}.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Queen's Thrall (Death) (Rank 8)'''
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Anything for my queen!"'' Every other Round, this Queen's Thrall will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Pact (+30%)}} on Krokopatra.
:*This Thrall can defeat itself using this cheat.
