Titan's Trident is the Storm Titan's weapon and the penultimate Boss of Empyrea. In order to defeat it, Wizards must deal 20,000 Damage to it three times.
*For the Standard version of this battle, see {{Link|Creature|Titan's Trident (Standard)}}.

'''Advanced Combat Rules:'''
*Spell Decks cannot be changed while in this instance. (Make sure to set the spells in a deck before entering this instance).
*No-PvP spells are disallowed in this fight.
*PvP-Only Spells are allowed in this fight.
*Wizards cannot re-join combat after fleeing mid-combat.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Global}} On the first Round, Titan's Trident will interrupt-cast an Equalize Global which sets the Damage cap to '''949,999'''. The Trident will recast the Global when it is replaced.
*{{Icon|Late}} Joining the battle late (after Round 1) will cause the Storm Titan to interrupt-cast Glowbug Squall (Shadow, 2,250) on the Wizard, and summons a random Refraction of the following.
**{{Link|Creature|Deadly Ice Refraction}}
**{{Link|Creature|Mythic Fire Refraction}}
**{{Link|Creature|Stormy Life Refraction}}
*{{Icon|Random}} The Storm Titan will pull up the Trident and slam it down randomly, removing all unprotected Wards and Charms from itself.
*{{Icon|Random}} The Storm Titan will randomly laugh at the end of a Round, and at the end of the following Round he will shoot lasers from his eyes, dealing '''1,375''' Shadow Damage to all and removing a Shadow Pip from all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Shadow Pip}} The Storm Titan will pull up the Trident at the end of the Round that it gets its fifth Shadow Pip, which will render the Trident untargetable. While in this state, the Boss can only pass or cast Glowbug Squall (Shadow, 2,250). At the end of the third Round after it was pulled up, the Storm Titan will swing the Trident, dealing '''5,500''' Shadow Damage to all Wizards, and return it to the battle.
**While the Trident is gone from the battle, the Storm Titan can still laugh, and subsequently use his laser eye attack the following Round.
