| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Cheater!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, then Ricky will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on himself three times in a row, healing for 100, 400, and 1,000 from each respective cast.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Nice try!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell, then Ricky will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the Wizard that received the Blade.
**If a triple Blade Spell is cast, then only the last part of the Blade will be removed (Fire for {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}}s and Life for {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blade}}s).
**If an all allies Blade Spell is cast, then Ricky will only interrupt-cast Steal Charm on the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Supercharge}} does not trigger this cheat.
