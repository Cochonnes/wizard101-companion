{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Polymorph}} '''{{Link|Polymorph|Tim-tim Snakeeye (Challenge)}}''' - Elephant Polymorph Transformation

*After Polymorphing, Tim-tim Snakeeye will cast an Aura that limits incoming damage on himself to a max of 100 for 99 Rounds.
*''"An elephant never forgets, do you?"'' - At the beginning of the 2nd Round, and every 2 Rounds after, Tim-tim Snakeeye will cast 3 different Blades on himself. This indicates the order of which schools attacks he will need to be hit by. The 3 Blades will always be one of the following possibilities:
**{{Icon|Death}} {{Icon|Life}} {{Icon|Myth}}
**{{Icon|Fire}} {{Icon|Ice}} {{Icon|Storm}}
**{{Icon|Balance}} {{Icon|Myth}} {{Icon|Ice}}
**The progress made into finishing the 3 hits will not be reset when Tim-tim Snakeeye recasts the Blades every 2 Rounds.
*''"You Remembered!"'' - Tim-tim Snakeeye will say this after each successful hit in the code.
*''"Your memory is perfect!"'' - After getting the code correct, Tim-tim Snakeeye will replace his damage limit aura with {{Link|Spell|Frenzy}}.

{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Polymorph}} '''{{Link|Polymorph|Tom-tom Snakeskin (Challenge)}}''' - Rhino Polymorph Transformation

*''"This is gonna hurt!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round of this Polymorph, Tom-tom Snakeskin will cast a '''+15%''' per Pip version of {{Link|Spell|Supercharge}}, a '''+10%''' per Pip version of {{Link|Spell|Supercharge}} and a '''+35%''' {{Link|Spell|Stormblade}} on himself.
**''"Charge!"'' - At the end of the first Round, Tom-tom Snakeskin will cast a non-dispellable version of {{Link|Spell|Storm Owl (Tier 5a)}}.

{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Polymorph}} '''{{Link|Polymorph|Tik-tik Snaketongue (Challenge)}}''' - Lion Polymorph Transformation

*''"Just a little catnap..."'' - If Tik-tik Snaketongue receives any damage during this Polymorph, he will cast a version of {{Link|ItemCard|Cat Nap}} that restores 100% max health. This cheat only activates once.

{{Icon|Enemy}}{{Icon|Polymorph}} '''{{Link|Polymorph|Tse-Tse Snaketail (Challenge)}}''' - Spider Polymorph Transformation (3 {{Icon|Round}})

*At the end of each Round, Tse-Tse Snaketail will cast a unique '''+30%''' {{Link|Spell|Shadowblade}} that stacks with all of his other Shadowblades.
*''"The Shadow Queen sends her regards!"'' - At the end of the third Round of this Polymorph, Tse-Tse Snaketail will cast {{Link|Spell|Dark Fiend}} and a version of {{Link|Spell|Sacrifice}} on himself that deals 100% damage and restores no health, defeating him.
