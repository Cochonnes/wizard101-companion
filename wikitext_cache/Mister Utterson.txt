| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Spell}} '''Mister Utterson'''
*''"Time to burn it to the ground."'' - If Mister Poole regularly casts {{Link|Spell|Fuel}} on a Wizard, then Mister Utterson will interrupt-cast a -35% version of Legion Shield.
*''"I'll follow your lead"'' - If Mister Poole regularly casts {{Link|Spell|Fire Trap}} on a Wizard, then Mister Utterson will interrupt-cast a +35% version of Ice Trap on the same Wizard.
*''"We'll have hard words indeed!"'' - If Mister Poole regularly casts his Natural Attack on a Wizard, then Mister Utterson will interrupt-cast his {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack}} on the same Wizard.
**If this cheat triggers while Mister Utterson has no Pips, then his Natural Attack will act as though he has one Pip.

{{Icon|Spell}} '''Mister Poole'''
*''"I'll match you, sir."'' - If Mister Utterson casts Iceblade, then Mister Poole will interrupt-cast a '''+40%''' {{Link|Spell|Fireblade}} on himself.
*''"I'll follow your lead"'' - If Mister Utterson regularly casts Ice Trap on a Wizard, then Mister Poole will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Fire Trap (+35%)}} on the same Wizard.
*''"Time to armor up!"'' - If Mister Utterson regularly casts Legion Shield, then Mister Utterson will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Fuel (+35%)}} on a random Wizard.
*''"Let's have some hard words!"'' - If Mister Utterson regularly casts his Natural Attack on a Wizard, then Mister Poole will interrupt-cast his Natural Attack on the same Wizard.
**If this cheat triggers while Mister Poole has no Pips, then his Natural Attack will act as though he has one Pip.
