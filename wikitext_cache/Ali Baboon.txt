*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You are late. How embarrassing"'' - If a Wizard is late to combat, he will interrupt-cast a Cloaked version of a negative Charm on all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Now, you made Ali Baboon angry"'' - At the beginning of Round 1, he will cast a Sun Spell that summons one of the following {{Link|Creature|Gobblorean Guard}} Minions:
::Gobblorean Guard (Balance)
::Gobblorean Guard (Death)
::Gobblorean Guard (Storm)
:*After defeating his first minion, he will cast a '''+45% in / +35% out''' version of {{Link|ItemCard|Berserk}}. Berserk is only cast the first time a minion is defeated.
:*At the start of every Round that Ali doesn't have a minion he will resummon another one of a random type.
*{{Icon|Absorb}}{{Icon|Heal}}''"Pick on Ali Baboon, not his men!"'' - If a Gobblorean Guard is attacked, he will cast an Aegis-protected {{Link|Spell|Mander Armor}} and {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on the Minion.
:*Hitting Ali with Spells that have an all enemies secondary effect, such as {{Link|Spell|Winged Sorrow}}, will still activate this cheat.
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Here, consider this a gift"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, he will interrupt-cast a Cloaked version of a negative Charm on all Wizards.
**Spell used to be a '''-55%''' version of Smoke Screen, but now the spell may be bugged.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Such tricks won't work on me"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} is placed on Ali Baboon, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)}} to remove all Traps from himself.
** Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} will not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent removal.
