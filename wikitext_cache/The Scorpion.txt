The Scorpion, also known as {{Link|NPC|Overlord Xerxes}}, is one of [[NPC:Old Cob|Grandfather Spider's]] children. He is the second born child, and brother to {{Link|Creature|The Rat}}.
*If a Wizard flees this duel and returns, then [[NPC:Ozzy (Mirage)|Ozzy's]]  ''5% Armor-Piercing bonus'' will be lost.

| speech =
:''"That reanimated corpse was just a ruse. I am Scorpion, son of Spider, and brother of the Rat, who I will avenge today upon this meddling Wizard!"''

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Oh, yes...you will fall, too."'' - If a Wizard is late to join the duel, starting on the third Round The Scorpion will cheat-cast Bad Juju (Shadow) on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"I invoke the power of Shadow!"'' - At the end of every third Round, he will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Poison (Shadow)}} to all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Now... your doom!"'' - At the end of every Round, he will cheat-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Detonate}} to all Wizards, as long as one Wizard has a DoT.
:*The HoT portion of Healing Spells, such as from {{Link|PetAbility|Spritely}}, can prevent a DoT from detonating when the HoT is positioned before the DoT. The HoT does not get removed by the Detonate.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"The Rat warned me of that!"'' - If a Wizard places a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on The Scorpion, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Shadow, -All)}} to remove all unprotected Traps from himself.
:*{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to avoid this cheat.
:*The Balance version of {{Link|ItemCard|Feint}} does not trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"I planned this special for you!"'' - If a Wizard uses a Healing Spell, he will cast his Natural Attack (Shadow 75+1430) at the Wizard.
:*The HoT portion of Healing Spells, such as from {{Link|PetAbility|Spritely}}, will not trigger this cheat.
:*''Maycast'' Pet Heals only seem to occasionally trigger this cheat.
