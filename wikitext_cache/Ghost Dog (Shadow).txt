| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Devote your body and soul to your master!"'' - At the end of the first Round, Ghost Dog will summon two Hagakure Blades Minions and one Seijun Killer Minion.  They become active during the second Round.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Feel the poetry of war!"'' - At the end of the second Round, and every five Rounds thereafter, Ghost Dog will interrupt-cast a Damage over Time Spell that deals 400 Death Damage per Round for ten Rounds, or 575 Death Damage per Round in Shadow form.
**When this cheat is triggered, Ghost Dog will target the Wizard with the most generated threat, or the Wizard in the first position (Sun spot) of the Duel by default.
**{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"The way of the Samoorai is found in death!"'' - At the end of the fifth Round, and every five Rounds thereafter, Ghost Dog will interrupt-cast a Death version of [[Spell:Detonate (Death)|Detonate]] on the Wizard who received the Damage over Time effect, dealing 2,800 Death Damage.
***If this cheat is Dispelled, then Ghost Dog will interrupt-cast Detonate again to hit his target.
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"Be resolved from the beginning!"'' - If a Wizard casts a helpful [[:Category:Ward Spells|Ward]], then Ghost Dog will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Shatter}} on the Wizard that cast the Ward.
** Pet spells do not trigger this cheat.
