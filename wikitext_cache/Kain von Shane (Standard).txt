| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"I am Lord Kain von Shane and I deserve dignity!"'' - If a feint is cast on Kain von Shane, he will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Dark Tribute}} on himself. This is an old version of Dark Pact that removes universal traps and applies a +30% balance blade on Shane.
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"I will not bow down to you!"'' - If a harmful Charm is cast on Kain von Shane, then he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the wizard who cast the Charm.
*{{Icon|Steal}} ''"Don't drain me!"'' - If a Drain spell is cast on Kain von Shane, he will cast an Aegis-protected +100% Balanceblade on himself.
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"More life for me!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, then Kain von Shane will interrupt-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Vampire}} that deals Death Drain Damage and returns half Health to the caster.
**Pet Maycast Heals will trigger this cheat.
**This cheat will not trigger for [[Spell:Monster Mash (1,170)|Beguiled]] Wizards.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Who let the sun in here?"'' - If a Wizard casts as spell that has a sun in it (such as {{Link|Spell|Power Nova}},) Kain von Shane will cast a Protected Universal Dispel on himself.
'''Interrupt Cycle''': This cycle begins on the second round and repeats every 3 rounds until it is reset.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Build my strength..."'' - At the beginning of the first round in this cycle, Kain von Shane will interrupt-cast a Star version of {{Link|Spell|Supercharge}} that applies a +10% per pip universal damage charm on himself and gives himself a pip.
*{{Icon|Dispel}} ''"...stun my prey..."'' - At the beginning of the second round in this cycle, Kain von Shane will interrupt-cast a Star spell that applies a Dispel to every wizard of their own school.
*{{Icon|Friend}}{{Icon|Damage}} ''"...and now the strike!"'' - At the beginning of the third round in this cycle, Kain von Shane will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Monster Mash}} on a random wizard.
'''Cycle Reset:'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"*hiss* Where was I?"'' - If Kain von Shane is targeted with an attack spell, then his cheat cycle will pause for one round and reset to the beginning.
**{{Icon|Damage over Time}} Damage over time spells will only reset his cheat cycle on the turn they are cast.
