| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You are late for your trial!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the first Round, he will interrupt-cast '''Grim Grrnadier (Tier 3b)''' on the late Wizard.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"These have no place in my court"'' - If a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} or {{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} is cast on Judge Veg, he will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (Star, -All)}} to remove all negative Wards from himself.
*{{Icon|Polymorph}} ''"If you ate more carrots, you'd see this coming"'' - At the end of the first Round, Judge Veg will Polymorph a random Wizard into one of the following Vegetable forms for 15 Rounds:
**{{Link|Polymorph|Life Vegetable}}
**{{Link|Polymorph|Storm Vegetable}}
**{{Link|Polymorph|Death Vegetable}}
**{{Link|Polymorph|Ice Vegetable}}

{{Icon|Polymorph}} Judge Veg will Polymorph one random Wizard into a random Vegetable based on the number of Wizards in combat:
*1 Wizard: ''"If you ate more carrots, you'd see this coming"'' - Every 9th Round.
*2 Wizards: ''"You should've eaten your broccoli"'' - Every 7th Round.
*3 Wizards: ''"Time for a balanced meal!"'' - Every 5th Round.
*4 Wizards: ''"I am the slaw!"'' - Every 3rd Round.

{{Icon|Spell}} Judge Veg also has unique lines for when he casts certain Spells:
*''"Bringing down the Veghammer"'' - Judge Veg will use his turn to cast '''Lamassu''' on the Wizard with the least Health.
*''"As the World Turnips!"'' - Judge Veg will use his turn to cast '''Wings of Night'''.
*''"Green Beanzooka!"'' - Judge Veg will use his turn to cast '''Grim Grrnadier (Tier 3b)''' on the Wizard with the most Health.
*''"I find you in contempt of corn"'' - Judge Veg will use his turn to cast '''Hungry Caterpillar''' on a random wizard (needs confirmation).
*Note: Some of these lines may occur at the same time, but only one Spell will be casted.
