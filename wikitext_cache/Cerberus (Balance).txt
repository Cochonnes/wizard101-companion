In their attempt to get to [[Creature:Hades the Unseen (Tartarus)|Hades]], Wizards have to find a way to deal with this mutt. After putting Cerberus to sleep, the heads spawn as spirits detached from the body to prevent Wizards from reaching his master.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Shield}} At the beginning of the first Round and every 5th Round afterward, The Balance and Myth heads of Cerberus will remove all Wards on themself and cast three '''-40%''' {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}}s.
**If a head of Cerberus has no helpful Wards, a head with a helpful Ward may cast a Spell to push all helpful Wards to that head.
**Defeating the Fire head of Cerberus will disable this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Guard me!"'' - If a Wizard attacks a head of Cerberus, each other head will cast their Natural Attack on that Wizard.
**Even though this Spell is not cast, it can be Dispelled.
**Casting {{Link|Spell|Beguile}} will trigger the targeted head to use its Natural Attack on the caster twice.
**Multi-target Damage Spells will trigger this cheat once for each head of Cerberus attacked.
**Steal Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Vampire}}, do not trigger this cheat.
**If a head of Cerberus is Beguiled and is Damaged by another head, it will use this cheat on the casting head.
*{{Icon|Trap}} If a Trap is placed on the Balance or Myth head, it will cast {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} to remove it.
**{{Link|Spell|Vaporize}} can be used to prevent this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to avoid the removal of Traps once.
**Only the final Trap will be removed from Multi-Trap spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Elemental Trap}}.
**Multi-Target Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, will not activate this cheat.
**Pet spells will not activate this cheat.
