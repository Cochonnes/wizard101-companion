| cheatnotes =
'''Ervin the Barbarian'''
*{{Icon|Shield}} At the start of the battle, Ervin cheat-casts a '''{{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (-90%)}}''' on himself. If removed by Damage, he cheat-casts it again as long as at least one Minion is alive.
**Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Steal Ward}} or {{Link|Spell|Pierce}} can be used to safely remove the shield, but he will still replace it when he takes Damage.
**Ervin will still cast the shield on the first Round even if all of the Minions are missing due to being defeated in a prior attempt.
*{{Icon|Death}} ''"Hah! Your miss leaves you open!"'' - Whenever a Wizard fizzles, Ervin cheat-casts a 0-Pip Skeletal Dragon on the Wizard who fizzled, dealing '''800 + 1,998''' Death Damage over 3 Rounds.
*{{Icon|Storm}} ''"I needed those thieves!"'' - After all of the Minions are defeated and repeating every second round, Ervin cheat-casts a 0-Pip Leviathan at the start of the round that deals '''2,030''' Storm Damage to the highest threat Wizard.
**Although this cheat is not cast, it may still be dispelled with {{Link|Spell|Dissipate}}.
**If all of the Minions were defeated in a prior attempt, this cheat will first trigger on the third Round (although the line is still spoken without any associated spellcast on the first Round).
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} If a Trap is placed on Ervin while any of the Minions are alive, then he will cast Cleanse Ward on himself.
**{{Link|Spell|Vaporize}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
**For {{Link|Spell|Elemental Trap}} and {{Link|Spell|Spirit Trap}}, only the last Trap of the three (Storm, Life) is removed.
**Multi-Trap Spells (such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}} or {{Link|Spell|Mass Death Prism}}) do not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to avoid this cheat.

'''Taurus the Clever'''
*{{Icon|Curse}} At the start of every third round, Taurus cheat-casts '''{{Link|Spell|Smoke Screen (-50%)}}''', making it easier for Ervin to use Skeletal Dragon.
**{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"This would look better on Ervin."'' - If a Wizard casts any Charm, Taurus will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the recipient and give a Deathblade to Ervin the Barbarian. If Ervin the Barbarian is defeated, Taurus will still cast Disarm without casting Deathblade.
**Dissipate can be used to prevent this cheat. If Disarm is dispelled, Taurus will not give Ervin a Deathblade.
**For {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}} and {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blade}}, only the first Blade of the three (Fire, Life) is removed.
**For multi-Charms like {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}, only the caster's Charm is removed.
**Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Plague}} and {{Link|Spell|Smoke Screen}} activate this cheat and Taurus will cast Disarm on the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Aegis}} can be used to avoid this cheat.
**Pet spells do not trigger this cheat.
