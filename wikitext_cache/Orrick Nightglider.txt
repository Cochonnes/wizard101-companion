| cheatnotes =
:''"Interrupt!"'' - This will appear during most of Orrick's cheats.
*{{Icon|Ward}} If a Trap is placed on Orrick, he will use {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} on himself to remove one Trap on himself.
**Prisms and mass Traps, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, do not trigger this cheat.
**This cheat can be [[Spell:Vaporize|Dispelled]] or prevented with {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}}.
*{{Icon|Round}} Every 3 Rounds, while Orrick's health is above 4,000, he will interrupt-cast a {{Link|Spell|Heck Hound}} on the Wizard dealing '''1,820''' Fire Damage over 3 Rounds. This is the greatest threat in this battle. This cheat can be [[Spell:Quench|Dispelled]].
**This Heck Hound will still be cast on a Wizard even if Orrick is Beguiled.
*{{Icon|Round}} Starting on Round 4, Orrick will interrupt with {{Link|Spell|Meteor Strike}} dealing '''750''' Fire Damage.
**This cheat can be [[Spell:Quench|Dispelled]].
**{{Link|Spell|Beguile}} can delay this cheat by one round when he has no allies.
'''Under 4,000 Health:'''
*{{Icon|Minion}} When Orrick's health drops below 4,000 during a round, then three Stompers join the duel at the end of the round and become active 2 rounds later.
**If Wizards flee and return, the Stompers will still remain.
*{{Icon|Damage}} When Orrick's health drops below 4,000, he will use Meteor Strike (750) at the start of every 3 Rounds once the current four round cycle completes.
*{{Icon|Ward}} When Orrick's health is between 4,000 and 2,000, he will interrupt to use a '''-65%''' {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on himself whenever he is damaged.
**[[:Category:Steal Spells|Drain Spells]] do not trigger this cheat.
**{{Icon|Damage}} If Orrick is hit with a multi-hit spell, he will use Tower Shield (-65%) on himself for every hit.
***This also includes Damage over Times, which will be cast after his turn ends.
'''Under 2,000 Health:'''
*{{Icon|Damage}} At the start of the round Orrick's health drops below 2,000, Orrick will interrupt with an {{Link|Spell|Earthquake}} that only deals '''750''' Myth Damage. Spell doesn't remove charms or wards. This cheat can be [[Spell:Vaporize|Dispelled]].
**{{Icon|Round}} In addition every 3 Rounds, while Orrick's health is below 2,000, he will interrupt-cast a Heck Hound on the Wizard dealing '''1,820''' Fire Damage over 3 Rounds.

:Orrick will not reactivate any of his previous health threshold cheats if Orrick heals himself via Life Drain spells.
