| descrip =
Lucia is the Cuckoo of Valencia's Great Clock, crafted by Master Gazpaccio to keep track of the time by singing at the arrival of every hour.  Like all of his creations, Gazpaccio magically imbued Lucia with emotions, granting her a sense of pride which serves as her drive to perform precisely on the hour.  After years of impeccable timekeeping, Lucia's pride has warped into vanity, and she now sings every few minutes, throwing the Great Clock and Valencian timekeeping into disarray.  After Wizards assist {{Link|NPC|Bernardo Dondi}}, Gazpaccio's Apprentice, with tuning the Great Clock's Core Mechanism, they ascend the to the Cuckoo's Nest to humble the Prima Cuckoo.

* An additional [[Creature:Clockworker (Tier 2)|Clockworker]] will appear if four Wizards are present.

*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"This is my show! MINE!"'' - At the start of every fourth Round, Lucia will interrupt-cast a Star version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} that steals all helpful Charms from all Wizards, and a Star version of [[Spell:Steal Ward (Star, -All)|Steal Ward]] that steals all helpful Wards from all Wizards.
