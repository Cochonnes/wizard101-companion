Malistaire Drake is the final Boss of Dragonspyre as well as the final Boss of the First Arc. He aims to summon the Dragon Titan to resurrect his fallen wife, {{Link|NPC|Sylvia Drake}}, by stealing the Krokonomicon from the {{Link|Location|Royal Museum}} without realizing that the Dragon Titan could destroy the Spiral.

:'''Malistaire:''' ''"My brother Cyrus ... you have apparently lost faith in my abilities. How unfortunate... and how blind of you.
