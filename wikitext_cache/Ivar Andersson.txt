Ivar Andersson is the Thane of {{Link|Location|Ander's Holt}}, a small village in the Vigrid Roughlands that was named after Ivar's father.  Recently, and for reasons unknown, he betrayed his people by handing over his village to the Red Claw, a band of bloodthirsty raiders that drove out the villagers of Ander's Holt and established their main fortress in Frostholm.  In helping {{Link|NPC|Egil Axebearer}} care for the village's refugees, Wizards met an elder named {{Link|NPC|Grimnir}} who, having known the Thane since Ivar was a cub, was convinced that the honorable son of Ander would never have willingly given his village over to a band of raiders, certain that he must be under some dark enchantment.  After bartering with the Grendel {{Link|NPC|Bolthorn}} and acquiring a magical artifact called the Eye of Truth, Wizards finally make their way to Frostholm, infiltrating the Red Claw until finally coming face to face with Ivar Andersson to discover the truth behind his actions.
