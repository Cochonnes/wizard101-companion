'''Buffs:'''
*During this fight a Wizard can cast {{Link|Spell|Shadow Surge}} and {{Link|Spell|Retribution}} during this battle.
*Additionally, Wizards can use all previously available inspired casts spells including: {{Link|Spell|Brush Fire}}, {{Link|Spell|Dismal Seraph}}, {{Link|Spell|Fabled Revenant}}, {{Link|Spell|Levin Bruin}}, {{Link|Spell|Rotting Fomori}}, {{Link|Spell|War Oracle}}, and {{Link|Spell|Monumental (All Allies)}} which gives all Wizards one {{Link|Spell|Monumental}} card.
*Inspire Cantrips may also be cast into this fight by anyone who hasn't joined after a player initiates the fight.

'''The Nullity:'''
*At the beginning of the first Round, The Nullity will cast a Shadow Spell that forces all Wizards to pass.
*At the end of every Round, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Power Current}} on itself.

The Nullity will announce a School at the beginning of the battle and will choose another School every three Rounds. The new School will be announced after the fourth turn begins. These Schools conform to Roshambo rules and govern what Charms, Wards, Heal- or Damage over Time Spells may be used. At the end of the third Round, The Nullity will cast an all enemies Power Nova version of the Scion Spells that benefits based on the chosen School.

Schools are announced in text as follows:
*''"The Nullity is Fired Up"'' - Fire Phase: {{Icon|Fire}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Fire Elf}} on a wizard.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Scald}} on all wizards.
***The Scion is powered up if two or more Damage over Time effects are on a Wizard.

*''"The Nullity Keeps Its Cool"'' - Ice Phase: {{Icon|Ice}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Tower Shield}} on itself.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Shield Wall}} on itself.
***The Scion is powered up if four or more positive Wards are on the Nullity.

*''"The Nullity is Electrifying"'' - Storm Phase: {{Icon|Storm}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Star Blade}} on itself.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Guiding Light}} (x3) on itself.
***The Scion is powered up if four or more positive Charms are on the Nullity.

*''"The Nullity Ascends to Legend"'' - Myth Phase: {{Icon|Myth}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Mythic Fuel}} on a wizard.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Mass Myth Trap}} on all wizards.
***The Scion is powered up if four or more negative Wards are on a Wizard.

*''"The Nullity Feels Alive"'' - Life Phase: {{Icon|Life}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Sprite}} on itself.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Sprite Swarm}} on all allies.
***The Scion is powered up if two or more Heal over Time effects are on the Nullity.

*''"The Nullity Foresees your Doom"'' - Death Phase: {{Icon|Death}}
**At the end of the first turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Threefold Fever}} on a wizard.
**At the end of the second turn of this cycle, The Nullity will cast {{Link|Spell|Mass Infection}} on all wizards.
***The Scion is powered up if four or more negative Charms are on a Wizard.

The Nullity is joined in battle by an elite Minion from a random School, both of which also follow Roshambo rules to counter Wizard Spells.  Fire counters positive and negative wards.  Ice counters positive and negative Charms.  Storm counters Damage over Time and Heal over Time effects.  Myth counters negative Charms and positive Wards.  Death counters positive Charms and Heal over Time effects.  Life counters negative Wards and Damage over Time effects.  Balance counters when over nine Pips.

This battle uses snake turn order (Beastmoon).
