| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Who misses Malistaire? I do!"'' - At the beginning of every second Round, Jake the Smoke Monster will cast Shift Malistaire on a random Wizard, dealing 85 Death Damage per Pip.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"In my version... Xibalba hit Celestia!"'' - If a Wizard casts an Aura, Jake the Smoke Monster will cast {{Link|ItemCard|Mass Supernova}}.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Everything Old is mine now!"'' - Jake the Smoke Monster will summon a Spiderling Minion.
*{{Icon|Minion}} ''"Behold my originality!"'' - If no Minions are left, Jake the Smoke Monster will summon another Jake the Smoke Monster (Minion).
*{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Electromagnetism!"'' - If a Wizard casts a harmful Charm, Jake the Smoke Monster will cast a Moon version of Insane Bolt, dealing either '''550''', '''1,050''', or '''1,550''' Damage to all enemies.
**Spells that apply harmful Charms as a secondary effect will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Boom! Morganthe is back!"'' - After an unknown number of turns, Jake the Smoke Monster will cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Death Morganthe Attack}} that deals 100% of the Wizard's Health.

{{Icon|Blade}}{{Icon|Global}}{{Icon|Trap}} '''When 2 or more Wizards are present'''
*''"Ooooh mystery!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Blade, Global, or a Trap Spell, Jake the Smoke Monster will respond with this.
*''"I dont like themes!"'' - If another Wizard casts a second Blade Spell, Global, or Trap on the same turn:
**Second Blade and/or Global Spell: Jake the Smoke Monster will use an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} that removes all Blades from all enemies.
**Second Trap Spell: Jake the Smoke Monster will use a Fire version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} that removes all Traps from all allies.
**Note: Both a Blade/Global and a Trap can be cast in the same turn. The cheat only triggers if two of the ''same'' hanging effects are cast.
