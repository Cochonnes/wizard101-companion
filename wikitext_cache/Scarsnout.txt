Scarsnout is the dim and short-tempered leader of a group of Boars that has allied with Red Claw.  While working to be allowed to see Ivar Andersson, Wizards are sent by {{Link|NPC|Gunnar Quickaxe}} to present to Scarsnout a set of golden arm bands, meant to symbolize that the Red Claw views Scarsnout and his boars as equals.  {{Link|NPC|Ulric Oathbreaker}}, an inside-Bear who is helping Wizards sabotage the Red Claw, devises a plan to instead give Scarsnout a flask of blueberry juice, knowing that he will take it as a serious insult.  Ulric's plan is successful, prompting Scarsnout to attack Wizards in protest.

Two additional Splithoof Bloodguards will appear if more than two Wizards are present.  These additional Splithoof Bloodguards will already be wandering the area prior to talking to {{Link|NPC|Scarsnout}} and can be defeated before having to duel him.
