| descrip =
Taffy is one of three Mongu warrior brothers encountered in the Rubal Wastes - the others being Ricky and Tiki. Taffy is the eldest of the three brothers.

| speech =
:''"Oh yes! How delightful! A new, tasty morsel to slice up with my blades. I would take you myself, but my brothers clearly need more practice. You will find this battle considerably more complex than the last two. Approach and take a bite of Taffy!"''

| cheatnotes =
'''Taffy'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"The more the merrier!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, Taffy will place an {{Link|Spell|Aegis}} version of Tower Shield (-55%) on Ricky and Tiki, and an Aegis version of Frozen Armor (185) on himself.
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"One dirty trick deserves another!"'' - If a Wizard places a [[:Category:Trap Spells|Trap]] on any of the brothers, he will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} to remove 1 Trap from the target.
:*{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent the removal of a particular Trap.
:*Spells that leave Traps behind, such as {{Link|Spell|Fire from Above}}, will trigger this cheat.
:*Using {{Link|Spell|Mass Feint (+75%)}} will cause Taffy to remove the Trap placed on the caster. This will not remove any of the Traps on the Bosses.
:*Spells like {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}} will not trigger this cheat.

'''Ricky'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Cheater!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, Ricky will triple-cast {{Link|Spell|Healing Current}} on himself, giving 100, 400, then 1,000 health.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Nice try!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell, then Ricky will cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} on the Wizard that received the Blade.
:*{{Link|Spell|Quench}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
:*Aegis can be used to prevent removal.
:*If a tri-Blade Spell is used, then only the last part of the Blade will be removed (Fire for {{Link|Spell|Elemental Blade}}s and Life for {{Link|Spell|Spirit Blade}}s).
:*If a multi-target Blade Spell is used (e.g. {{Link|Spell|Bladestorm}}), only the Wizard who cast the Spell will be affected.
:*Steal Charm can be used to steal back the Blade without a penalty.
:*{{Link|Spell|Supercharge}} does not trigger this cheat.

'''Tiki'''
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You should be ashamed!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after it has already begun, Tiki will cast {{Link|Spell|Guiding Light}} on himself and Ricky and an Aegis-Protected {{Link|Spell|Spirit Armor}} on Taffy.
*{{Icon|Damage over Time}} ''"Let's spice things up!"'' - If a DoT is cast on anyone in the duel, he will remove it.
:*Pet Spells will trigger this cheat.
