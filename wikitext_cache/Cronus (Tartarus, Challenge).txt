| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Trap}} At the beginning of every Round, Cronus will cast a Sun Spell that applies a '''+40%''' Trap of a random School on all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"For every shield, I have a trap!"'' - Whenever a Wizard casts a Shield, Cronus will cast a Sun Spell on the Wizard that applies a '''+40%''' Trap of a random School.
*{{Icon|Jinx}} If a harmful Ward is cast on Cronus, he will remove it.
**If a Multi-Trap Spell such as {{Link|Spell|Elemental Trap}} is cast on Cronus, he will only remove one Ward, and Cronus will place a '''Marked''' Stun Block on himself. After Cronus is attacked while he has a '''Marked''' effect, he will remove the Mark and all harmful Wards from himself.
**All Enemies harmful Ward Spells such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}} will cause the Ward to be removed from Cronus only.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Attacks alone will never hurt me!"'' - If Cronus is attacked when he doesn't have a '''Marked''' effect, Cronus will cast a version of his Natural Attack based on the School of the Spell that hit him.
**Fire Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Storm)}}
**Ice Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Fire)}}
**Storm Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Ice)}}
**Life Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Death)}}
**Myth Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Life)}}
**Death Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Myth)}}
**Balance or Shadow Spell -> {{Link|Spell|Natural Attack (Cronus Balance)}}
**Damage Spells that hit multiple times such as {{Link|Spell|Minotaur}} will trigger this cheat multiple times.
**Damage over Time Spells will only trigger this cheat on their initial hit.
