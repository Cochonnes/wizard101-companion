Nekhbet is one of the reawakened Tuts, residing in one of the chambers found in the [[Location:Krokosphinx|Krokosphinx's]] Hall of Champions. When Wizards arrive in the hall in search of {{Link|NPC|General Khaba}}, the Mander {{Link|NPC|Rami}} informs them that the missing general will only work with those bearing the Seal of the Fang, which marks one as an ally of the Order of the Fang.  Unfortunately, Nekhbet was able to steal one prior to the completion of the Great Sleep's casting.  Wizards face Nekhbet to recover the seal, both to gain an audience with General Khaba and to prevent the Tuts from luring him into a trap.

*Two additional Sokkwi Protectors will appear if more than two Wizards are present.
