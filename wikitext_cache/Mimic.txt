The Mimic is a Polymorphing Boss that changes its School of magic at the beginning of every third Round.<br/>Incoming boost/resist is based on the current Polymorphed School.<br/>This Boss is not to be confused with the Stone Skeleton Key Boss "{{Link|Creature|Verboten Mimic}}".
