| cheatnotes =
*''"More Wizards sprouting? Such a tree thing to do"'' If a Wizard joins the battle after the first Round, Treemugger casts {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} on the late Wizard.
*''"I will break this, with my rage!"'' - At the start of the duel, Treemugger will use a Shadow Spell to Remove all Pips from all Wizards, impose a 99-Round version of '''{{Link|Spell|Impede}}''', and use {{Link|Spell|Blinding Light (85%)}}.
*''"One-two PUNCH!"'' - At the end of every Odd Round, Treemugger will say this.
*''"Three-four PUNCH!"'' - At the end of every Even Round, Treemugger will say this.
**In both these stages he will remove all existing Pips and use individual Donate Power Spells to sequentially increase the number of Pips based on the number of Rounds which have elapsed.
**For example, on the first Round, each Wizard is given one white Pip; second Round, two white Pips, etc., until each Wizard is given seven Power Pips.
**During the stage when Power Pips are given, even though all Pips get removed during the animation, the single pips return back to the Wizard when the next Round starts.
*''"Make like a tree and fall down!"'' - If a Wizard doesn't have an '''Impede''' active on them and any Wizard in the fight casts any spell, Treemugger will cast the 99-Round '''Impede''' on all Wizards without an Impede on them.
**Replacing the Aura will allow the Wizard to regain Pips, until any Wizard casts another spell, repeating this cheat.
