Krampus is a Boss that appears during the Yuletide event.  He is located in Krampusgarten, which requires a {{Link|House|Gold Skeleton Key}} to enter.
*Minions can be any combination of {{Link|Creature|Chilling Helper (Tier 2)}} and {{Link|Creature|Stormy Helper (Tier 2)}}.
*This Tier is for level 51-109 Wizards.

| speech =
:''"Naughty, naughty. Krampus sees all!"''

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}}''"Naughty, naughty! Punctuality is a virtue!"'' - If a Wizard joins after the duel has started, Krampus will cheat-cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Scald}} dealing Fire Damage and a '''-75%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Smoke Mantle}} on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Blade}}''"A gift for Krampus? You shouldn't have!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]] Spell, he may cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} that steals all Charms from the Wizard who cast the Blade.
**He will occasionally cast this throughout the battle.
**[[:Category:Global Spells|Global]] Spells will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Feint}} ''"Naughty, naughty! Krampus sees all!"'' - If a Wizard casts a {{Link|Spell|Feint}} Spell, he will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} twice (which makes {{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} ineffective).
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Krampus is everywhere!""'' -  At the beginning of every third Round, he will cast a Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Dimension Shift}} that transfers all negative hanging effects that are on him (i.e. [[:Category:Trap Spells|Traps]]) to a random friend or foe.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"'Tis the season of Krampus!"'' - At the beginning of Round 5, he will summon any combination of Chilling Helper (Rank 10) and Stormy Helper (Rank 10).
**Whenever a Minion is defeated, he will resummon it after one Round.
