*{{Icon|Late}}''"You're Late, or Are You Early?"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle late, Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will use {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} that will last two turns.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will cast a Mass Shadow version of Empower on itself and its allies to gain 1 Power Pip and hidden-casts a Spell that switches the first turn of the battle.
*{{Icon|Feint}} If a Feint is placed on Time Butterfly (Tier 4), then it will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} to switch the Traps with the Wizard who cast it.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} will not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent this cheat.

{{Icon|Minion}} '''Minions:'''
*Time Butterfly (Tier 4) will summon Minions starting with {{Link|Creature|Young Morganthe (Tier 4)}} on the first turn. If Young Morganthe (Tier 4) is defeated before Time Butterfly (Tier 4), both Young Morganthe (Tier 4) and {{Link|Creature|Roberto (Tier 4)}} will be summoned on the following turn. If Young Morganthe (Tier 4) and Roberto (Tier 4) are defeated, Time Butterfly (Tier 4) will resummon both of them, and also {{Link|Creature|Broodmother (Tier 4)}} on the following turn.

{{Icon|Death}} '''Young Morganthe:'''
