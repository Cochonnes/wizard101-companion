'''Captain Hockins'''
*{{Icon|Charm}} ''"I won't go down easy!"'' - When hit once, Hockins gives the Wizard who attacked a {{Link|Spell|Dragonspear}}.
**''"Still got a few tricks up my sleeve!"'' - When hit a second time, Hockins gives the Wizard who attacked a  '''+20%''' Dragonspear.
**''"I'll shake this curse yet!"'' - When hit a third time, Hockins gives the Wizard who attacked a  '''+30%''' Dragonspear.
***The speech recycles after Hockins is hit for a fourth time, but the Dragonspears continue to gain +10% Armor Piercing with each attack up to +100% maximum.
***Damage over Time Spells will not trigger the Dragonspear cheats.

*{{Icon|Global}} ''"Let's keep things under control"'' - At start of the Duel, the Witch Doctor interrupt casts a version of {{Link|Spell|Saga of Heroes}} which lowers a Wizard's critical chance by -75.

*{{Icon|Late}} ''"A curse on the un-punctual!"'' - If a Wizard joins late, then the Witch Doctor will cast {{Link|Spell|Steal Pips (Moon)}} that steals 1 Pip.
