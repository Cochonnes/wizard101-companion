| descrip =
To spawn the boss, a player must find a Pumpkin Head in the Howling Lands and bring it to the Covered Bridge for each encounter. Only 1 Pumpkin Head will spawn per realm. After placing the Pumpkin Head next to the bridge, the Headless Horseman will charge across the map. A player must stand in the way of where the Horseman will run across to begin the battle. If no players are in its path, it will despawn once he reaches the other side of the enclosed area.
