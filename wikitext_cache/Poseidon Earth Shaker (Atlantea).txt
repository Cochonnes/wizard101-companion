Poseidon Earth Shaker is the final Boss of Atlantea. Defeating him is the final step in completing the second trial of the Immortal Games, and becoming the [[Basic:Aquila Badges| Valorous Silver Archon]].
*For the Challenge version of this battle, see {{Link|Creature|Poseidon Earth Shaker (Atlantea, Challenge)}}.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Aura}}''"We immortals are infallible!"'' - During the first Round, and any other Round when he does not have an Aura, Poseidon will use his turn to cast Infallible.
*{{Icon|Curse}}/{{Icon|Jinx}}''"You seek to weaken me? Drink from the depths!"'' - If any single-target Negative Charm, Traps, or Prism is cast on Poseidon, he will use his Natural Attack, which removes all Traps that were placed on him.
**Multi-target Spells, such as {{Link|Spell|Windstorm}}, {{Link|Spell|Mass Storm Prism}}, or others, do not trigger this cheat.
**Spells with single-target secondary effects will trigger this cheat.
