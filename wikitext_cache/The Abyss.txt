| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You are tardy!"'' - If a Wizard joins after the battle has started, The Abyss casts a protected -75% version of Mass Infection.
*''"The Abyss fears no attack!"'' - At the start of the first Round:
**The Abyss casts {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}
**The Abyss casts a Shadow version of Guardian Spirit. (Unknown Effect)
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"The Abyss has a secret for you..."'' - At the beginning of every 2nd Round, The Abyss will Beguile a Wizard for 2 Rounds:
**Round 2: Beguiles Slot 1 {{Target|Sun}}
**Round 4: Beguiles Slot 2 {{Target|Eye}}
**Round 6: Beguiles Slot 3 {{Target|Star}}
**Round 8: Beguiles Slot 4 {{Target|Moon}}
**This cycle repeats, so Sun will be beguiled again on Round 10, Eye on Round 12, Star on Round 14, and Moon on Round 16, and so on.
**If a slot that would be targeted on a specific Round is empty, then The Abyss will skip this cheat.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"The Abyss will defend itself"'' - At the beginning of every 3rd Round, The Abyss will use a Shadow Spell that applies a -80% Healing Shield on self
**Though this hanging effect has the appearance of a shield, {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward}} can be used to remove it
*{{Icon|Global}} ''"The Abyss cannot allow you to heal!"'' - At the beginning of the 4th Round, and every 2nd Round following, The Abyss will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom and Gloom (-75%)}}.
**{{Link|Spell|Strangle}} can be used to prevent this cheat
*{{Icon|Heal}} ''"The Abyss cannot tolerate these Heals..."'' - If a Wizard casts a Healing Spell, The Abyss may cast one of these Spells:
**{{Link|Spell|Dragonlance}}
**{{Link|Spell|Power Current}}
**{{Link|Spell|Power Stunner}}
**{{Link|Spell|Ominous Scarecrow (Death)}}
**Applies a -20% incoming Healing Aura for 6 Rounds that increases Healing to Damage by 20%.
***Aura resembles Virulence in appearance.
