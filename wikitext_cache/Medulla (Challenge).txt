*Medulla is the final Boss in the first half of Empyrea.  Wizards must defeat him to recover their mind-controlled friends, and to rescue {{Link|NPC|Mellori}}.
*For the Standard version of this battle, see {{Link|Creature|Medulla (Standard)}}.

'''Advanced Combat Rules:'''
*Spell Decks cannot be changed while in this instance. (Make sure to set the spells in a deck before entering this instance).
*No-PvP spells are disallowed in this fight.
*PvP-Only Spells are allowed in this fight.
*Wizards cannot re-join combat after fleeing mid-combat.

| cheatnotes =
:'''Medulla'''
*{{Icon|Round}} After Sparck's first cycle, Medulla will command these cheats.
**''"Mr. Sparck, man the Essence Cannon"'' - This is a one Round warning before Sparck fires the Essence Cannon.
**''"Fire!"'' - At the end of the Round, Sparck will use a Shadow Spell to fire the Essence Cannon, which deals '''600''' Shadow Damage to and removes a Shadow Pip from all Wizards.
*{{Icon|Random}} Before Beans' Blossom's Thorns cheat, Medulla will use the following cheat.
**''"Prepare to be mind-meddled!"'' - Medulla will cast a Shadow Spell to randomly 'Confuse' a Wizard for two Rounds.

*'''Sparck'''
**{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Sparck! I need technical support"'' - If a Wizard casts an Aura, Medulla will say this, then Sparck will cheat-cast {{Link|ItemCard|Mass Supernova}}.
**{{Icon|Round}} '''Cycle 1''' - Sparck will begin the first cycle
**''"Medulla is my captain now"'' - Sparck will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Magnify (6 Rounds)}}.
**''"As you wish, Boss"'' - Sparck will cheat-cast a Spell to apply a +70% Trap on all enemies.
**''"You're up Mr. Sparck"'' - Medulla will shout this out, then Sparck will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Niles' Knock-Out}}.

*'''Beans''' 
*{{Icon|Trap}} ''"What’s up doc?”'' - If a Wizard casts a Trap, Beans will cast an all allies Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Cleanse Ward (-2)}}.
*{{Icon|Harmful Aura}}{{Icon|Curse}} ''"Beans, Beans. He's good for the heart!"'' - If a Wizard casts a negative Damage Aura or a harmful Charm, Medulla will shout this, then Beans will cheat-cast an all allies Shadow version of {{Link|Spell|Double Cleanse Charm}}.
**{{Icon|Round}} '''Cycle 2''' - Beans will begin the second cycle.
**''"Anything you say, Boss"'' - Beans will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Goat Monk (Tier 5b)}}.
**''"I must heal my master...bc I'm a Dr...Darnit"'' - Beans will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Pigsie (Tier 5b)}}.
**''"Your turn Doctor"'' - Medulla will shout this out, then Beans will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Blossom's Thorns}}.

*'''Pork''' 
**{{Icon|Round}} '''Cycle 3''' - Pork will begin the third cycle
**''"I live to serve"'' - Pork will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Scald}}.
**''"Yes Boss"'' - Pork will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Fire Dragon}}.
**''"Your turn Captain Bacon"'' - Medulla will say this, then Pork will cheat-cast {{Link|Spell|Bernie Notice}}.

{{Icon|Friend}} '''Ally Cheats'''
''"Sparck!" / "Medulla's mind-control spell is broken. Fascinating!"''  - When Sparck is defeated, he will assist the Wizard team.
*''"Preparing to fire the Essence Cannon"'' - At the end of the Round, Sparck will cast a Shadow Spell to fire the Essence Cannon, which deals '''3,932''' Shadow Damage to and removes a Shadow Pip from all enemies.
