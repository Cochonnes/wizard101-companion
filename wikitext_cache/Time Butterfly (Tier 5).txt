*{{Icon|Late}} ''"You're Late, or Are You Early?"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle late, Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will use {{Link|Spell|Stun (Shadow)}} that will last two turns.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will cast a Mass Shadow version of Empower on itself and its allies to gain 1 Power Pip and hidden-casts a Spell that switches the first turn of the battle.
*{{Icon|Feint}} If a Feint is placed on Time Butterfly (Tier 5), then it will cast an Ice version of {{Link|Spell|Swap Ward}} to switch the Traps with the Wizard who cast it.
**{{Link|Spell|Melt}} can be used to dispel this cheat.
**{{Link|ItemCard|Mass Feint}} will not trigger this cheat.
**{{Link|Spell|Indemnity}} can be used to prevent this cheat.

{{Icon|Minion}} '''Minions:'''
*Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will summon Minions cast as Shadow, starting with {{Link|Creature|Young Morganthe (Tier 5)}} on the first turn. Occasionally, there will be three Minions summoned on the first turn rather than just one, in which case all three Minions will be resummoned once.
*If Time Butterfly (Tier 5) is defeated before or on the same turn that all of the Minions are defeated, then it will revive itself and all of the Minions by casting a Mass Sun version of Blessing that restores 100% Health.
*If all Minions in battle are defeated before Time Butterfly (Tier 5), then Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will re-summon the defeated Minions along with the next Minion with a Shadow Spell on the following turn. The Minions are summoned in order:
**Example: (Morganthe, Morganthe & Roberto, Morganthe, Roberto, and Broodmother)
***{{Link|Creature|Young Morganthe (Tier 5)}}
***{{Link|Creature|Roberto (Tier 5)}}
***{{Link|Creature|Broodmother (Tier 5)}}
*When all three Minions have been defeated before Time Butterfly (Tier 5), then Time Butterfly (Tier 5) will stop respawning itself and its allies.
*If Time Butterfly (Tier 5) is defeated on any turn it summons Minions, then it will not revive itself. It is unclear if this is a bug or not.
