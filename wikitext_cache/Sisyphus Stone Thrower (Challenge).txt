| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Global}} ''"This isn't a sprint. It's a test of endurance!"'' - At the beginning of the first Round, and repeating each time he casts his Natural Attack, Sisyphus will interrupt-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Equalize}} that sets the Damage cap to one of the following values based on the number of times Sisyphus casts his Natural Attack:
**Beginning of 1st Round: '''500'''
**One Natural Attack: '''1,000'''
**Two Natural Attacks: '''3,000'''
**Three Natural Attacks and onward: '''9,999'''
**The Equalize will be recast if replaced, and the Damage cap will not increase.
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Boulder you than me!"'' - At the end of every third Round, occurring one Round earlier per Wizard present if there are 2 or more Wizards present, Sisyphus will cast his Natural Attack on a random Wizard, dealing 5% max Health Damage. If the Wizard has a helpful Charm, this Damage increases by 5%, and by 15% for each additional Charm after the first.
*{{Icon|Spell}} ''"Who will cast the first stone?"'' - After casting his Natural Attack, Sisyphus will give a random Wizard one Rock Put:
**{{GivesSpell|spells = 1|size = x150px|Rock Put}}
*{{Icon|Curse}}{{Icon|Jinx}} ''"Hya! Pushed away!"'' - If a harmful Charm or Ward is placed on Sisyphus, he will push the effects to the caster.
**Dispels will not trigger this cheat.
**Harmful Aura effects that decrease outgoing Damage, such as {{Link|Spell|Starfall}}, will trigger this cheat.
**Secondary effects from Spells will trigger this cheat.
