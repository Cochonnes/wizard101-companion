| descrip =
Lucia is the Cuckoo of Valencia's Great Clock, crafted by Master Gazpaccio to keep track of the time by singing at the arrival of every hour.  Like all of his creations, Gazpaccio magically imbued Lucia with emotions, granting her a sense of pride which serves as her drive to perform precisely on the hour.  After years of impeccable timekeeping, Lucia's pride has warped into vanity, and she now sings every few minutes, throwing the Great Clock and Valencian timekeeping into disarray.  After Wizards assist {{Link|NPC|Bernardo Dondi}}, Gazpaccio's Apprentice, with tuning the Great Clock's Core Mechanism, they ascend the to the Cuckoo's Nest to humble the Prima Cuckoo.

*''"The star is only as good as her supporting cast!"'' - In response to most Spells cast by Wizards, Lucia will interrupt-cast a Sun version of {{Link|Spell|Blessing}} that restores 100% max Health to all allies.
**This Spell is not affected by harmful Charms, Wards, or Globals that affect Healing Spells.
**The following types of Spells will ''not'' trigger this cheat:
***Harmful Charms
***Minion Spells
*''"Let's even the odds!"'' - At the beginning of any Round, if there are more Wizards on the Wizards' team than there are enemies on Lucia's team, then Lucia will interrupt-cast a Star Spell that summons a Clockworker until the Wizards' and enemies' teams have an equal number of members.
**Summoning a Minion will not trigger this cheat.
*''"This is my show! MINE!"'' - At the start of every fifth Round, Lucia will interrupt-cast a Star version of {{Link|Spell|Steal Charm}} that steals all helpful Charms from all Wizards, and a Star version of [[Spell:Steal Ward (Star, -All)|Steal Ward]] that steals all helpful Wards from all Wizards.

**The cycle resets after this cheat occurs.
