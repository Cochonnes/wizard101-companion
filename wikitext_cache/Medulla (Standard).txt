Medulla is the final Boss in the first half of Empyrea.  Wizards must defeat him to recover their mind-controlled friends, and to rescue {{Link|NPC|Mellori}}.

| cheatnotes =
{{Icon|Spell}} At the end of the first Round, all Wizards will be given 3 of the following Spell, which instantly removes Pork, Beans, or Sparck from combat, and moves them to the Wizard team:
*{{GivesSpell|size = x150px|Clear Mind}}
*If Pork, Beans, or Sparck is healed on the same Round that Clear Mind is cast on them, they regain their prior Health, and remain on Medulla's team.

{{Icon|Friend}} '''Ally Cheats'''
*''"Pork!" / "You freed my mind, Wizard. Let's finish this!"'' - When Pork is defeated, he will assist the Wizard team.
**''"We must work together!"'' - Randomly at the beginning of the Round, Pork will cast {{Link|Spell|Smoke Screen (-45%)}} at the enemy team.

*''"Beans!" / "The doctor is in!"'' - When Beans is defeated, he will assist the Wizard team.
**''"Hold still - I'll have you fixed up in a jiffy!"'' - If an ally is less than 50% Health, Beans will randomly cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Pigsie}}, restoring 550 Health to all Wizards.

*''"Sparck!" / "Medulla's mind-control spell is broken. Fascinating!"''  - When Sparck is defeated, he will assist the Wizard team.
**''"Preparing to fire the Essence Cannon"'' - At the end of every 5th Round, Sparck will cast a Shadow Spell to fire the Essence Cannon, which deals '''6,000''' Shadow Damage to all enemies and removes one Shadow Pip from each enemy.
