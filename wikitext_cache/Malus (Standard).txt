| descrip =
Malus is the final Boss within Nightmare. To avoid the Meteor Strike cheat, Wizards must use 4 Dream Water, collected from the leaky pipes in the maze, on each of the sleeping avatars around him to dismiss the Minions.
*For the Challenge version of this battle, see {{Link|Creature|Malus (Challenge)}}.
*{{Link|NPC|Dream Morp}} will appear after the duel.

| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Their hatred is a tonic. It fuels me."'' - At the beginning of the first Round, if Malus has any Minions, he will cast a Death version of {{Link|Spell|Meteor Strike}} that deals 100% max Health Damage to all Wizards. Max Health Damage ignores all Resist.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Embrace your hatred!"'' - At the beginning of every Round, Malus will cast one of the following Spells:
**{{Link|Spell|Black Mantle (-55%, 3 Pip)}}
**{{Link|Spell|Choke}}
**{{Link|Spell|Efreet (1,000)}}
***This version of Efreet leaves a -95% Curse {{Icon|Curse}} on the target and Malus will always target the Wizard with the lowest amount of Health {{Icon|Health}}.
**{{Link|Spell|Monster Mash (1,170)}}
***This version of Monster Mash beguiles the target and Malus will always target the Wizard with the highest amount of Health {{Icon|Health}}.
**{{Link|Spell|Muzzle}}
**{{Link|Spell|Tower Shield (Sun -90%)}}
**Dispels can prevent some of these cheats.
**If two or fewer Wizards are present in the duel, these cheats will only occur every other Round.
***This cheat condition variant is currently '''bugged''' so that the cheat does NOT occur at all.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"Let your rage drive you. Strike me down!"'' - Randomly, at the end of a Round, Malus will cast a "Flames of Hatred" Aura on himself.
**If a negative aura is used to replace Malus' aura, the aura will be replaced but the casted negative aura will be removed.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"Heroism is vanity. FIGHT ME!"'' - If a Wizard targets Malus while his "Flames of Hatred" Aura is active, he will cast a 0 Damage Star version of Supernova on himself, removing the Aura.
*{{Icon|Afterlife}} ''"Good. AGAIN!"'' - If Malus is defeated while the "Flames of Hatred" Aura is active, Malus will heal himself for 50% Health (75,000 Health), and applies a '''+200%''' outgoing Damage Aura on himself.
