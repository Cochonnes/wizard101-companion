| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Tardiness will not be tolerated!"'' - If a Wizard joins the duel after the second Round, then Simon will interrupt-cast an enhanced version of {{Link|Spell|Mana Burn}} that deals 400 Balance Damage on the Wizard.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"Simon says no attacks!"'' - For the first three Rounds, using a Damage Spell on Simon or one of his Minions will result in Simon retaliating against the caster.
**{{Icon|Damage}} ''"Simon didn't say!"'' - If a Damage Spell is cast on Simon or one of his Minions while Simon says "no attacks", then Simon will interrupt-cast a version of {{Link|Spell|Insane Bolt}} that always hits the Wizard who cast the Damage Spell.
***[[:Category:Steal Spells|Steal]] Spells do not trigger this cheat.
**{{Icon|Round}} ''"Simon says you can attack now!"'' - On Rounds 4 through 6, using a Damage Spell will not trigger the ''"Simon didn't say!"'' cheat.
**{{Icon|Round}} On Round 7, Simon will once again use the ''"Simon says no attacks!"'' cheat, and this will last for three more Rounds.
**The cycle of being unable and then able to attack for three Rounds at a time will repeat indefinitely for the duration of the duel.
*{{Icon|Round}} ''"I have my friends too!  See?"'' - At the beginning of the second Round, Simon will interrupt-cast a Spell that will either summon a Heartburner Golem or Virulent Gas Golem Minion.
**If any of Simon's Minions are defeated before him, then Simon will summon another Golem two Rounds after they are defeated.
***If multiple Minions are defeated before Simon, then Simon will summon one new Golem every two Rounds.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Simon says... none of that!"'' - If a Wizard casts a [[:Category:Blade Spells|Blade]], then Simon will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Disarm}} on the caster.
*{{Icon|Ward}} ''"My Pet knows tricks too!"'' - If a Wizard casts a Prism or positive [[:Category:Ward Spells|Ward]], then Simon's Polterpooch will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Feint}} on the caster.
**{{Link|Spell|Stun Block}} will trigger this cheat.

'''Daniel'''
*{{Icon|Round}} On Round 3, and randomly after that, Daniel will interrupt-cast {{Link|Spell|Curse}} on Simon after any other cheats that Simon uses at the beginning of that Round.
*{{Icon|Round}} After Round 3, Daniel will rarely interrupt-cast a Death version of {{Link|Spell|Fairy}} that restores 300 Health on all Wizards and also applies {{Link|Spell|Guidance}}.
**This Spell can be affected by negative Healing [[:Category:Charm Spells|Charms]] and Life Dispels that have been placed on Simon.
**''"I got this."'' or ''"Easy as pie."'' - Daniel will always use this line before casting Fairy, although he uses it for Curse as well.
