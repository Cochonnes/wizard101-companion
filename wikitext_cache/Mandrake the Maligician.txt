| cheatnotes =
*{{Icon|Late}} ''"Late Arrivals Disrupt the Show"'' - If a Wizard joins the battle late, Mandrake will cast {{Link|Spell|Camp Bandit (Tier 5a)}} on them.
*{{Icon|Aura}} ''"I Am Unstoppable!"'' - At the start of the battle, Mandrake puts on a '''-10%''' version of {{Link|Spell|Fortify}} that lasts four Rounds. Each succeeding Round that he takes new Damage, Mandrake will cast a new version of the Aura Spell that increases his Resist by -10%, until it is at -100% Damage. (Note: This Resist Aura is not always shown in the Creature's effects.)
**Later ticks of Damage over Time Spells do not appear to trigger this increase.
*{{Icon|Health}} ''"Feel the Power of Mandrake"'' - If Mandrake is at full health at the end of a Round, he'll cast a '''-15%''' Health Leafstorm that removes 15% of the total Health of the target to all enemies.
**After this spell is cast, {{Link|Spell|Enfeeble}} will be cast on all enemies.
**Leafstorm will trigger {{Link|Spell|Adapt}}'s spell requirement giving a Power Pip.
**If Mandrake is not at full health at the end of a Round, he'll cast a version of Blessing to restore '''5%''' of his total Health (+698).
**{{Link|Spell|Entangle}} will not prevent this heal.
*{{Icon|Healing}} ''"By the Power of Mali-Magic"'' - If a Healing Spell is cast, Mandrake will cast a '''+20%''' {{Link|Spell|Dragonspear}} on the healer.
**Pet May Casts will trigger this cheat.
*{{Icon|Manipulation}} ''"I knew you would try that!"'' - If Mandrake's Aura is removed or changed, he will change his Aura to a '''-100%''' version of Fortify.
**If Mandrake's Aura is replaced with a negative Aura, such as with {{Link|Spell|Pathogen}}, this cheat will be activated and the Aura's appearance will be altered, but the effects of it will not.
**If Humango Assistant is not defeated by the time Mandrake's -10% Aura expires, this cheat will be triggered. 
**If Humango Assistant is defeated by the time Mandrake's -10% Aura expires, Mandrake will replace it with a '''-20%''' version of Fortify, triggering this cheat 1 Round later. (needs verification for trigger)
