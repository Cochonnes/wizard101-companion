*{{Icon|Absorb}} ''"Jade ancestors, protect me!"'' - On the fifth Round, Jade Oni will cast a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Armor}} on himself.

{{Icon|Death}}{{Icon|Ice}} '''Death Oni'''
*{{Icon|Shield}} ''"Spirits of the Dead Protect Me!"'' - Death Oni casts a {{Link|Spell|Spirit Shield}} on himself after using a Shield spell.
*'''x4''' {{Icon|Ward|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Think you're tough?"'' - If Death Oni reaches a gambit of having four Shields, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Doom Oni}}. Wards will be removed after this gambit has been used.

{{Icon|Balance}}{{Icon|Death}} '''Plague Oni'''
*{{Icon|All Friends (Squared)}}{{Icon|Weakness}} ''"A pox upon your house!"'' - On the third Round, Plague Oni casts {{Link|Spell|Plague}}.
*{{Icon|Blade}} ''"Your Doom is Imminent"'' - Plague Oni casts a {{Link|Spell|Balanceblade}} on itself and {{Link|Spell|Mass Infection}}, when it has a Blade, and a Wizard has a negative Charm. This warns of the impending gambit.
**This Balanceblade can stack with the Balanceblade he casts from his hand.
*'''x2''' {{Icon|Blade|20px}} '''& x2''' {{Icon|Curse|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Greed will be your downfall!"'' - If Plague Oni reaches a gambit of having two Blades on itself, and 2 harmful Charms on the Wizard, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Tribunal Oni}}. All Charms will be removed after this gambit on both teams.

{{Icon|Fire}}{{Icon|Life}} '''War Oni'''
*{{Icon|Heal over Time}} ''"Let the war rage on!"'' - War Oni casts {{Link|Spell|Sprite}} on himself after he has a HOT on himself.
*'''x2''' {{Icon|Heal over Time|20px}} '''Gambit:'''
**''"Survival is the dominian of the fittest!"'' - If War Oni reaches a gambit of having two HOTs, he will cast {{Link|Spell|Infernal Oni}}. HOTs will be removed after this gambit has been used.
