Lemuria Main Quest Line
This guide is meant as a tool to follow your progress and keep an eye on the Lemuria Quest Line. It can also explain when a cheating boss is coming up.
I list the quests you have to do
in a certain area
. The areas are listed in the order you should do them in (unless otherwise specified). The area is not always one hundred percent correct, as certain quests can require you to run back and forth between areas to talk to people. To simplify matters, I have not listed this kind of running; the quest will say “talk” but not where the NPC is located. Also, if there is only one quest in an area, I will not mention the area separately. Located
between brackets
are the different parts of each quest, as many quests require you to do multiple things.
I have divided the quests in the Spiral into the following groups:
Talk:
Quests that require an interaction with an NPC.
Mob:
Quests that require the defeat of regular monsters.
Elite:
Quests that require the defeat of an elite creature.
D&C:
Quests that require the collection of something from certain monsters (Defeat and Collect).
Boss:
Quests that require the defeat of a boss. Elite monsters are not counted as bosses.
Minor Cheat
:
Quests that require defeating a boss that cheats a little (these cheats can generally be ignored).
Cheat:
Quests that require the defeat of a cheating boss (these cheats will hinder normal gameplay somewhat).
Major Cheat:
Quests the require the defeat of a cheating boss (these cheats will hinder normal gameplay significantly).
Solo
:
Quests you need to do alone.
Instance:
Quests that require the completion of a place behind a sigil with at least 2 battles.
Interact:
Quests that require interaction with a certain object.
Explore
: Quests that just require you to run towards a certain area, NPC or item.
Collect:
Quests that require you to collect multiple items from areas.
Photomancy:
Quests that require you to take a picture of an object using Photomancy.
Play as Your Pet
:
Quests that require you to utilize the Play as Your Pet feature.
Investigate:
Quests that require you to search and collect for clues.

For the Lemuria final Battle – Update Below
As at Jan 2023 – an update may assist.
– The Card swapout cheat appears to have been removed if all Wiz’s join at the same time (late cheat unconfirmed).
– The Shadoe no longer casts an intercept.
– The overtime cheat removal now seems to have been removed (enables the 7 pip fire dragon spell)
The general strategy should remain the same – all 4 wizards – Aura(Frenzy) followed by blade and 7 pip spell hit to left hand side (LHS). (order of blade/frenzy doesn’t matter) All 4 should go close to defeating the LHS. (Pack a few 2 pip hits as TC’s if it all goes to pot!)
Flee and return. I usually just collect 2 mana pips from Lemuria and dungeon recall.
Rejoin – and repeat for the RHS team.
While it is possible not to flee and return – the build to get up to 5/7 pips again is slow. and requires a 3 person hit to kill the LHS so that a 4th hitter can take out the RHS – which is all well and good unless your hitter is defeated (seen it more than once)
Unconfirmed – but I suspect the Duck Savage Cheats appear (5k shield and 50% shield) to have been nerfed as well.
General tips – elemental and spirit blades – I avoid due to the pip cost (2 turns – expensive for the utility). (They can be used to advantage by support players if a different strategy is used – eg: 45’s are swapped for 22% balance blades).
I try to aim for a build that is as close to 100% power pips that you can achieve.
For Death wizards I recommend Dark Pact as a backup blade scenario that can be cast across other team members if necessary.
Typical Deck (Death) Sharpen(s), Blade, Frenzy, (7 Pip Spell -Scarecrow), 1 Wand, 2 pip spell in TC’s, Pet Blade if you have one or a second blade.

Having run the Malughast #31 a few times – I suspect there is a second cheat/weakness that is worked in. This relates the rate at which shadow pips are generated. Under normal mob battles ( eg: Humango’s) – with a shadow pip rating of 133 – averaged over 10 runs – my generation rate is 3.2 turns.
In a corresponding 10 solo runs (with identical kit)of the Malughast the generation rate I obtained was one shadow pip per 5.6 turns.
This can effect the availability of spells for folks running a shadow biased deck.
In terms of strategy for soloing this boss – I used Empower TC on the first round for a pip build, waited until after the first boss hit and then cast the Malughast feint on Malughast. I also found using 20% Pierce valuable.
